# Company Analysis Report

## アークシステム株式会社 / Ark System Co., Ltd.

---

## Executive Summary

Ark System Co., Ltd. is a well-established Japanese IT company headquartered in Yokohama, Japan. Founded in 1988, the company has over 37 years of experience in system development, IT solutions, and package software. The company is particularly known for its construction industry software solutions, including the "Rakuoh" (楽王) series of cost estimation and quantity takeoff software.
