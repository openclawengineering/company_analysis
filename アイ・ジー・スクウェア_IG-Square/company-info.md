## Executive Summary

IG Square Co., Ltd. is an established Japanese IT services company headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. Founded in 1990, the company has grown from a small 14-person operation to a 60-employee organization specializing in system engineering services (SES), software development, and IT consulting. The company serves major Japanese corporations across finance, telecommunications, manufacturing, and public sectors.

## 5. Company Philosophy and Culture

### KIUI Activities (企画運営委員会)

The company's distinctive cultural centerpiece is **KIUI** (nicknamed "Kiwi" - 企画運営委員会, Planning and Management Committee), a monthly employee-organized event program held on Saturdays. This initiative demonstrates the company's commitment to:

- **Employee bonding and teamwork**
- **Work-life balance**
- **Cross-departmental communication**
- **Mentorship between senior and junior employees**

#### KIUI Activities Include:
- **社員旅行 (Company Trips):** Annual 2-day excursions to various locations (Gunma, Yamanashi, Ibaraki prefectures)
- **懇親会 (Social Gatherings):** Regular networking events with new hires and internal employees
- **BBQ (Barbecue Events):** Annual outdoor cooking events
- **新年会・忘年会 (New Year/Year-End Parties):** Traditional Japanese corporate celebrations
- **Sports Activities:** Bowling, volleyball, badminton, futsal, billiards, darts
