# Gigalogy - Company Information

## Overview

**Company Name:** Gigalogy Inc. (Gigalogy株式会社)
**Website:** https://www.gigalogy.com

**Headquarters:** Tokyo, Japan
**Industry:** AI Solutions / Software Development

## Mission

Empowering businesses to unlock the full potential of AI. Bridging the gap between technology and user experience.

## Products & Services

### 1. Personalizer
- Omnichannel personalization for E-Commerce businesses
- AI-powered product discovery and user engagement
- Revenue growth and LTV optimization

### 2. Maira
- No-code platform for building generative AI-powered smart applications
- Build personalized AI advisors/assistants
- Product recommendations and customer guidance

### 3. G-Core
- Fully managed AIOps infrastructure
- ML model hosting, training, and deployment
- End-to-end automated solution

### 4. SmartAds
- AI-driven programmatic advertising management
- Cross-platform performance measurement
- Real-time budget optimization

## Key Features
