## Key People

### 岡田 篤 (Okada Atsushi) - President & CEO

**Role:** 代表取締役社長 (Representative Director & President)

**Background:**
- Founded the company solo initially
- Incorporated in 2004
- 20+ years of company leadership
- Grandson of 岡田徹夫氏 (Tetsuo Okada) who received 勲五等瑞宝賞 (Order of the Sacred Treasure, 5th Class) for contributions to Yokohama's Sakuragicho development

**Management Philosophy:**
> 当社は、人と人、人と社会の繋がりを大切にし、各々にとって本当に良い関係や状態で生きていけるよう応援・教育し、そして共存していくアットホームな会社です。

(Translation: We value connections between people and society, supporting and educating each individual to live in good relationships and conditions, coexisting as an at-home company.)

**Company Track Record:**
- 21 consecutive years of profitability (黒字)
- Zero company-initiated layoffs in 20 years
- Focus on sustainable growth over rapid expansion
