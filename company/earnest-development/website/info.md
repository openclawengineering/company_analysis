## Company Name Meaning

The company name "Earnest" (アーネスト) represents:
- **誠実な** (Sincere/Honest)
- **真剣な** (Serious/Dedicated)
- **熱心な** (Enthusiastic)

**Mission:** To be a company trusted by people who embody earnest engineering.
