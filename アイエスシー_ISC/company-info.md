## 3. Main Products and Services

### Core Business Areas

ISC Co., Ltd. specializes in **software and hardware development**, with a primary focus on:

#### 3.1 Image Processing Inspection Systems (製品検査・外観検査装置)

The company's flagship offering is automated visual inspection equipment using image processing technology:

**Types of Inspection:**

1. **Color Inspection (色検査)**
   - Detection of color unevenness, coating defects, color tone differences
   - Applications: Automotive parts, plastic components, printing quality, pharmaceuticals, food (baking color, produce sorting)

2. **Foreign Object Inspection (異物検査)**
   - Detection of foreign material contamination in industrial products and food
   - Critical for food safety compliance

3. **Scratch and Stain Inspection (キズ・汚れ検査)**
   - Detection of fine scratches, dirt, and defects
   - Replaces human visual inspection with higher precision

4. **Shape Inspection (形状検査)**
   - Dimensional measurement, bend angles, marking position verification
   - Comparison against registered master templates

**Technical Capabilities:**
- High-speed processing using **GPU acceleration**
- 3-line CCD cameras (2048-bit resolution)
- Minimum defect detection: 0.5mm
- Inspection speed: up to 18,000 sheets/hour

#### 3.2 Video/Image Analysis and Object Recognition (映像解析・物体認識)

Advanced applications of their image processing expertise:

- **Driver Assistance Systems:** Vehicle distance measurement, automatic braking
- **Security Systems:** Face recognition, license plate recognition
- **Business Analytics:** Customer demographics analysis, store traffic patterns

#### 3.3 VR and AR Technology Solutions (VR・AR技術)

Cutting-edge virtual and augmented reality applications:

- **Manufacturing AR:** Work instructions via head-mounted displays, real-time guidance for wiring/assembly
- **Factory Simulation:** VR-based production line design verification
- **Exhibition/Display:** Interactive museum guides, product demonstrations

#### 3.4 Software and Hardware Development

- Windows application development
- Driver development
- Web application development
- Hardware integration and control systems
- High-speed camera firmware development

### What Sets ISC Apart

The company emphasizes that it is a **solution provider, not a product manufacturer**:
- Custom-built systems tailored to client needs
- Not restricted to existing product lines
- Consultative approach to understand manufacturing challenges
- Total system design from sensors to software
