## Products and Services

### Core Service: 経理まるごとサポート (Keiri Marugoto Support)

The company provides comprehensive outsourced accounting services for Japanese SMEs:

#### 1. 記帳代行サポート (Bookkeeping Support Service)
- Data entry into accounting software
- Processing receipts, bank statements, sales invoices, and purchase invoices
- Preparation of financial statements:
  - 試算表 (Trial Balance)
  - 損益推移表 (Profit & Loss Trend Report)
  - 現金出納帳 (Cash Journal)
  - 預金出納帳 (Bank Journal)
  - 売掛残高一覧表 (Accounts Receivable Summary)
  - 買掛残高一覧表 (Accounts Payable Summary)
- Year-end adjustments including tax-saving measures
- Coordination with client's designated tax accountant or introduction to partner tax accountants

#### 2. 経理代行サポート (General Accounting Support)
- Comprehensive accounting operations management

#### 3. 振込・支払代行サポート (Payment & Fund Transfer Support)
- Bank transfer processing
- Payment execution on behalf of clients

#### 4. 給与計算代行サポート (Payroll Calculation Support)
- Monthly salary calculations
- Social insurance and withholding tax calculations
- Payslip preparation

#### 5. 請求書代行サポート (Invoice Support Service)
- Invoice preparation and distribution
- Accounts receivable management

#### 6. その他サポート (Other Support Services)
- 資金繰り (Cash flow management)
- 融資相談 (Financing consultation)
- 資産管理 (Asset management)
- クラウド導入 (Cloud system implementation)

## Summary

株式会社アミエル (Amielle Inc.) operates as a comprehensive accounting support service provider for Japanese small and medium enterprises through its "経理まるごとサポート" (Keiri Marugoto Support) brand. The company, working alongside its affiliated tax corporation (アミエル税理士法人), offers bundled accounting services including bookkeeping, payroll, payment processing, and invoice management at transparent monthly rates ranging from ¥80,000 to ¥300,000+.

Led by Representative Director and Tax Accountant Todome Shin, the company emphasizes customer-centric service, innovation, staff empowerment, and social contribution to Japan's SME sector. Their value proposition centers on providing stable, cost-effective, and digitized accounting operations that eliminate the challenges of in-house accounting staff management.
