## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
株式会社ニッシン (Nisshin Co., Ltd.) is a Kanagawa-based IT services company specializing in POS system assembly, kitting services, and system localization. Founded in 2002 with capital of 10 million yen, the company employs 27 staff members and generates approximately 175 million yen in annual revenue.

### Strengths
1. **20+ Years Experience** - Established expertise in system assembly and kitting
2. **Major Client Relationships** - Partnerships with NCR Japan and other industry leaders
3. **DX-Ready Services** - Well-positioned for retail digital transformation
4. **Diversifying Portfolio** - Expanding into 3D printing and industrial products
5. **Stable Foundation** - Consistent growth since 2002

### Business Focus
- POS register and self-checkout system assembly
- Server and PC configuration services
- Contract software development
- SES (System Engineering Services)
- New ventures in 3D printing and custom manufacturing

### Key Differentiators
- Inherited expertise from major foreign enterprise operations
- Focus on Japanese market localization
- Comprehensive kitting services
- End-to-end system deployment capability

### Location Note
The company operates from two locations:
- **HQ:** Atsugi City, Kanagawa
- **Ebina Office:** Primary operations center
