# 株式会社Ａｉｎ (Ain Inc.)

## Company Overview

Ain Inc. is a printed circuit board (PCB) manufacturer founded in 1968 (Showa 43), headquartered in Chino, Nagano Prefecture at the foot of the Yatsugatake Mountains. With capital of 60 million JPY, the company has been dedicated exclusively to PCB manufacturing for over 50 years. They provide integrated production from design through manufacturing, including multi-layer PCBs, ceramic wiring boards, and assembly services. The company holds ISO 9001 certification for quality management.
