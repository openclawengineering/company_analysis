# 株式会社宮崎通信 (Miyazaki Tsushin Inc.)

## Overview
株式会社宮崎通信 is a comprehensive IT services company founded in 1995 in Yokohama, Japan. The company provides one-stop IT solutions including software development, network infrastructure, electrical construction, and IT support services. With offices in Yokohama (headquarters) and Miyazaki, the company serves both the Kanto and Kyushu regions.

**English Name:** Miyazaki Tsushin Inc.
**Website:** https://www.mtnet.co.jp/

## History
- **December 1995:** Founded in Yokohama as a comprehensive consultant for computers and communication equipment
- **2001:** Relocated to Hiyo-honcho, established PC Rescue Center
- **2002:** Joined Naolu.com Solution Partner, started MTNET support services
- **2005:** Opened Miyazaki branch (certified as Miyazaki Prefecture invited company)
- **2007-2012:** Various relocations and service expansions
- **2013:** Obtained ISO27001 certification (Miyazaki branch), electrical contractor license
- **2014-2019:** Rebranded support centers as "PC Emergency Centers"
- **2022:** Capital increased to 20 million yen
- **2025:** Construction license transferred to Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism
