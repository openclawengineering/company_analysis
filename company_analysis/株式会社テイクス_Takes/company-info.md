# 株式会社テイクス

## Company Overview

株式会社テイクス (Takes Inc.) is a major Tokyo-based IT system development and infrastructure services company with the vision of becoming the "No.1 IT Technical Support Enterprise." Takes provides comprehensive one-stop IT solutions including system development (embedded, control, and web systems), infrastructure construction, and operations/maintenance. The company operates a nationwide network of 18 offices — 7 main offices and 10 satellite offices in the greater Tokyo area plus regional locations, plus a dedicated learning center for employee training. Takes serves major system integrators across public/government, financial, and education sectors.

**Website:** https://www.takes.ne.jp/
