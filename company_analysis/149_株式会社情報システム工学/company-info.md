# 株式会社情報システム工学 (Information Systems Engineering Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社情報システム工学 (Information Systems Engineering Co., Ltd., abbreviated as ISE) is an independent company that provides high-quality, short-lead-time software development services. They specialize in system development and consulting across various industries including distribution, finance, marketing, manufacturing, and government.

## Business Description

The company provides system development and consulting services for:
- Distribution industry
- Financial industry
- Marketing
- Manufacturing
- Government and public sector

ISE deeply understands customer business and provides reliable systems that meet their needs. They deploy specialists called "MAC" for each project to create more advanced systems.
