# 鈴与シンワート株式会社 (Suzuyo Shinwart Corporation)

## Company Overview

Suzuyo Shinwart Corporation is a publicly listed IT system development company (TSE Standard, Securities Code: 9360) headquartered in Minato-ku, Tokyo. A member of the Suzuyo Group, the company provides system solutions, IT infrastructure services, and software development. The company emphasizes "共生 (Tomoiki)" — the spirit of coexistence and mutual prosperity — as its management foundation.

**Website:** https://www.shinwart.co.jp/

## History

Founded as the system development arm of the Suzuyo Group. The company has grown through continuous expansion of its IT services, cloud solutions, and system development capabilities.
