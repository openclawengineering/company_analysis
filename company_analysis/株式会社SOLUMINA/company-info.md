# 株式会社SOLUMINA (SOLUMINA Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

SOLUMINA Co., Ltd. is an independent IT systems company headquartered in Minatomirai, Yokohama, Kanagawa. With 160 employees and capital of 42 million JPY, the company operates in system integration and healthcare/medical solutions. The company name combines "solution" and "luminous" — "brightening society with reliable solutions." Their mission is "Develop joy and wonder for all people."
