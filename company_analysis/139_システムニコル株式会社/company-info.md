### Company Philosophy

**Customer First (顧客第一)**
As a technical team that goes beyond being just engineers, we understand the customer's position, respond flexibly to changes, and cooperate with them.

**Plus Alpha Approach (プラスαの取組み)**
We are not satisfied with standard quality. We actively engage in proposal activities, new technologies, and environmental initiatives.

**Characteristics (特徴)**
As an independent company with unique systems and mechanisms, we maintain a homey atmosphere from top executives to members.

## Message from the President

"As times change and the mechanisms of society evolve, what we create must always be 'useful to society.' To create good things, you need dedication. However, that dedication should not be self-centered.

We must always stand from the user's perspective, think, discuss, and pursue better things. Such an attitude is necessary.

And System Nicol, filled with engineers who have the ambition to 'help people,' will help the world as a company that 'obsesses over systems' (システムに凝る)."
