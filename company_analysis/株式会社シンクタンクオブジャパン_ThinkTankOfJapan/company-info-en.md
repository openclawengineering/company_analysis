# Think Tank of Japan Co., Ltd.

## Company Overview

Think Tank of Japan Co., Ltd. is an IT company founded in March 1983. Branding itself as "Japan's brain trust," it responds to customer needs with high-level consulting work and cutting-edge IT technology. Headquartered in Nakahara Ward, Kawasaki City, Kanagawa (KDX Musashi-Kosugi Building), it also operates a Niigata office. With 100 employees, it counts NEC Group as its primary client. The company operates across four business divisions: network construction, product development (AI, cryptocurrency, gaming, security, finance, healthcare, image recognition, vein authentication), embedded software, LSI development, and third-party evaluation. It holds Privacy Mark certification.
