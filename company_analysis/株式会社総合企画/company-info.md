# 株式会社総合企画 (Sogokikaku Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Sogokikaku Co., Ltd. is a diversified services company founded in 1976, headquartered in Ginza, Chuo-ku, Tokyo. Originally established as an industrial illustration (technical illustration) production company, the company has continuously expanded its service offerings across four core areas: healthcare solutions, manual/catalog production, 3D/2D illustration, and publishing/e-books. The company pioneered computer-aided illustration and manual production in the Japanese industry.

Key healthcare products include "Smart Theatre" (MRI bore interior projection system) and "Smart Window" (MRI/CT examination room video system), leveraging projection mapping technology.

**Website:** https://www.sogokikaku.co.jp/
