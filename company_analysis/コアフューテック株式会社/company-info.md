# コアフューテック株式会社 (CoreFutec Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

CoreFutec Co., Ltd. (formerly Elint System Inc.) is a software testing and development company founded in May 1983, with over 40 years of history in the software verification industry. Headquartered in Nakahara-ku, Kawasaki, Kanagawa, the company has built its reputation on quality assurance, specializing in third-party software verification for office equipment, network cameras, security products, and more. The company has also expanded into the nursing care field with the development of "e-Dennosuke-kun," a monitoring device for elderly care.

**Website:** https://www.corefutec.co.jp/

## History

- **May 20, 1983** - Company founded (as Elint System Inc.)
- **2016 (December)** - Atsushi Nagasaka appointed as Director of Elint System
- **2017 (March)** - Obtained labor dispatch business license
- **2018 (July)** - Capital increased to 24 million JPY; launched "e-Dennosuke-kun" nursing care monitoring device
- **Present** - Renamed to CoreFutec Co., Ltd.
