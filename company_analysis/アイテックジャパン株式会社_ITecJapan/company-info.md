# アイテック株式会社 (ITec Corporation)

## Company Overview

アイテック株式会社 (ITec Corporation) is a specialized machinery company with over 20 years of experience in cutting machines and wire processing equipment. The company focuses on providing cutting solutions and wire processing machinery.

**Website:** [https://i-tec.co.jp](https://i-tec.co.jp)

## History

The company has been involved in cutting machines and wire processing equipment for over 20 years, building expertise in this specialized field.
