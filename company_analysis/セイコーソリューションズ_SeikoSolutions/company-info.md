## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
セイコーソリューションズ株式会社 (Seiko Solutions Inc.) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Seiko Group Corporation, established on December 13, 2012, and began operations on April 1, 2013. The company was formed through the consolidation of Seiko Group's system solution businesses, combining Seiko Precision's System Business Division and Seiko Instruments' Chronotrust Business.

### Business Strengths
With capital of 500 million yen and over 1,700 consolidated employees across 10 group companies, Seiko Solutions has established itself as a comprehensive IT solutions provider with particular strengths in:

1. **Time Synchronization Technology** - Leveraging Seiko's century-long expertise in timekeeping
2. **Financial Sector DX** - Loan cloud platforms, electronic contracts, and credit information systems adopted by 30+ regional banks
3. **Network Infrastructure** - Time servers, load balancers, and console servers with strong domestic market presence
4. **AI & Digital Transformation** - Generative AI platforms, predictive maintenance, and document review systems
5. **Healthcare IT** - Regional medical coordination systems and electronic consent services

### Strategic Position
The company operates under Seiko Group's 8th Medium-Term Management Plan "SMILE145," with the goal of becoming a "Solution Company." Through strategic M&A activities since 2017, the company has expanded its capabilities across IT infrastructure, cloud services, business system development, and specialized industry solutions.

### Recent Developments
- Active expansion through acquisitions (GCC, INFRONT, Computer Science Corporation)
- Pioneering AI-powered solutions (Seiko Futureworks, LUiNa)
- Strong growth in electronic contract and loan cloud platform services
- Innovation in broadcast technology with PTP-based transmission systems
- Expansion of Mobility+ vehicle management services
