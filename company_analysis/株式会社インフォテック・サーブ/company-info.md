# 株式会社インフォテック・サーブ (Infotec Service Inc.)

## Company Overview

Infotec Service Inc. is an IT education and consulting company founded in January 1998, headquartered in Kanda-Kogawa-cho, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo. The company operates across three core business areas: IT education and training, publishing (IT educational materials), and consulting for human resource development strategies. Infotec serves educational institutions and corporations with curriculum development, industry-academia collaboration, and software development for educational use.

**Website:** https://www.infotech-s.co.jp/
