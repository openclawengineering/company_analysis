# 株式会社アイネット (INET Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

INET Co., Ltd. is a Yokohama-based IT services company headquartered in the Minatomirai district. The company provides information processing services, system development, and IT staffing solutions. It is known for its cross-disciplinary hiring approach, actively recruiting from all academic backgrounds regardless of technical specialization.

## Business Description

INET provides information processing services and IT engineering support. The company's recruitment philosophy emphasizes:

- **All-discipline hiring** — "理系・文系を問わず、全学部・全学科を対象" (regardless of science or humanities, all faculties and departments)
- **Communication skills** — Seeks candidates who are "明るく、元気で、やる気のある、コミュニケーション能力に長けた方" (bright, energetic, motivated, with strong communication skills)
- **New graduate and mid-career recruitment** — Actively hires at both entry and experienced levels
- **Internship programs** — Offers internship opportunities for students

## Key Characteristics

- **Minatomirai premium location** — Yokohama Symphonia Stage West Tower 13F, a landmark office building in Yokohama's waterfront district
- **Communication-first culture** — Unusual emphasis on interpersonal skills over pure technical ability in hiring
- **Diverse talent pipeline** — Active recruitment from humanities and social science backgrounds, not just engineering
