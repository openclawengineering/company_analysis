# ジスクソフト株式会社 (JISC Software Corporation)

## Company Overview

ジスクソフト株式会社 (JISC Software Corporation) is a software development company with over half a century of history. The company specializes in software development and operation/maintenance services, focusing on connecting "things" and "people" smartly to support social infrastructure.

**Website:** [https://www.jisc.co.jp](https://www.jisc.co.jp)

## History

- Founded over 50 years ago
- Has been supporting social infrastructure through software development and operation/maintenance services throughout its history
