# 株式会社アイポケット (i-Pocket Inc.)

## Company Overview

i-Pocket Inc. is a Yokohama-based solution company providing marketing, business development, and IT support services primarily for small and medium-sized businesses. The company positions itself as a "business companion" that walks alongside clients to solve operational challenges through consulting, digital marketing, subsidy application support, and organizational development.

## Business Description

i-Pocket provides five core service pillars:

1. **Subsidy Consulting** — Identifying and applying for government subsidies and grants (including 持続化補助金 sustainability subsidies) to help clients fund business improvements

2. **New Business Development** — Ideation and planning support for new business ventures and service offerings

3. **Digital Marketing & Web Sales** — Website optimization, online customer acquisition, and digital promotion strategies

4. **Operational Efficiency** — Business process improvement and digital transformation support

5. **Organizational Development** — Staff training, recruitment support, and organizational culture building
