# 株式会社エスエフシー新潟 (SFC Niigata Inc.)

## Company Overview

SFC Niigata Inc. is a specialized software development company focused on hospital and medical institution software. The company develops, sells, and maintains proprietary software including the No. 1 market-share nursing staff shift scheduling system for medium-to-large hospitals (200+ beds) nationwide. Founded in 1979, the company operates with an integrated approach from planning and development through sales, implementation, and maintenance.

The company's motto is "If you use SFC's software, you can feel at ease." Centering management philosophy on "customer creation" rather than "profit priority," the company builds thorough software for customers. Their client base includes approximately 2,700 medium and large hospitals nationwide.

**Website:** https://www.sfc-niigata.co.jp/
