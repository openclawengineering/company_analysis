# 株式会社エターナルサイエンス (Eternal Science Inc.)

## Company Overview

Eternal Science Inc. is a system development company headquartered in Kanagawa, Japan. The company provides comprehensive software development services — from system design through operations support — across various industries including medical/welfare, public infrastructure, and manufacturing. Approximately 75% of business is software development. The company specializes in training inexperienced employees to become capable "standalone" engineers quickly.
