## 4. Products and Services

The company's motto: **"We make 'I wonder if this is possible...' a reality."**
(「こんなことできないかなぁ・・・」を実現します。)

They provide end-to-end solutions: proposal, requirements definition, design, development, testing, on-site deployment, operational training, and maintenance.

### 4.1 ArenaScoreDisplaySystem (ASDS)
- **Official Twitter:** [@ASDS_Official](https://twitter.com/asds_official)
- **Description:** Indoor sports scoring display system
- **Supported Sports:** Basketball, Handball, Volleyball, Table Tennis, Martial Arts, Futsal
- **Key Features:**
  - Synchronization of clocks across different equipment (hardware & software)
  - Dynamic team/player introduction displays
  - Clear, readable information presentation
- **Deployment:**
  - Used in professional basketball and handball official matches
  - Installed in large gymnasiums for national sports events (国体 - Kokutai)

### 4.2 Public Racing Industry Services (公営競技向けサービス)
Services for horse racing, cycling (keirin), boat racing, and auto racing facilities:

- Audio announcement systems
- Odds/race results information display
- Reserved seat sales for special viewing areas
- Entrance gate management
- Point management systems
- Membership management
- Event coordination
- Prize money calculation for athletes
- Salary calculation for staff

### 4.3 Digital Signage & Information Display (各種施設向け案内表示)
Solutions for public and private facilities:

- Event information signage
- Signage control systems

- Large scoreboard displays for baseball and soccer stadiums

### 4.4 Remote Monitoring & Control Systems (遠方監視制御)
