## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
株式会社ISTソフトウェア (IST Software Co., Ltd.) is a well-established Japanese IT services company with over 50 years of history since its founding in 1972. Based in Tokyo with five offices across Japan (Sendai, Nagoya, Osaka, Fukuoka), the company serves as a consolidated subsidiary of 株式会社アイネット (I-NET Corp., listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange First Section).

### Financial Strength
- Solid financial position with 79% self-capital ratio
- Revenue of 8.1 billion yen (FY2025)
- Capital of 1.02 billion yen
- Strong asset base with 5.3 billion yen in total assets

### Human Capital & Culture
- Highly stable workforce with 15-year average tenure
- Strong employee retention and development focus
- Industry-leading work-life balance initiatives
- 100% childcare leave achievement rate
- High female manager representation (12%)
- Comprehensive health and wellness programs

### Technical Expertise
- Broad technology stack: Java, PHP, Python, low-code, AWS, AI, Microsoft, Google, PMP, SAP
- Strong capabilities in:
  - Large-scale system integration for government and enterprise clients
  - Embedded software development
  - Cloud services (SDDC model adoption)
  - Cybersecurity solutions
  - Generative AI applications

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Long-term stability:** 50+ years in business with established customer relationships
2. **Comprehensive certifications:** ISO 9001, 27001, 27017, Privacy Mark
3. **Strong financial health:** High self-capital ratio and consistent profitability
4. **National coverage:** Multiple offices across major Japanese cities
5. **Employee excellence:** High retention, skilled workforce, continuous training
6. **Innovation focus:** Active R&D, generative AI solutions, cloud-native approaches
7. **Customer-centric culture:** Philosophy of "Customer First" demonstrated through service quality

### Strategic Position
IST Software operates in the mid-to-large enterprise IT services market, with particular strength in government and public sector projects. The company has successfully transformed from traditional software development to modern cloud, AI, and cybersecurity solutions while maintaining strong employee relationships and corporate stability.

### Recent Developments
- Active expansion into generative AI market with "ゼロプロ" service (January 2026)
- Continued investment in cloud infrastructure and SDDC capabilities
- Focus on DX (Digital Transformation) services
- Strong emphasis on ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) initiatives
- Recognition for health management and workplace diversity

### Challenges & Opportunities
- Competition from larger IT services firms
- Need to maintain technological edge in rapidly evolving AI and cloud markets
- Opportunity to expand international services and overseas market penetration
- Potential for growth in generative AI and cybersecurity segments
- Leveraging strong employee base for continued innovation
