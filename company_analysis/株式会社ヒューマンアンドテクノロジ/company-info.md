# 株式会社ヒューマンアンドテクノロジ (Human And Technology Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Human And Technology Co., Ltd. (HAT) is a system development company headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa. The company develops computer systems and software across four business areas: railway-related systems, electronic money systems, embedded systems, and business applications. The company emphasizes "humanware" — maximizing the individuality, sensibility, and creativity of each employee as its most valuable asset.
