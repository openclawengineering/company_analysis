## Summary

株式会社インフォテック・サーブ (Infotech Serve Inc.) is a specialized IT education company established in 1998 in Tokyo. With approximately 20 employees, the company operates three core business lines: publishing IT education materials, providing training services, and offering HR development consulting.

The company is particularly known for its comprehensive study materials for Japan's IT certification exams (IT Passport, Fundamental IT Engineer, Applied IT Engineer) and has been certified as an Education and Training Partner by the i Competency Dictionary Association (iCDA).

Infotech Serve distinguishes itself through its focus on employee development and operational standardization, implementing the iCD framework to visualize work processes and support strategic business growth. Despite its small size, the company maintains significant influence in Japan's IT education sector through its high-quality publications and training programs.
