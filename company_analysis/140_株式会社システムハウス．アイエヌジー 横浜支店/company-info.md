# 株式会社システムハウス．アイエヌジー 横浜支店 (System House. ING Co., Ltd. Yokohama Branch)

## Company Overview

株式会社システムハウス．アイエヌジー (System House. ING Co., Ltd.) is a system integrator with extensive experience in the financial and distribution industries. The company is committed to continuous evolution and innovation in system development.

## Business Description

### Solution & Services
- System development
- Infrastructure development
- Web system development
- Smartphone application development
- Network construction

### Core Philosophy
The company's mission is to constantly evolve to maintain stability and reliability in the rapidly changing technology landscape. They develop unknown technologies and continuously pursue the highest performance.
