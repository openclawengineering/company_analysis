## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
オオイテクノ株式会社 (Oitechno Corporation) is a well-established Japanese software development company founded in 1986 and headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. With approximately 59 employees and ¥530 million in annual revenue (as of March 2025), the company specializes in contract software development for information systems and production management.

### Key Strengths
1. **Long-standing History:** Nearly 40 years of continuous operation since 1986
2. **Quality Certifications:** ISO9001:2015 and ISO/IEC27001:2013 certified
3. **Diverse Capabilities:** Windows/Linux/Web applications, embedded systems, network infrastructure
4. **Strong Parent:** Backed by Ooi Electric Co., Ltd. (75% shareholder)
5. **Dual Location:** Yokohama headquarters and Mizusawa (Iwate) office

### Business Focus
- Application software development (monitoring, control, embedded)
- Business application systems (production management, accounting, sales)
- Internal system construction and maintenance
- Remote maintenance services via VPN
- Personnel dispatch services

### Target Markets
- Manufacturing and production management sectors
- IT companies requiring cost management solutions
- Organizations needing attendance and workforce management systems
- Companies requiring network infrastructure and remote maintenance

### Competitive Position
Oitechno operates as a specialized B2B software contractor within the Ooi Electric group ecosystem, maintaining strong relationships with major Japanese corporations including Mitsubishi Electric group companies and Komatsu. The company's focus on quality (ISO certifications) and long-term client relationships positions it as a reliable partner for custom software development needs.

### Growth Trajectory
The company continues to evolve with technological trends, acknowledging AI, robotics, and IoT as key areas of industry transformation. Their management philosophy emphasizes environmental sustainability alongside technological innovation.
