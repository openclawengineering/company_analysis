## Company Name Meaning

**アーネスト (Earnest)** means:
- 真面目な (Serious)
- 熱心な (Enthusiastic)
- 誠実なエンジニア (Sincere engineer)

The company aims to be trusted by clients who seek sincere, enthusiastic, and serious engineers.
