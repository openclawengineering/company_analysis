# 株式会社エフィシエント (Efficient Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Efficient Co., Ltd. is a Yokohama-based technology startup founded in 2019, specializing in IoT systems, AI-powered video analysis, and custom software development. The company develops both client-facing solutions and its own products, including the AI-powered interview practice app "Steach" and an anti-resale AI system originally developed for Kawasaki Frontale (J-League football club).

## Business Description

Efficient operates across three main business areas:

1. **IoT System Development** — From manufacturing to service industries, providing end-to-end IoT solutions including sensor selection, data storage, and data utilization. Manufacturing floor IoT implementation is a key strength.

2. **AI-Powered Video Analysis Solutions** — Developing systems that capture and analyze video/images of human behavior and production floor operations. Building proprietary AI engines for big data analysis focused on human-centric applications.

3. **SES (System Engineering Services)** — Dispatching engineers to client sites, primarily in Kanagawa but expanding to Shonan and Ken-O areas.

### Key Products

- **Steach** — AI-powered interview practice app that analyzes eye gaze, posture, speaking speed, and transcribes content to provide objective feedback on presentation skills. Released May 2022; pilot tested at Yokohama schools.

- **Anti-Resale AI System** — Originally developed for Kawasaki Frontale to detect and prevent unauthorized resale of signed merchandise using AI image recognition. Joint patent obtained with the club (October 2022). The underlying AI engine has broad applicability.
