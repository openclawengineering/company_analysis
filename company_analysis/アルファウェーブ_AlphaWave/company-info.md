## 4. Products & Services

### Core Business Divisions

#### 1. システム開発事業 (System Development Business)
- **System Development Support**
  - Dispatch, SES, LAB, and hybrid contract models
  - Full project lifecycle support (requirements → design → development → testing → maintenance)
  - Latest technologies: Cloud, AI, IoT, RPA

- **Contracted System Development**
  - Custom enterprise systems
  - Web and mobile applications
  - System replacement and optimization

- **Solution Delivery**
  - Business process automation (DX promotion)
  - Cloud migration (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  - Security measures and risk management

#### 2. ネットワーク・サーバー・クラウド事業 (Network/Server/Cloud Business)
- Network design and construction (Cisco, Juniper expertise)
- Server equipment design and deployment
- Kitting services (PC, peripherals setup)
- Cloud environment construction (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Security configuration (firewalls, access control)

#### 3. 情報セキュリティサービス事業 (Information Security Services)
- **脆弱性診断** (Vulnerability Assessment) - Comprehensive system/network scanning
- **セキュリティコンサルティング** (Security Consulting) - Risk management, threat countermeasures
- **ISMS認証取得/CSIRT構築支援** - ISMS/P-mark certification and CSIRT setup support
- **教育・トレーニング** (Education & Training)
  - CTF-based training
  - Targeted email attack training
  - Incident response training
  - CISO-led security lectures

#### 4. ITエンジニア教育事業 (IT Engineer Education Business)
- 3-month comprehensive new employee training program
- Original curriculum taught by internal engineers
- Covers programming basics to advanced concepts
- Career path support (SE → NE → ISE transitions possible)

## 6. Company Philosophy & Culture

### Management Philosophy (経営理念)

> "Based on the technology and experience cultivated over many years in the IT field,
> 
> We sincerely provide valuable services to our customers.
> 
> Together with our colleagues, we continue to grow and take on challenges,
> 
> We remain a company that employees can be proud of to their loved ones."

*— Alpha Wave Co., Ltd.*

### Corporate Values

From the CEO's message (奥田史一 - Shinichi Okuda):

1. **Customer-Centric Approach**
   - "Alpha Wave engineers are trustworthy" — building lasting client relationships
   - Acting as a "伴走者" (running partner/companion) to clients, not just a vendor

2. **People-First Culture**
   - Passion for technical excellence as engineers
   - Developing professional manners and business sense
   - Encouraging work-life balance with hobbies and social activities
   - Building "豊かな人生" (rich, fulfilling lives)

3. **Continuous Growth**
   - "人を大切にし、技術を磨き、共に成長し続ける" (Cherish people, polish skills, grow together)
   - Sharing enjoyable times with all stakeholders
   - Contributing to social development

4. **Trust-Based Business**
   - Long-term partnerships through reliable delivery
   - Quality management throughout all development phases
   - Consistent support from development to maintenance

### Company Culture Highlights

- **Young Workforce:** Average age of 32.7 years reflects a dynamic, growing organization
- **Comprehensive Training:** 3-month new employee training with internal instructors (not outsourced)
- **Career Flexibility:** Engineers can transition between SE, NE (Network Engineer), and ISE (Information Security Engineer) roles
- **Long-Term Vision:** Focus on being "a company employees can be proud of to their loved ones"
- **Industry Agnostic:** Independent software vendor approach — not tied to specific industries

## Summary

**株式会社アルファ・ウェーブ (Alpha Wave Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese IT services company founded in 1984 with nearly 40 years of experience. Based in Tokyo's Minato ward, the company has grown from a small software development firm to a comprehensive IT solutions provider with 252 group employees.

The company operates across four main business segments: system development, network/server/cloud infrastructure, information security services, and IT engineer education. Their client base spans diverse industries including aviation, finance, manufacturing, retail, and government.

Alpha Wave distinguishes itself through:
- Long-term client relationships built on trust
- A people-first corporate culture
- Comprehensive in-house training programs
- Full lifecycle support from development to maintenance
- ISO 27001 (ISMS) certification and CSIRT capabilities

With a management philosophy centered on providing value while ensuring employees can build fulfilling careers and be proud of their company, Alpha Wave represents a stable, growth-oriented player in Japan's IT services industry.
