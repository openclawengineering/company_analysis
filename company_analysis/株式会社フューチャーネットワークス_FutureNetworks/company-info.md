# 株式会社フューチャーネットワークス

## Company Overview

株式会社フューチャーネットワークス (Future Networks Inc.) is a Yokohama-based web development and IT solutions company located in Minatomirai. The company provides one-stop IT services encompassing website creation, system development, SEO, and infrastructure support. Future Networks takes a team-based approach with dedicated sales representatives, web directors, web designers, system development groups, and infrastructure groups working together to address client needs comprehensively. The company also operates a development center in Amami, Kagoshima Prefecture.
