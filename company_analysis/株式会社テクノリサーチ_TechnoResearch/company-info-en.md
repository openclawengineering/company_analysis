# Techno Research Co., Ltd.

## Company Overview

Techno Research Co., Ltd. is an IT company founded in April 1988 in Odawara City, Kanagawa. The representative director is Suginoma Nobuo. With a capital of 10 million yen and 34 employees (as of 2020), it operates across four divisions: software development, internet business, hardware development/sales, and content business. It developed and sold an in-house product called "Digiten" in 1990. Major delivery clients include PS Mitsubishi, Hitachi System and Services, Anritsu, Fujifilm, and Japan NCR. The company owns its own building in Kofu, Odawara City, and banks with Yokohama Bank, Sagami Shinkin Bank, Shizuoka Bank, and Shizuoka Chuo Bank.
