## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Philosophy (企業理念)

#### "技術を通じて社会に貢献します" (Contributing to society through technology)

**Core Values:**
1. **All for One, One for All spirit** - The belief that collective effort creates value
2. **Creating a more convenient, rational, and comfortable society** through IT development and dissemination

### Three Pillars

#### 1. 夢を大切にします (Cherishing Dreams)
> "The most important thing in life is 'dreams.' Management means nurturing diverse individual dreams into bigger dreams. We strive to create an environment that values the joy of creation and gratitude in pursuit of realizing each dream."

#### 2. 新しい企業文化の創造を大切にします (Creating New Corporate Culture)
> "We value embracing diversity, respecting individuality, and taking on new challenges. This is the corporate culture we aim for."

### Action Philosophy (行動理念)

1. **日々の粘り強い努力 (Daily Perseverance)**
   - "Dreams come equally to everyone. What's needed to realize them is daily persistent effort. And sometimes, that brings miracles."

2. **自分の頭で考え、自分の足で立つ (Think for Yourself, Stand on Your Own Feet)**
   - "Originality is born from asking 'Why? Why?' and thinking with your own head. Not relying on others but standing firmly on your own makes work more fulfilling."

3. **前向きな取り組み姿勢 (Positive Attitude)**
   - "In any situation, start with YES, not NO. Don't wait passively—take proactive action. This attitude creates new relationships with customers."

### Company Culture (社風)
- **"まずはやってみよう" (Let's try it first)** - The company's guiding spirit
- In the fast-evolving IT industry, responding quickly to customer needs is critical
- Rather than over-planning and missing opportunities, they prioritize action and adapt along the way
- **Free and open atmosphere** where all team members think, speak, and act independently
- Active exchange of opinions across hierarchical boundaries

### Employee Development
- Pre-employment e-learning for new hires
- New employee training including Java web application development
- Ongoing technical OJT and training
- **Certification bonus system** for self-improvement
- Annual performance-based project awards

### Work-Life Balance Support

- Various special leave (maternity, paternity, nursing care, etc.)
- Housing allowance (¥20,000/month for under-30 heads of household)

## 9. Summary

**株式会社YSLソリューション** (formerly Yokohama System Laboratory) is a well-established Japanese IT services company founded in 1991 as part of the YSG Holdings group. Over its 30+ year history, the company has evolved from a small software development firm into a comprehensive IT solutions provider with approximately 384 employees and annual revenues of ¥6.75 billion.

### Key Strengths:
1. **Diversified Industry Expertise** - Strong presence in financial/insurance, construction, government, and manufacturing sectors
2. **Proprietary Products** - Owns construction industry solutions (CheX, LAXSY, AQuick) and pharmaceutical approval management tools
3. **Technical Breadth** - Expertise across modern (Java, .NET, cloud) and legacy (COBOL) technologies
4. **Geographic Reach** - 5 domestic offices plus subsidiaries in China and Vietnam
5. **Corporate Stability** - Part of century-old YSG Holdings group with strong financial backing

### Recent Strategic Moves:
- Expansion through M&A (acquired EXE Consulting and Sogo System Joho)
- International expansion with Vietnam joint venture (2024)
- Industry-academia partnerships with Tokyo Institute of Science
- SDGs certification demonstrating commitment to sustainability
- Sports sponsorships enhancing brand visibility in Yokohama region

### Company Culture:
Characterized by a "try it first" mentality, the company fosters a flat, open organizational structure where employees are encouraged to think independently and take initiative. Strong emphasis on work-life balance with comprehensive benefits including refresh leave, childcare support, and employee development programs.

### Business Focus:
Core business revolves around system integration (custom development for enterprise clients) and solution services (proprietary products for construction and pharmaceutical industries). The company positions itself as solving social problems through IT, aligned with its mission of creating a "more convenient, rational, and comfortable society."
