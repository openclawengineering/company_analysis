# 株式会社計装エンジニアリング (Keiso Engineering Co., Ltd.)

## Overview
株式会社計装エンジニアリング is a Japanese IT and engineering company with over 35 years of experience. The company operates three main business divisions: contracted development, SES (System Engineering Service), and plant-related business. Originally founded on plant instrumentation engineering, the company has expanded into comprehensive software development and IT services.

**English Name:** Keiso Engineering Co., Ltd.
**Website:** https://www.keiso.co.jp/

## History
- **Founded:** Over 35 years ago
- **Origins:** Started with plant instrumentation engineering business
- **Evolution:** Expanded from plant engineering to comprehensive IT services
- **Expertise:** Built extensive knowledge and achievements in electrical instrumentation design over 35 years
