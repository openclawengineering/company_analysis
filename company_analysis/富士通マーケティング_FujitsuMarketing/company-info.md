## 9. Summary

**富士通マーケティング株式会社 (Fujitsu Marketing Ltd.)** is a Fujitsu Group subsidiary established in June 2015, now operating as **toBeマーケティング株式会社 (toBe Marketing, Inc.)**. The company specializes in Salesforce consulting, marketing automation, and digital transformation services.

### Key Highlights
- **100% Fujitsu-owned subsidiary** with 100 million JPY capital
- **Salesforce specialty partner** focusing on MA, CRM, and AI solutions
- **Core expertise**: Agentforce, Data Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Account Engagement (Pardot)
- **Leadership**: CEO Tomokazu Koike, with experienced executives from Salesforce, Oracle, SAP, and other major tech companies
- **Business model**: End-to-end consulting, implementation, and ongoing support for digital marketing transformation
- **Target**: B2B companies seeking to optimize marketing-sales alignment and maximize customer LTV

The company has evolved from its original software development and information processing services focus to become Fujitsu's dedicated Salesforce and digital marketing consulting arm, helping enterprises leverage AI, data integration, and marketing automation for business growth.
