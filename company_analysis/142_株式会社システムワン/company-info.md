# 株式会社システムワン (System One)

## Company Overview

株式会社システムワン (System One) is an IT system construction company focused on fulfilling social responsibility through IT system development and cultivating human capabilities.

The company specializes in:

- Corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives
