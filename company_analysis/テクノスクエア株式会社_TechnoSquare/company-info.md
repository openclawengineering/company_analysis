# テクノスクエア株式会社 (Techno Square Inc.)

## Company Overview

Techno Square Inc. is an IT solutions company specializing in innovative technology applications for agriculture, fisheries, and rural community systems. The company focuses on leveraging AI and drone technology to solve social problems in rural and island regions of Japan.

**Website:** https://www.techno-square.com

## Services/Products

### Smart Agriculture Solutions
- **AI Copy Cultivation** - AI-powered agricultural technology that cultivates crops optimized for local climate conditions, addressing the challenge of an aging farming population
- **Smart Farming Systems** - ICT utilization for agricultural productivity improvement and labor reduction

### Smart Fisheries Solutions
- **AI Indoor Seaweed Cultivation** - Japan's first indoor seaweed cultivation facility using AI technology to address declining seaweed production
- **AI-enhanced Fishing Operations** - Integration of AI technology in traditional fishing methods

### Drone Technology
- **Fishery Drone Solutions** - Drone technology for efficiency improvements in traditional fishing operations
- **Environmental Monitoring** - Automated drone systems for marine debris monitoring
- **Wildlife Management** - Drone applications for wildlife monitoring and trap management in rural areas

### Rural Community Systems
- **Small-scale Regional OS** - Community operating system for rural settlements and remote islands, facilitating resident participation in local governance through LINE-based communication
- **Environment Coordination Funds** - Systems for environmental cleanup support through tourist cooperation fees

## Clients

## Philosophy

The company aims to solve social problems in rural and aging communities through innovative technology. Their philosophy centers on leveraging AI and drone technology to create sustainable solutions for Japan's declining rural populations and traditional industries.

### Core Focus Areas
- Addressing agricultural and fishing labor shortages through automation
- Supporting rural governance with digital tools
- Environmental sustainability initiatives
- Preservation of traditional industries through technological enhancement
