# S&A JAPAN株式会社 (S&A Japan)

## Company Overview

S&A JAPAN株式会社 is an innovation company based in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, specializing in management consulting, ISO certification consulting, and system engineering services. Founded in December 2011 during the year of the Great East Japan Earthquake, the company was established to meet reconstruction needs and contribute to "Japan's regeneration."

The company operates as a comprehensive trading company focusing on communication business, regional revitalization, and environmental initiatives. S&A emphasizes human-to-human networking and aims to be an innovation company that contributes to society through business development suited to the times.

**Website:** [https://s-and-a.jp](https://s-and-a.jp)

## History

- December 2011: Company established during the Great East Japan Earthquake to support reconstruction efforts
- 2011-2026: Expanded into management consulting, ISO certification consulting, and system engineering services
- 2022: Compliance policy established with emphasis on legal compliance and corporate ethics
- Ongoing development of communication, regional revitalization, and environmental business initiatives
