## AI-Driven Company Initiatives

### Core Philosophy
- **Vision**: Create tomorrow's common sense through today's challenges
- **Mission**: Shape possibilities with AI, create unseen value, and create the future's commonplace

### AI Implementation Strategy
1. **Internal AI Integration**: All employees use AI in daily work as standard practice
2. **Generative AI Projects**: Centered on engineer employees, promoting efficiency and high-quality technical provision
3. **AI Certification**: All-employee acquisition of Generative AI Passport exam by GUGA (Generation AI Usage Promotion Association)
4. **Secure AI Tools**: Developed and deployed "Nareful Chat" - an enterprise-specific closed-environment generative AI tool
5. **AI Culture Building**: AI usage contests and award systems to embed AI in daily operations

### AI Products and Services

#### CLINKS Chat AI
- **Purpose**: Enhance external user experience
- **Features**: Corporate site visitors can freely ask questions and get real-time answers
- **Examples**: "What is current remote work rate?", "Tell me about workplace atmosphere", "What are main businesses?"

#### Knowleful (Nareful Chat)
- **Purpose**: Enterprise closed-environment secure generative AI chat tool
- **Features**: 
  - Beginner-friendly generative AI interface
  - Easy prompt sharing across organization
  - Secure AI usage in closed environment
- **Website**: https://www.knowleful.ai/

#### AI Development Services
- **Focus**: Generative AI system development with consulting support
- **Range**: Small-scale PoC to large-scale development using unique development steps
- **Approach**: Companion consulting for successful implementation
