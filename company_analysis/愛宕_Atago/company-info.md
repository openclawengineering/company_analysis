## 5. Human Resources & Culture

### 5.1 Company Philosophy: "人間力と技術力" (Human Power & Technical Power)

Atago emphasizes two core values:

1. **人間力 (Human Power)**: Interpersonal skills, manners, communication
2. **技術力 (Technical Power)**: Technical expertise and development capabilities

> *"When you hear 'development,' you might imagine someone silently programming, thinking technical skills are most important. However, we believe interpersonal skills (manners, communication) are crucial."* — Atago Philosophy

The company believes that software development requires constant human interaction—receiving explanations, asking questions, and presenting completed work. Therefore, collaboration with clients and other team members is essential.
