# 株式会社エルテックス (ELTEX Inc.)

## Company Overview

ELTEX Inc. is a software development company founded in 1986 (Showa 61), headquartered in Yokkaichi, Mie Prefecture. With 157 employees and capital of 20 million JPY, the company is a pioneer and leader in EC (e-commerce) and mail-order management systems. ELTEX provides comprehensive packages for online shops including order management, inventory management, membership management, EC frontend, promotions, and contact centers.

The company has multiple business locations across Japan: Mie (HQ and engineering), Tokyo (Shinjuku), Kanagawa (Kawasaki), Kyushu (Kagoshima), and closed offices in Fukushima and elsewhere.

**Website:** https://e-eltex.co.jp/
