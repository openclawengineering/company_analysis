# CLINKS Co., Ltd.

## Company Overview

CLINKS Co., Ltd. is an IT company established in December 2002. Promoting itself as an "AI DRIVEN COMPANY," it operates across four business pillars: IT outsourcing, system development, AI services, and educational training. As of January 2026, the company employs 1,277 people, making it a mid-to-large IT firm. It maintains three offices: Tokyo headquarters (Yaesu, Chuo Ward), Osaka branch, and Yokohama office (Landmark Tower, 33rd floor). The company has developed proprietary products including the enterprise AI chat tool "Knowleful," cloud-based attendance management system "KINTAI-CLOUD," and telework support system "ZaiTark." It holds ISMS, Privacy Mark, and Healthy Management Excellence Corporation 2024 (large enterprise division) certifications.
