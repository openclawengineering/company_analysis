# アイエスシー株式会社 (ISC Inc.)

## Company Overview

アイエスシー株式会社 (ISC - Integrated System Creator) is a Japanese company specializing in image processing inspection systems for manufacturing. With 36+ years of history since founding, ISC develops and provides visual inspection equipment for product and appearance inspection, leveraging expertise in image/video processing technology. The company serves major enterprise clients with automated inspection solutions for production lines.

**Website:** http://www.isc-net.co.jp

## Products & Services

### Image Processing Inspection Systems
- **色検査 (Color Inspection)** — Detects color unevenness, missing paint/plating, and color tone differences using image analysis
- **異物検査 (Foreign Matter Inspection)** — Detects foreign matter contamination in industrial products and food
- **キズ・汚れ検査 (Scratch/Contamination Inspection)** — Detects fine scratches and dirt on products to maintain quality
- **形状検査 (Shape Inspection)** — Detects subtle differences in dimensions, bending angles, and marking positions

### Additional Services
- **映像解析・物体認識 (Video Analysis & Object Recognition)** — Applied systems using image/video analysis and object recognition
- **VR/AR Solutions** — Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality technology proposals
- **Software Development** — Web applications, drivers, and hardware development
- **System Integration** — End-to-end solutions from development to hardware
