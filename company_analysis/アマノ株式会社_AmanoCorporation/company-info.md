# アマノ株式会社 (Amano Corporation)

## Company Overview

アマノ株式会社 (Amano Corporation) is a major Japanese corporation specializing in time information systems, parking systems, environmental systems, and cleaning robot solutions. The company is listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market and is headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture.

**Website:** [https://www.amano.co.jp](https://www.amano.co.jp)

## History

- **1931:** Founded as "Amano Manufacturing Works" (天野製作所) by Shuichi Amano in Tokyo, aiming to manufacture Japan's first time recorders
- **1939:** Incorporated as "Amano Manufacturing Works Co., Ltd."
- **1942:** Headquarters moved to Yokohama
- **1945:** Established "Yokohama Machinery Co., Ltd." and resumed time recorder manufacturing
- **1961:** Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange Second Section
- **1966:** Changed company name to "Amano Corporation" (アマノ株式会社)
- **1967:** Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange First Section; Started parking business with "Parking Master"
- **2022:** Transitioned to Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market

## Capital & Employees

- **Capital:** ¥18.239 billion (as of March 2025)
- **Revenue:** ¥175.4 billion (Consolidated, FY2025)
- **Employees:** 5,541 (Consolidated), 1,957 (Non-consolidated) as of March 2025
- **Subsidiaries:** 7 domestic, 25 overseas

## Services/Products

### Time Information Business
- Time and attendance management systems (TimePro series)
- Cloud-based attendance management services
- Personnel and payroll management systems
- Time recorders (IC card-based)

### Parking Business
- Parking system equipment and cloud services
- Ticketless parking systems
- Parking lot management and operations

### Environment Business
- Dust collectors and mist collectors
- Oil mist collection devices

### Clean & Robot Solution Business
- Commercial cleaning robots (HAPiiBOT)
- Floor cleaning machines and polishers
- Electrolyzed water cleaning solutions

### Outsourcing Services
- Information systems outsourcing
- Parking operation outsourcing
- Cleaning services

## Clients

Major clients include:
- Japan Railways (JR) - Time recorders
- Various corporations for attendance management systems
- Municipalities and commercial facilities for parking systems
- Manufacturing facilities for environmental equipment

## Philosophy

**Corporate Message:** "Time & Airの未来へ" (To the Future of Time & Air)

### Company Motto (社訓)
- **鈍 (DON)** - "Don" means correct behavior. Not foolish slowness, but honesty, brightness, respect for personality, harmony among people, and spirit of service.
- **根 (KON)** - "Kon" means unwavering effort. This connects to having love for work and a spirit of research and improvement.
- **運 (UN)** - "Un" means blessed fortune. When "Don" and "Kon" are well practiced, "Un" opens up, and a rich heart and rich life come to fruition. "Un" is not something to wait for while sleeping.

### Behavioral Guidelines (行動規範)
1. 正直に、謙虚に生きる - Be honest and modest
2. 基本に忠実、基本の徹底 - "True to the Basics"
3. 思いやりと感謝で人と社会に尽くす - Serve people and society with consideration and gratitude
4. 時代の変化に心を開く - Be open to change
5. 笑顔で挨拶、顔見て会話 - Be Pleasant and Communicate
6. 広く世界に目を向ける - Look out to the entire world
7. 地道な努力を惜しまない - Be willing to put in the effort
8. お客様とともに成長する - Grow with our customers
9. 有言実行、最後までやり抜く - Commit to and Deliver on Our Goals
10. 常に現場を見る - Always observe the worksite
11. 心身ともに健康ありき - Engage in active and healthy lifestyles
12. 一人ひとりがアマノブランド - Be a representative of the Amano Brand

The company aims to:
- Visualize time and build a safe and comfortable society

- Contribute to a sustainable society through continuous innovation
