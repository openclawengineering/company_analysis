## 11. Summary

**株式会社コスモスシステム (Cosmos System Co., Ltd.)** is a specialized software development company established in 2025 through a corporate split from its parent company 株式会社コスモス (founded in 1996). Headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa, the company focuses on control system development with deep expertise in image processing, semiconductor systems, environmental monitoring, space development, and air traffic control systems.

### Key Strengths:
1. **Deep Technical Specialization** - 80% of work in C/C++ for control systems
2. **Diverse Industry Portfolio** - Manufacturing, aerospace, infrastructure, aviation
3. **Strong Parent Company Heritage** - Nearly 30 years of accumulated technical know-how
4. **Employee-Centric Culture** - Focus on training, welfare, and growth
5. **Renewable Energy Commitment** - Parent company achieved 100% renewable energy in 2016

### Business Model:
- Contract software development for control systems
- Worker dispatching services
- R&D support and consulting

### Competitive Advantages:
- Specialized expertise in high-complexity control systems
- Balanced team structure from junior to veteran engineers
- Strong relationships with major manufacturers and government agencies
- Commitment to employee development and satisfaction

### Notable Projects:
- Robot vision and 3D inspection systems
- Satellite control systems
- Dam management and telemetry systems
- Semiconductor wafer inspection
- Air traffic control equipment
