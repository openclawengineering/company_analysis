# 株式会社ネオ・システム (NEOSYSTEM Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

NEOSYSTEM Co., Ltd. is an independent system integrator (SIer) founded in 1989, originally based in Yamanashi and expanding throughout the Greater Tokyo area. With 206 employees and capital of 60 million JPY, the company provides diverse information system solutions and software development with "customer satisfaction and trust" as its top priority. NEOSYSTEM specializes in Microsoft Azure, Power Platform, generative AI implementation support, and Aras Innovator PLM solutions.
