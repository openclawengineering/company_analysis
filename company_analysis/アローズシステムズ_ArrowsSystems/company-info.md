## Summary

**株式会社アローズ・システムズ (Arrows Systems Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established IT company founded in 1999, headquartered in Yokohama's Minatomirai district. With 166 employees (263 group-wide) and capital of approximately ¥61 million, the company specializes in web system development and offers diverse engagement models including lab-type development, contract development, and engineer dispatch services.

The company serves major Japanese corporations across telecommunications (NTT DOCOMO), e-commerce (Rakuten, LINE Yahoo), aviation (ANA), and hospitality sectors. Through its group companies, it also operates accommodation industry solutions including booking system platforms.

Arrows Systems emphasizes employee growth, work-life balance, and CSR activities, positioning itself as a technology partner that combines "service" and "system" perspectives to fulfill client wishes. Their motto of "always moving one thing forward" reflects their commitment to continuous improvement and customer value maximization.
