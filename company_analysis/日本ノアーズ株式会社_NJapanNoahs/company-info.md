# 日本ノアーズ株式会社

## Company Overview

日本ノアーズ株式会社 (NOAH'S) is a Yokohama-based software development and IT services company founded in March 1998. The name "NOAH'S" embodies the company's aspiration to carry "Japan's tomorrow" — the belief that those working with computers bear the responsibility of shaping Japan's future. Noah's provides comprehensive software development across microcomputer, open systems, and general-purpose platforms, as well as server construction, operation, and monitoring services. The company maintains key client relationships with NEC, Hitachi Solutions, and other major Japanese technology firms.

### Company Name Origin
"日本ノアーズ" — the name reflects the belief that those in computing carry "Japan's tomorrow" on their shoulders.

### Company Culture
"At-home" atmosphere with "親切丁寧" (kind and thorough) motto. Monthly employee gatherings called "前進" (Forward) where employees discuss company improvements — recent outcomes include skill-up programs and salary system revisions, including newly introduced certification allowances.
