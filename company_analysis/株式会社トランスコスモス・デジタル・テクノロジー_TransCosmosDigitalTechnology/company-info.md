# 株式会社トランスコスモス・デジタル・テクノロジー

## Company Overview

株式会社トランスコスモス・デジタル・テクノロジー (transcosmos digital technology inc.) is a Yokohama-based IT systems integrator and digital solutions provider. As part of the transcosmos group, the company specializes in system integration, cloud solutions, and digital transformation services. Their service portfolio includes PoC (Proof of Concept) support, business system construction and operation, web system development, system maintenance, and cloud solutions including Salesforce, ServiceNow, Power Automate, and UiPath (RPA). Additional offerings include cloud migration, security diagnostics, and legacy OS migration.
