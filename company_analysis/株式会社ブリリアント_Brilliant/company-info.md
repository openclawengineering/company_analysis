# 株式会社ブリリアント

## Company Overview

株式会社ブリリアント (Brilliant Co., Ltd.) is a Tokyo and Yamanashi-based system development company founded in 1993. With a mission to be a "technology group that continues to think about the best for customers," Brilliant specializes in computer system investigation, analysis, design, software development, and information services. The company operates from two bases — a Tokyo headquarters in Tachikawa and a Yamanashi headquarters in Fuefuki — and has over 30 years of experience in software development. Brilliant is ISO 9001 certified and holds membership in multiple industry associations including JISA, KIA, and YSA.
