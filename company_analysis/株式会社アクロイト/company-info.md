# 株式会社アクロイト (Acroit Corporation)

## Company Overview

Acroit Corporation is a Kanagawa-based software engineering company established in July 2019 through a corporate split from Ad Co., Ltd. (now Ad Soa Corporation, the parent company). The company specializes in software development, technical support, web design, and internet-based education consulting.

## Business Description

Acroit was created through a business split to focus specifically on software engineering — including business systems, web systems, embedded server systems, and network engineering. The company provides:

1. **Computer system technical support and custom development** — Primarily engaged in dispatched and contracted software development work
2. **Website and web content planning, design, and production** — Web system development capabilities
3. **Internet-based education and career education consulting** — Education support services

The company emphasizes flexible, speed-oriented management decisions and aims to propose "better products, better management, and better processes" to clients rather than merely developing systems.

## Key Characteristics

- **KSP (Kanagawa Science Park) tenant** — Located in KSP West Building, Kawasaki, alongside many other tech companies
- **Spin-off from established company** — Parent company Ad Soa Corporation provides stability; split allowed software-focused agility
- **Active in local industry associations** — President Tamura serves in multiple Kanagawa IT Industry Association committees including corporate management, industry-academia collaboration, and diversity
- **Community involvement** — Board member at local high school and vocational school committees
- **Government advisory role** — Member of Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare working group on workplace adaptation support
