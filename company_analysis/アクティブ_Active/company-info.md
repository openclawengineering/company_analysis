## 4. Products and Services

### Core Business Areas

#### 1. オリジナルソフトの企画･設計･開発 (Original Software Planning, Design & Development)
- Custom PC-based software systems tailored to client requirements
- Technologies used: **Visual Basic**, **Access**, **Java**
- Key advantages:
  - More flexible than off-the-shelf packages
  - Easy modifications possible for clients
  - Cost-effective PC-based solutions
  - Data from business operations can be utilized for reports/lists

#### 2. システムコンサルティング (System Consulting)
- System introduction, review, evaluation, and improvement consulting
- Advanced computer system utilization consulting
- Strategic information system planning and construction
- Comprehensive services from planning to operation

#### 3. ソフトウェア開発 (Software Development)
- Leveraging extensive experience in software and package development
- Business analysis and office workflow analysis
- Conceptual system construction
- System design based on client-approved basic requirements
- Program development

#### 4. インフラ系運用サービス (Infrastructure Operation Services)
- **Platforms:** UNIX, Windows, Linux
- Network construction and design
- Server construction and management
- Failure/trouble response
- Firewall and network security construction
- Router/switch network equipment configuration
- Based on extensive operational experience

#### 5. ホームページ作成サービス (Website Creation Service)
Two service tiers available:
- **Simple Production** (¥30,000): Using pre-designed templates; clients provide text and photos
- **Free-Order Production** (¥50,000+): Fully customized to client specifications

## 6. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Mission
> "アクティブはアウトソーシングを通してIT業界の発展に貢献する企業です"
> 
> *"Active is a company that contributes to the development of the IT industry through outsourcing."*

### Workplace Culture Characteristics

#### Positive Work Environment
- **Good interpersonal relationships** among employees
- **Excellent workplace atmosphere**
- **Employee opinions are easily reflected** in company decisions
- **Ideal for those who want to be involved in the core of management**

#### Work-Life Balance
- **Minimal overtime**
- **Generous time off**
- **Perfect for those who value their private life**
- **Telework-first policy** since May 2022 (new employees initially work on-site)

### Employee Development & Growth

#### Career Progression Path
```
Programmer → SE (Systems Engineer) → PL (Project Leader) → PM (Project Manager)
```

#### Training Programs
- **Leadership training**
- **Manager training**
- **Layer-by-layer (hierarchical) training programs**

#### Evaluation & Compensation
- **Concrete evaluation system** for transparent performance assessment
- **Fair salary structure** aligned with "equal pay for equal work"
- **Individual development plans**
- **Mentor system** for employee support

### Ideal Candidate Profile
The company seeks employees who:
1. Can consistently perform assigned tasks
2. Have the ability to acquire necessary skills independently and solve problems
3. Are diligent and interested in new technologies
4. Treat others with respect and empathy, never looking down on others

### Recognition & Certifications

#### ハタラクエール (Hataraku-Elle) Certification
- Certified for **5 consecutive years** (2022-2026)
- Recognizes companies actively promoting **welfare benefits**
- Demonstrates commitment to:
  - Employee well-being
  - Work-life balance
  - Progressive workplace policies
