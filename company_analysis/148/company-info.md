# 株式会社ショウナンソフトウェア (Shonan Software Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社ショウナンソフトウェア (Shonan Software Co., Ltd.) is a regional IT solution company based in the Shonan area of Kanagawa Prefecture. They provide comprehensive IT services from consulting to operation and maintenance.

## Business Description

The company develops software including business applications, network-related systems, and communication/control systems. They provide total services from consulting to operation and maintenance.

### Services
- Business application development
- Network system development
- Communication/control system development
- Package software
- Consulting from design/development to maintenance/operation
- Complete IT services
