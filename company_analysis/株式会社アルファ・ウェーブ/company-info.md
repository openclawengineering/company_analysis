# 株式会社アルファ・ウェーブ (Alpha Wave Inc.)

## Company Overview

Alpha Wave Inc. is an independent system development company founded 41+ years ago. Headquartered in Kumamoto, Kyushu, the company is also an expert group in the semiconductor field, providing comprehensive solutions from equipment fabrication to parts sales. The company has grown by realizing the needs and ideas of semiconductor factories across Kyushu, Japan, and Asian countries. Their slogan is "No Limit to Scope of the System."

The company cultivates engineers through in-house training regardless of science or humanities backgrounds, leveraging individual sensibilities. Veteran technicians with 30+ years of semiconductor experience provide dedicated support.
