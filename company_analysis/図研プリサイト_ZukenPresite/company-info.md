# 株式会社図研プリサイト (Zuken Presite Inc.)

## Overview
株式会社図研プリサイト is a Japanese IT company specializing in PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) systems and knowledge management solutions. The company focuses on virtual verification in the pre-prototyping stage, providing hybrid electrical and mechanical design review (DR) solutions.

**Website:** https://www.presight.co.jp/

## History
Part of the Zuken Group, a major Japanese EDA (Electronic Design Automation) software company. Zuken Presite focuses on providing solutions that enable virtual verification before physical prototyping.

## Capital
Part of Zuken Group (株式会社図研)

## Services
- **PLM Systems:** Product Lifecycle Management solutions
- **Knowledge Management Systems:** Corporate knowledge organization and management
- **DR Solutions:** Design Review solutions for electrical and mechanical hybrid systems
- **Virtual Verification:** 3D model-based verification for safety standards
  - Insulation distance measurement (spatial and surface distances)
  - Static electricity and EMC verification
- **Lightweight 3D Models:** Ultra-lightweight 3D models for verification purposes

## Clients
Manufacturing companies requiring electronic design automation and PLM solutions, particularly in the electrical and mechanical engineering sectors.

## Philosophy
Focused on enabling virtual verification in pre-prototyping stages to reduce development time and costs while ensuring compliance with safety regulations.
