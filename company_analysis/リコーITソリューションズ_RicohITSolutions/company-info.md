## 3. Company History & Milestones

### Founding and Early Development (1982-1997)
- **October 1982:** Founded as Ricoh System Development Co., Ltd. (Tokyo) to undertake domestic software development functions within the Ricoh Group
- **September 1983:** Ricoh Tottori Technical Development Co., Ltd. established
- **October 1989:** Ricoh System Development Co., Ltd. (Kitami) established
- **April 1990:** Ricoh System Development Co., Ltd. (Akita) established
- **October 1990:** Ricoh System Development Co., Ltd. (Kagoshima) established
- **April 1993:** Ricoh Tottori Technical Development Co., Ltd. Kanazawa Development Office opened
- **April 1997:** Ricoh System Development Co., Ltd. (Kitami) Sapporo Development Center opened

### Integration and Expansion (2005-2014)
- **January 2005:** Integrated five software development companies (Ricoh System Development Tokyo/Kitami/Akita/Kagoshima and Ricoh Tottori Technical Development) to strengthen solution capabilities and improve software development efficiency. Changed company name to Ricoh Software Co., Ltd.
- **July 2009:** Integrated SE personnel from Ricoh Software Co., Ltd., Ricoh Technosystems Co., Ltd., Ricoh Hokkaido Co., Ltd., Ricoh Sales Co., Ltd., Ricoh Kansai Co., Ltd., and Ricoh Company, Ltd. Changed company name to Ricoh IT Solutions Co., Ltd.
- **April 2011:** Acquired all shares of Softcom Co., Ltd.
- **July 2014:** Transferred B2B IT business to Ricoh Japan Co., Ltd.

### Recent Developments (2017-2026)
- **April 2017:** Transferred some of Ricoh Company, Ltd.'s information system division functions
- **April 2025:** Nagasaki Office opened
- **January 2026:** Concluded location agreement with Kagawa Prefecture and Takamatsu City
- **March 2026:** Takamatsu Office opening ceremony announced
- **March 2026:** Released beta version of "RICOH Short Video Designer" - an AI-powered short video planning support tool
- **March 2026:** Participated as a partner company in Kanagawa Prefecture's largest innovation summit "Kanagawa Innovators Day 2026"

### Corporate Evolution
The company has evolved from multiple regional software development subsidiaries into a unified IT solutions company, consolidating technical capabilities and expanding service offerings within the Ricoh Group.

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
リコーITソリューションズ株式会社 is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Ricoh Company, Ltd., established in 1982 as the IT backbone of the Ricoh Group. With 988 employees and 250 million yen in capital, the company operates from 9 strategic locations across Japan, providing comprehensive IT solutions and software development services.

### Core Strengths
1. **Technical Excellence:** Four decades of experience in software development and IT infrastructure, with strong technical capabilities in embedded systems, cloud platforms, and AI applications
2. **Group Integration:** As a 100% Ricoh-owned subsidiary, the company has deep integration with Ricoh Group's business operations and strategic direction
3. **Regional Presence:** Nationwide network of offices enabling regional development and local talent recruitment
4. **Full-Service Capability:** End-to-end services from planning and development to operation and maintenance
5. **Innovation Focus:** Active development of cutting-edge solutions including AI, IoT, and digital transformation services

### Business Model
The company operates two primary business pillars:
1. **Internal Ricoh Group Support:** Providing IT infrastructure, software development, and system integration services for the entire Ricoh Group ecosystem
2. **External Solution Services:** Developing and commercializing innovative products like Digital Guide Deck ARGS for the tourism industry

### Competitive Advantages
- **Strong Parent Company Support:** Backed by Ricoh's global presence and resources
- **Technical Track Record:** 40+ years of successful software development for complex industrial products
- **Geographic Diversity:** Offices across major regions of Japan, enabling talent acquisition and regional economic contribution
- **Certifications and Standards:** Multiple industry certifications demonstrating quality and compliance excellence
- **Agile Innovation:** Rapid development and deployment of new products and services

### Growth Strategy
The company is pursuing several strategic initiatives:
- **Digital Transformation:** Expanding DX capabilities and obtaining government certifications

- **Product Innovation:** Developing new products like Digital Guide Deck ARGS and Short Video Designer
- **Talent Development:** Investing in digital talent training and career development programs
- **AI Integration:** Leveraging AI for business solutions and product development

As a key IT services provider within the Ricoh Group, the company plays a critical role in:
