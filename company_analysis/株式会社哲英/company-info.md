# 株式会社哲英 (Tetsuei Inc.)

## Company Overview

Tetsuei Inc. is a management and IT consulting company founded on April 1, 2014, headquartered in Shin-Yokohama, Kohoku-ku, Yokohama, Kanagawa. The company provides business consulting and IT consulting services. Tetsuei operates the "Shin-Yokohama Software Development Center (SYSDeC)" and is an official supporter of the Yokohama DeNA BayStars (professional baseball team). In 2021, the company received the IT Coordinator Association 20th Anniversary Commemorative Excellence Award.

**Website:** https://tetsuei-japan.co.jp/
