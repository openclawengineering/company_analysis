## 8. Summary

Apricot Co., Ltd. is a well-established Japanese IT services company with over 55 years of continuous operation. Founded in 1971 with the motto "Trust and Service," the company has grown from a small Tokyo-based startup to a multi-branch organization with 280 employees and ¥90 million in capital.
