## Company Profile

株式会社ICON is a long-established company with nearly 30 years of history, originally focused on software engineer support services. In recent years, the company has shifted its focus to educational technology, particularly the innovative programming education toy **"KUMIITA"**.

### Company Evolution
- Started as software engineer support specialist
- Expanded into educational technology with KUMIITA
- Diversified into security solutions
- Global expansion of KUMIITA product

### 3. ものづくり事業・教育事業 (Manufacturing & Education Business)

- 教育用ロボットの研究、開発、販売 (Research, development, and sales of educational robots)
- Designed for ages 0 to adults with no programming experience
- Self-propelled robot that moves on panels with instructions
- Develops logical thinking through panel arrangement
