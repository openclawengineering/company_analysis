# 株式会社宮川製作所 (Miyagawa Seisakusho Co., Ltd.)

## Overview
株式会社宮川製作所 is a Japanese manufacturing and technology company with over 85 years of history, specializing in "connecting technologies." The company provides IoT solutions, RFID systems, face recognition systems, environmental monitoring, and audio/visual products. They serve various sectors including transportation, communications, energy, fire/disaster prevention, factories, agriculture, and logistics.

**English Name:** Miyagawa Seisakusho Co., Ltd.
**Website:** https://www.msk.co.jp/

## History
- Founded over 85 years ago
- Started with manufacturing microphones and coils for telephones
- Consistently focused on "connecting technologies" to deliver human voices more naturally and reliably
- Evolved to include sensor technologies for IoT applications
- Company motto: "誠実・努力" (Sincerity and Effort)

## Services
1. **RFID Solutions (rafis)**
   - Custom RFID solutions combining proprietary hardware and software
   - Tailored to customer-specific requirements

2. **Face Recognition Solutions**
   - Japanese-made face recognition terminals and software
   - Knoctoi series with edge AI and NFC readers
   - Various business requirement support

3. **Environmental Monitoring Solutions**
   - Factory and building monitoring
   - Remote energy monitoring
   - Power, temperature, and humidity monitoring
   - Scalable from small to large installations

4. **LTE Routers**
   - M2M/IoT-ready LTE communication terminals
   - Industrial routers (mobile gateways)
   - Japanese-made products

5. **Audio/Visual Products**
   - Over 80 years of product development experience
   - Products designed for easy use anywhere
