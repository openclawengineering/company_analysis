# 株式会社アイネット・データサービス (INET Data Service Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

INET Data Service Co., Ltd. is a special subsidiary of the INET Group that specializes in paper document digitization services. The company collects paper documents such as ledgers and receipts from enterprises, and digitizes purchase, sales, and customer-related paper data. The company is part of the INET Group (I-NET Corp.), which was founded in 1971 and provides cloud services and data center operations.

The company has been recognized for its exceptional workplace health management, receiving the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry's "Certified Health Management Organization" for 3 consecutive years (2023-2025) and Yokohama Health Management Certification at the highest Class AAA level.
