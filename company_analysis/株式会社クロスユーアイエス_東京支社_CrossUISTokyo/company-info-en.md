# CROSS UIS Co., Ltd.

## Company Overview

CROSS UIS Co., Ltd. is a system integrator founded in January 1987. Formerly known as "Unichika Information Systems Co., Ltd." (renamed in June 2015). It operates from three offices: Osaka HQ (Minamisenba), Nagoya branch, and Tokyo branch. As of April 2026, it employs 95 people with a capital of 100 million yen. The company holds ISO27001 and ISO9001 certifications. Its business spans information processing services and system development contracting, FA (Factory Automation) system development, general-purpose software package development/sales, and IT education/consulting. It was certified as a "System Integrator Enterprise" by the Ministry of International Trade and Industry (MITI) in 1991.
