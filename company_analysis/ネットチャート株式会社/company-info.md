# Company Analysis Report: ネットチャート株式会社 (Net Chart Japan Inc.)

## Executive Summary

**ネットチャート株式会社** (Net Chart Japan Inc.) is a Japanese IT company specializing in network construction, maintenance, and security services. Founded in 2006 as a 100% subsidiary of Internet Initiative Japan Inc. (IIJ), the company provides comprehensive network infrastructure solutions, system support, facility management, and disaster recovery services. Headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, with additional offices in Tokyo and Sendai, Net Chart Japan serves clients across Japan with a focus on network reliability and security.
