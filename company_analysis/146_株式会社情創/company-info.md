# 株式会社情創 (Johso Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社情創 (Johso Co., Ltd.) is a system development company that aims to provide optimal systems and courtesy/reverence (礼節) to customers. The company celebrated its 25th anniversary in August 2025.

## Business Description

### Contracted Development (情創の受託開発)
The company handles various types of projects, using their experience and achievements to provide optimal solutions that meet customer requirements by utilizing specialized knowledge and technology in specific industries and technical fields.

### Manufacturing Industry Comprehensive Solutions (製造業向け総合ソリューション)
Supports production line and manufacturing process efficiency, optimizes inventory management and supply chain, and achieves cost reduction and production flexibility.

### Education Industry Solutions (教育事业向けソリューション)
Comprehensive support for DX transformation in educational settings using open-source software, from construction to operation support. Adapts to diverse learning styles and enhances the quality and efficiency of education.

### Johso Trend Technology Solutions (情創トレンド技術ソリューション)
Has extensive achievements in various fields, from business efficiency tools and modern development using hot frameworks to ICT utilization support services. Supports customer problem-solving and growth with technology power.

### Gorikai (ゴリカイ)
"Gorikai" represents Johso's young developers who want to do full-scale small-scale development with Java or Python. It's an initiative to provide cost-effective development opportunities for young staff to gain experience.

## Company Philosophy

### Core Value
"最適なシステムと礼節を提供する"

Translation: "Providing optimal systems and courtesy/reverence"

### Service Approach
"お客様と共に 最終GOALを見据え 迅速・柔軟・真摯に 全力で取り組みます。"

Translation: "Together with customers, keeping the final GOAL in sight, we work with speed, flexibility, and sincerity with full power."

### Gorikai Philosophy
"情創の若手がJavaやPythonで小規模開発を思い切りやりたい！という想いが炸裂しております。安価でOKです。経験を積ませるためにゴリゴリと開発させてやりたい、先輩一同のおもいをご理解ください。宜しくお願い致します。"

Translation: "Our young staff at Johso want to do full-scale small-scale development with Java or Python! Low cost is fine. To let them gain experience, we want to let them develop vigorously. Please understand the feelings of all seniors. Thank you."
