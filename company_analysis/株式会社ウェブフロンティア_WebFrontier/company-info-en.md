# WebFrontier Co., Ltd.

## Company Overview

WebFrontier Co., Ltd. is a system development company established in September 2005. Headquartered in Yokohama Minatomirai (Landmark Tower, 31st floor), it operates an Osaka office and Fukuoka satellite office. Its core businesses include system consulting, software research/development/sales, and software package development/sales/maintenance. As of April 2026, it employs 137 people. The company holds ISMS (ISO27001), Privacy Mark, and worker dispatch business permits. It has three group companies: THEMISYS Co., Ltd. (established January 2025), GOOD EFFECT Co., Ltd. (established March 2021), and Umiria Co., Ltd. (established February 2021). Major clients include Kyocera, NTT Data NJK, Nippon Steel Solutions, Sky Corporation, Fujisoft, Justsystem, Systena, Userbase, and Revamp.

**Website:** https://webfrontier.co.jp/
