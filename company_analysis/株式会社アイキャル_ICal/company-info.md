## Management Philosophy

### 経営理念 (Management Philosophy)
1. **人の成長** (Human Growth): 人や環境を通じて己を磨き高める (Polish and elevate oneself through people and environment)
2. **会社の発展** (Company Development): 人の役に立ち喜ばれ、感謝される仕事をする (Be helpful to others and do work that is appreciated and thanked)
3. **社会へ貢献** (Social Contribution): 企業活動で利益を出し、社員の生活を守り社会貢献を果たす (Generate profits through business activities, protect employees' livelihoods, and contribute to society)

### アイキャルスローガン (I-Cal Slogan)
人、会社、社会の調和をとり豊かな社会を築く (Harmonize people, company, and society to build a rich society)
