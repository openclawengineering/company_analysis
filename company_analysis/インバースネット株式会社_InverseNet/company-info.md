# インバースネット株式会社 (InverseNet)

## Company Overview

インバースネット株式会社 (InverseNet Co., Ltd.) is a comprehensive trading company specializing in used IT equipment, computer recycling, and BTO (Build-To-Order) computer manufacturing. Established in 1951 and part of the Yamada Holdings Group, the company has evolved from a communication equipment scrap processing business to a major player in the IT equipment lifecycle management industry.

The company operates nationwide with multiple locations and manages "Shop Inverse" retail stores across Japan. They focus on creating value through the entire lifecycle of IT equipment, from new custom-built computers to recycling and reuse, with strong emphasis on information security and environmental responsibility.

**Website:** [https://www.inversenet.co.jp](https://www.inversenet.co.jp)

## History

- February 1951: Company founded (Showa 22)
- December 14, 1951: Officially incorporated as a company (Showa 26)
- Originally started as communication equipment scrap processing business
- Expanded into IT equipment recycling and sales
- 2013: Started handling "FRONTIER" brand BTO computers
- Continued expansion of nationwide retail operations
- Acquired various certifications including ISO 27001 for information security
- Developed comprehensive IT asset lifecycle management services

## Services/Products

### Brand Business
- **BTO Computer Manufacturing** - Custom-built PCs and small office products
- **Product Planning & Development** - Original IT equipment development
- **Quality Manufacturing** - Strict quality control and testing processes

### Reuse Business (Purchase & Sales)
- **Computer & Smartphone Purchase** - Buying used IT equipment from individuals and corporations
