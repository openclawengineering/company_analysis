# 株式会社アローズ・システムズ (Arrows Systems Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Arrows Systems Co., Ltd. is a software development company based in Minatomirai, Yokohama. Founded in 1999 (Heisei 11), the company specializes in web system development, primarily through direct contracts with major internet companies, focusing on B2C web services and content development. The company is part of a group that includes lodging facility customer acquisition support company "Shuken."
