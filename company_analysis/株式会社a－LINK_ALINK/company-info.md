# 株式会社a－LINK (a-LINK Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社a－LINK (a-LINK) is a software development company based in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. The company operates as a member of the CIJ Group — a publicly listed (Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime) independent SIer with over 20 years of trading history with major manufacturers.

a-LINK specializes in proposal-based software contract development, offering both domestic and offshore development capabilities. The company maintains a team of domestic SE engineers with broad cutting-edge technical skills, along with an overseas subsidiary that provides cost-effective, high-quality deliverables. The combined domestic and international capabilities enable a-LINK to deliver advanced technology solutions at competitive prices.

The company focuses on embedded system development, C#/C/C++ programming, and JavaScript development. Notable project experience includes multi-function printer print applications and image analysis cloud platforms for major listed equipment manufacturers. The company also actively pursues in-house product development and self-directed technology theme development.

**Website:** [https://www.alink-group.com/](https://www.alink-group.com/)
