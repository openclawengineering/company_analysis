# 株式会社システムズナカシマ 東京支店 (Systems Nakashima Co., Ltd. Tokyo Branch)

## Company Overview

株式会社システムズナカシマ (Systems Nakashima Co., Ltd.) is a comprehensive system development company with offices in Okayama, Tokyo, and Osaka. The Tokyo branch is one of their key locations.

## Business Description

The company provides a wide range of solutions across multiple domains:

### Equipment Solutions
- Design solutions for construction and facility industries
- CAD systems for plumbing, electrical, and fire protection
- PDF drawing conversion systems

### Business Solutions
- SFA systems on kintone platform
- AI-powered engines
- Calendar applications
- Survey tools
- Groupware solutions
- Web expo tools

### Manufacturing Solutions
- Production management systems
- 2D CAD systems

### Education Solutions
- Educational technology solutions

### AI/IoT/RFID Solutions
- AI platform "roboflow"
- IoT and RFID integration

### Municipal Solutions
- Solutions for local government

### Medical Field
- Healthcare-related systems

### Overseas Business
- International operations and partnerships
