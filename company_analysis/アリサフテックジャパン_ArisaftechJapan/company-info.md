# Company Analysis Report

## アリサフテック・ジャパン株式会社
### Arisaftech Japan Co., Ltd. (AriSaf Tech Ltd.)

---

## Executive Summary

Arisaftech Japan is a Japanese IT outsourcing company with a unique dual-shore business model, maintaining its sales and business development office in Yokohama, Japan, and its development center in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The company specializes in comprehensive software development services, from web and mobile applications to cutting-edge AI solutions.

- Bangladesh Development Center: House # 1048 (1st Floor), Road # 09 (New), Avenue # 09, Mirpur DOHS, Dhaka-1216, Bangladesh
