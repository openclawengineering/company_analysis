# 株式会社ハクエイ

## Company Overview

株式会社ハクエイ (Hakuei Co., Ltd.) is a Yokohama-based SES (System Engineering Services) company. The company provides IT engineering staffing and software development services, specializing in application development (Java, C++, C#, C, Python, PHP, Perl, PL/SQL, VB, Shell) and infrastructure construction (Linux, Windows Server, AWS, Azure). Hakuei actively recruits inexperienced IT engineers and provides educational programs in collaboration with partner companies. The company is a member of the 神奈川県情報サービス産業協会 (KIA), 高度情報技術活用コンソーシアム (AITUC), and 神奈川東部職業能力開発推進協議会. The company has also engaged in international recruitment, conducting hiring activities in Myanmar.

**Website:** https://www.hakuei-net.co.jp/
