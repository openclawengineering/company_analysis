# 株式会社ＮＴＴデータＩＭジェイエスピー (NTT Data IM JSP Inc.)

## Company Overview

NTT Data IM JSP Inc. (formerly JSP Inc. / ジェイエスピー株式会社) is a software and system development company based in Yokohama and Tokyo, Japan. Founded in 1980 and established as a corporation in 2024, the company became a wholly-owned subsidiary of NTT Data IntraMart in September 2023. The company specializes in IoT sensing technology, providing factory IoT systems, business systems, and engineering services.

As a member of the NTT Data Group, the company aims to contribute to the realization of IOWN and digital twins while continuing its existing business. The company's mission is to use IT technology to make customers, employees, and society happy.
