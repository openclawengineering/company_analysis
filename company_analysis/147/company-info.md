# 湘南技術センター株式会社 (Shonan Technology Center, Inc.)

## Company Overview

湘南技術センター株式会社 (Shonan Technology Center, Inc.) is celebrating its 50th anniversary as a leading company that combines high technical expertise in various design fields with IT technology to provide multi-technical support. They continue to challenge new stages.

## Business Description

### Engineering Services (エンジニアリングサービス)
- Contract design (mechanical, electrical, control, residential equipment)
- Technical personnel dispatch
- Design and manufacturing
- Contracted analysis (structural/thermal/fluid analysis)
- Printed circuit board defect location/repair

### System Integration (システムインテグレーション)
- System development (on-premises/cloud)
- Website construction
- CAD customization and design automation
- Self-developed IoT technology
- AI utilization promotion

### Education/Development (教育 / 開発)
- Instructor dispatch
- Introduction support
- Research and development
- Industry-academia collaboration

### Environmental Energy (環境エネルギー)
- Solar power generation
- O&M (Operation and Maintenance)
- Electricity supply agency
