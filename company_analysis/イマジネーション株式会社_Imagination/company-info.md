# イマジネーション株式会社 (Imagination)

## Company Overview

イマジネーション株式会社 (Imagination.LMT) is a media and entertainment company established in 2008, specializing in the planning, production, development, and sales of various media content. Based in Tokyo's Shinagawa ward, the company focuses on television programs, commercials, web content, and various other media-related businesses.

The company has a diverse portfolio including TV program production, commercial production, book/magazine publishing, web content creation, smartphone application development, toy planning, game development, and creative agency services. They are known for producing popular TV shows and working with major broadcasters and brands.

**Website:** [http://www.imagination.jp](http://www.imagination.jp)

## History

- April 2008: Company established in Shinagawa-ku, Tokyo
- 2008-2026: Expanded into various media production fields including TV, web, and mobile content
- Developed relationships with major broadcasters including TV Asahi, TV Tokyo, Nippon Television, and Fuji Television
- Created content for major brands and corporations
