# 株式会社Fabbi Japan (Fabbi Japan Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Fabbi Japan is a technology company that serves as the Japanese arm of the Fabbi Group, a Vietnam-originated ICT company. Founded in February 2019 in Tokyo, Fabbi Japan provides software development, offshore development, DX (digital transformation) consulting, Agile/Scrum development, cloud services, AI services, and Salesforce development to the Japanese market.

The company was selected as one of Japan's "Best Venture 100" in 2023, making it the only Vietnamese-origin company to receive this distinction. Fabbi Group maintains over 300 employees across Vietnam and Japan, with a customer satisfaction rate exceeding 95%. The group has delivered 350+ projects across 90+ clients in industries including education, finance, healthcare, and e-commerce.

Fabbi's parent company, 株式会社Fabbi, was established in 2018 in Hanoi, Vietnam, with group companies Fabbi AI and Fabbi CRM also launched in 2020. The group has achieved ISO/IEC 27001:2013 and ISO 9001:2015 certifications, and received multiple awards including the Vietnam IT Association's "Top 10 Fastest Growing ICT Companies 2021" and "Top 10 AI-IoT Companies 2021."

## Philosophy

"Technology that moves the world from Asia." Fabbi is a technology partner centered on the Japanese market with global reach. The company believes in providing optimal solutions "fast, deep, and sustainable" to each client. The company motto is "Employees are the spirit of the company" — investing in people as the foundation for growth. Vu Van Tu's vision includes global expansion beyond Japan and Vietnam, with aspirations to build a company of tens of thousands of employees.
