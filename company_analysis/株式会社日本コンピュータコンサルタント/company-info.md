# 株式会社日本コンピュータコンサルタント (Nihon Computer Consultant Co., Ltd. / NCC)

## Company Overview

Nihon Computer Consultant Co., Ltd. (NCC) is an IT company founded in 1980 as a partner of a Hitachi-affiliated software company. With 40+ years of development track record, NCC has been solving challenges for a wide range of clients including banks, securities companies, and government agencies. The company operates under the mission of "Contributing to the development of social infrastructure through the power of technology."

NCC's strengths are its trusted quality and execution capabilities, built through thorough adherence to quality and deadlines over four decades as a partner to major domestic SIers, combined with the flexibility to respond to on-site needs.
