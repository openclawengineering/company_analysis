## 10. Summary

**株式会社ソフテム (Softem Co., Ltd.)** is an independent system integrator based in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture, with approximately 40 years of experience in the IT industry. The company provides comprehensive IT solutions including contract development, technical staffing (SES), and network infrastructure services.

### Key Strengths:
1. **Long History** - Nearly 40 years of industry experience
2. **Diverse Service Portfolio** - Development, staffing, and network services
3. **Proprietary Products** - Specialized solutions for research, child welfare, and police departments
4. **AI Innovation** - Implementing interactive AI solutions
5. **Public Sector Focus** - Strong presence in government and institutional markets

### Notable Products:
1. **ChemWatcher** - Chemical management for research institutions
2. **Jiso Network** - Child consultation system for local governments
3. **PoliceOnline** - Police consultation management system
4. **nobado** - Work style reform dashboard

### Business Model:
- Contract software development
- System engineering services (SES)
- Network design and operations
- Proprietary software products

### Competitive Advantages:
- Four decades of accumulated technical expertise

- Combination of traditional and modern technologies
- One-stop development capability
