## 4. Products and Services

### 4.1 Proprietary Products (かいけつシリーズ - "Kaikestu Series")

#### Attendance Management Systems
| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **かいけつ出席 授業版 (Class Version)** | IC card-based attendance management for universities and vocational schools - fast and simple |
| **かいけつ出席 イベント版 (Event Version)** | Attendance management for irregular events like training, meetings, seminars |
| **かいけつ出席 入退履歴版 (Entry/Exit Version)** | Records entry/exit to specific areas, calculates dwell time |
| **かいけつ出席 システム連携 (System Integration)** | Data integration with third-party attendance management systems |

#### Child Safety Products
| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **置ききり防止装置 KT-Z01 (Left-Behind Prevention Device)** | Certified product (A-038) to prevent children from being left in school buses; uses small transmitters worn by children |
| **見守り MM-AP01 (Monitoring System)** | Helps prevent children from getting lost or left behind during outdoor activities |
| **児童見守りシステム (Child Monitoring System)** | Displays children's attendance status on large monitors for staff to share |

## 7. Summary

**株式会社アルファメディア (Alpha Media Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese IT company with over 30 years of history since its founding in 1992. Based in Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture, with branches in Kobe and Nagoya, the company operates across multiple business domains:

**Core Strengths:**
1. **Proprietary Products**: The "Kaikestu" (かいけつ) series of attendance management and child safety systems
2. **Contracted Development**: Full-stack software and hardware development capabilities
3. **IT Staffing**: Technical personnel dispatch and SES services
4. **Education**: Operating Fujitsu Open College with programs for seniors, children, and corporations

**Notable Achievements:**
- World's first Japanese Sign Language electronic dictionary (1996)
- Patent holder for visually impaired support technology
- Partnerships with major J-League soccer clubs for programming education
- Kawasaki SDGs Gold Partner and Monozukuri Brand certification

The company demonstrates a strong commitment to social good through products focused on child safety, accessibility for the disabled, and IT education—all aligned with their philosophy of creating "a people-friendly society through co-creation and transformation."
