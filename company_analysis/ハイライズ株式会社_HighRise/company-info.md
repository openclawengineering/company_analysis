# ハイライズ株式会社 (High Rise Corporation)

## Company Overview

ハイライズ株式会社 (High Rise Corporation) is a software development company specializing in workforce management solutions. The company developed "Smart One Call," a system for managing attendance at work sites.

## Services/Products

### Smart One Call - Work Site Attendance Management System

A cloud-based system for managing worker attendance at construction and work sites.

**Key Features:**
- Automatic attendance tracking with just one phone call
- Real-time monitoring of sites with absent workers
- Immediate response to customer attendance inquiries
- Monthly subscription starting from ¥15,000 (for up to 50 sites)

**Benefits:**
- Simple one-call check-in/check-out
- Real-time visibility of site attendance
- Quick response to customer inquiries
- Cost-effective implementation

## Clients

- Construction companies
- Temporary staffing agencies
- Companies with multiple work sites
- Field service companies

## Philosophy

The company focuses on simplifying workforce management for companies with multiple work sites, making attendance tracking easy and efficient.
