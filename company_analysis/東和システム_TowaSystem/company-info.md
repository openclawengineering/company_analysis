## 5. Products and Services

### Core Business Model
The company operates as a **contracted software development (受託開発)** company, providing customized IT solutions for clients across various industries.

### Service Offerings

#### 1. System Development (システム開発)
- Custom software development
- Full-stack development from design to implementation
- Application development for various platforms
- kintone business system implementation (Cybozu certified partner)
- Legacy system modernization
- Cloud migration solutions

#### 2. Infrastructure Construction (インフラ構築)
- Server design and construction
- Network infrastructure setup
- Cloud infrastructure deployment
- TOWA Cloud Service (proprietary cloud offering)
- IT infrastructure consulting

#### 3. Offshore Development
- Through wholly-owned subsidiary 大連遠東計算机系統有限公司 (Dalian, China)
- Cost-effective development with Japanese quality standards
- 24/7 development capability

#### 4. IT Operations Support
- System maintenance and operation
- Infrastructure monitoring
- Help desk services

### Key Strengths
- **Broad industry/business knowledge** × **Advanced development technology** × **Solid project execution capability**
- Half-century of accumulated technical expertise
- Strong relationship with Fujitsu as core partner
- Proven offshore development capabilities since 1989

## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Mission Statement
> "システム開発を通じて、社会のたゆまぬ発展に貢献し続けることを我々の存在意義/Missionと位置づけております。"
> 
> **Translation:** "We position our mission as continuously contributing to society's ceaseless development through system development."

### Core Values

#### "People Development" (人づくり) Culture
The company's foundational philosophy centers on nurturing human talent:

- **"First, try it ourselves"** (まずは自分たちの手でやってみる) - The spirit from founding days
- Employees build their own environments and take on challenges
- Fostering "self-thinking and action" abilities and "sense of unity"
- Growth happens in the "field" (現場) - working alongside colleagues on real projects

#### Philosophy from CEO (矢部 昭雄)
> "人が最も成長できるのは、仲間とともに挑戦する'現場'にある。そこには学びの種があり、成長のチャンスがあふれています。"
>
> **Translation:** "People grow most when they take on challenges together with colleagues in the 'field'. There are seeds of learning and overflowing opportunities for growth there."

### Cultural Beliefs
1. **Technology and humanity grow together** - Not just technical skills, but human capabilities
2. **Continuous growth** - "Human development has no goal"
3. **Strongest companies have growing people** - "Organizations where people continue to grow are the strongest companies"
4. **Co-creation approach** - Working with clients to share challenges, think together, and create solutions

### Work Environment
- **Flextime system** available
- **Dormitory available** for single employees
- **Annual holidays: 123+ days**
- Strong emphasis on training and development, especially for new graduates (including liberal arts majors learning programming from zero)

## 9. Summary

株式会社東和システム (Towa System Co., Ltd.) is a well-established Japanese software development company founded in 1972 with over 50 years of history. Headquartered in Tokyo with branches in Osaka and Nagoya, the company employs approximately 400 people and generates nearly 7 billion JPY in annual revenue.

### Key Differentiators:

1. **Strong Fujitsu Partnership** - As a Fujitsu Core Partner with 39.5% ownership by Fujitsu, the company has deep integration with one of Japan's largest IT companies, providing stable business and technical resources.

2. **Early Offshore Pioneer** - Since 1989, the company has operated offshore development in Dalian, China (now 100% owned subsidiary), demonstrating forward-thinking global strategy decades ahead of many competitors.

3. **"Human Development" Culture** - The company's distinctive philosophy emphasizes growing people alongside technology. Liberal arts graduates are trained in programming from zero, creating a diverse, adaptable workforce.

4. **Broad Industry Coverage** - From manufacturing to government, financial to social infrastructure, the company has accumulated expertise across virtually all major sectors of the Japanese economy.

5. **Stable Financial Growth** - Consistent revenue growth over recent years with strong profitability and a solid balance sheet (95 billion JPY in assets).

6. **Co-Creation Approach** - Rather than simply delivering technology, the company positions itself as a partner that thinks with clients and creates value together.

### Business Model:
The core business is contracted software development (受託開発), supplemented by infrastructure services, offshore development, and IT operations support. Revenue comes primarily from large enterprise clients and government organizations, with Fujitsu as the anchor client.

### Future Outlook:
With strong foundations in traditional system development, the company is positioning for the AI and cloud era, backed by stable finances and a culture of continuous learning and adaptation.
