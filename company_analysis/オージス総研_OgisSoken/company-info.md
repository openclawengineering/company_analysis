**Establishment:** June 29, 1983

**Key Milestones:**
- **1983:** Company founded as a subsidiary of Osaka Gas Co., Ltd.
- **2000s:** Expanded into comprehensive IT services including systems development and consulting
- **2010s:** Diversified into cloud services, IoT solutions, and embedded software development
- **2023 (June):** Celebrated 40th anniversary; established corporate "Purpose"
- **2024:** Launched AI-powered services including "Biz-AIナビ" and expanded SaaS offerings
- **2025:** Introduced new SaaS platform "Smoother-ServicePlatform®" and AI agent capabilities

## 9. Summary

株式会社オージス総研 (OGIS-RI Co., Ltd.) is a well-established Japanese IT services company founded in 1983 as a subsidiary of Osaka Gas Co., Ltd. With over 40 years of experience, the company has evolved from a traditional systems integrator into a comprehensive digital transformation partner.

**Key Strengths:**
1. **Diverse Service Portfolio:** Offers end-to-end solutions from consulting to system operation, covering systems development, cloud services, security, business applications, and specialized consulting services.

2. **Technology Leadership:** Strong expertise in emerging technologies including AI, IoT, cloud computing, and embedded systems. Notable products include the Biz-AIナビ™ generative AI platform and yonobi® rule-based development platform.

3. **Industry Expertise:** Serves major clients across manufacturing, financial services, energy, retail, and other sectors, with deep domain knowledge in each area.

4. **Innovation Focus:** Actively developing new SaaS platforms and AI-powered solutions to address modern business challenges.

5. **Social Responsibility:** Demonstrates commitment to CSR through PC donation programs, diversity initiatives, and environmental sustainability efforts.

6. **Strategic Growth:** Recent acquisitions and partnerships indicate expansion strategy, particularly in ERP business and Asia-Pacific region.

**Recent Developments:**
The company is actively pursuing digital transformation initiatives, launching AI-powered services, expanding SaaS offerings, and strengthening partnerships with major cloud providers like AWS. The recent acquisition of ERP business in the Philippines signals international growth ambitions.

**Market Position:**
As a mid-sized Japanese IT services company with strong backing from Osaka Gas, OGIS-RI occupies a solid position in the Japanese IT market, particularly known for its technical expertise in embedded systems, business rule management, and growing capabilities in AI and cloud services.

**Future Outlook:**
With its focus on AI integration, SaaS platforms, and international expansion, OGIS-RI appears well-positioned to capitalize on the ongoing digital transformation trends in Japan and the broader Asia-Pacific region.
