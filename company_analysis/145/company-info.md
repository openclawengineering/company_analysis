# 株式会社シャフト (Schaft Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社シャフト (Schaft Co., Ltd.) is a technology company specializing in mobile applications, websites, and e-commerce solutions. The company focuses on building products that people love.

## Business Description

### Services Offered
- Mobile application development
- Website development
- E-commerce solutions
- Communication systems

### Technical Specializations

#### Mobile
Development of 4G/LTE compatible data communication smart phones, Android applications, development/marketing/distribution of application games, multimedia, Felica support, and providing the latest technology knowledge to engineers.

#### Automotive Electronics
Automotive information systems, HMI (Human Machine Interface), ECUs, ITS fields, automotive device manufacturing/fabrication, in-vehicle communication devices, body control systems, communication drivers, various diagnostics.

#### Industrial Control
Including manufacturing site PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) control, HMI (Human Machine Interface) for controller devices, programming for controller devices, development of application/maintenance tools for controller devices, and providing the latest technology skills in corresponding fields.

#### Factory Automation
High-precision image inspection using high-performance industrial cameras, defect inspection, medical equipment image analysis, medical equipment development, support for medical equipment development and maintenance, and providing the latest technology skills for medical equipment fields.

#### Medical Equipment
Extensive development experience in robots, electric vehicles, medical equipment, etc., supporting various integrated systems including LS development boards, and providing the latest technology skills tailored to customer needs.

#### Smartphone/Formwork Development
Extensive development experience in robots, electric vehicles, medical equipment, etc., supporting various integrated systems including LS development boards, and providing the latest technology skills tailored to customer needs.
