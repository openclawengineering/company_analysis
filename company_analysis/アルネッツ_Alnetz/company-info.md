## 6. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Mission (ミッション)
> **「超速」で『価値ある未来』を創造する**
> *Create a "valuable future" at "super speed"*

The company believes that simply being able to develop systems is no longer enough to add value. By combining hardware and software development capabilities with exceptional speed, Alnetz delivers superior solutions that earn client trust.

### Vision (ビジョン)
> **火中の栗を拾い、大切な誰かのピンチを救う**
> *Pick chestnuts from the fire, save someone important from their crisis*

This vision embodies the spirit of selflessly helping others—whether customers, colleagues, or family—without fear of personal risk. It reflects the values built by founding members and employees over the years.

## Summary

**株式会社アルネッツ (Alnetz Inc.)** is a Japanese IT solutions company founded in 1998, now operating as a group company of FRONTEO. With over 25 years of experience, Alnetz distinguishes itself through its unique "one-stop" approach—handling both hardware and software development internally—and its commitment to "super speed" (超速) delivery.

The company's core offerings include:
- **Velmor LMS** - AI-powered learning management system
- **Mendix DX services** - Low-code development platform partnership with Siemens
- **IoT solutions** - Edge devices, sensors, digital signage
- **Cloud infrastructure** - AWS, Azure, GCP expertise
- **Custom system development** - Healthcare, manufacturing, education sectors

With a strong company culture built on speed, continuous learning, gratitude, and embracing failure as a path to success, Alnetz positions itself as a trusted partner for organizations undergoing digital transformation.
