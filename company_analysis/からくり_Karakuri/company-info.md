# 株式会社からくり (Kara-kuri Corporation)

## Company Overview

Kara-kuri Corporation is a Yokohama-based software development company founded in January 2013 by CEO Hideshi Shinagawa. With a team of 20 employees (average age: 33) and capital of 10 million JPY, the company specializes in contract software development, embedded systems, and SES (System Engineering Service).

The company name "Karakuri" is inspired by traditional Japanese mechanical devices that represented cutting-edge technology, brought surprise and joy, and contained creative ingenuity. The company aims to apply this traditional Japanese engineering spirit to modern software development.

**Website:** http://www.kara-kuri.jp/

## Philosophy

### Corporate Vision
As a Japan-origin IT company, the company aims to develop systems with the clever ingenuity of traditional karakuri mechanical devices — combining technical excellence, human-centered design, and creative engineering.

### Historical Inspiration
Referencing the 1796 mechanical engineering book "Karakuri Zui" by Hanzo Hosokawa: "Though it may seem like child's play, for those who perceive its depth, it can serve as an aid to innovation and new ideas."
