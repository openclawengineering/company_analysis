# 株式会社からくり (Kara-kuri Corporation)

## Company Overview

株式会社からくり is a Yokohama-based software development company founded in January 2013 (平成25年1月). The company specializes in software development, IT solutions including AI utilization, and worker dispatch services. A member of the Kanagawa IT Industry Association.
