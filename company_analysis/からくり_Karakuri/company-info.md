> からくりはその当時において先端技術が詰まった装置であり、その挙動が人々に驚きや感動を与えてくれ、そしてなんといっても様々な仕掛けにはユーモアがあり、人々を魅了しました。

### Core Philosophy (経営理念)
The company name "Karakuri" (からくり) is inspired by traditional Japanese mechanical devices that:
- Represented cutting-edge technology of their era
- Brought surprise and excitement to people
- Contained humor and creativity in their design

### Historical Inspiration
Reference is made to the 1796 mechanical engineering book "機巧図彙" (Karakuri Zui) by 細川半蔵 (Hanzo Hosokawa):

> 「実に児戯に等しけれども　見る人の斟酌に依ては起見生心の一助とも成なんかし」
> 
> *(Translation: "Though it may seem like child's play, for those who perceive its depth, it can serve as an aid to innovation and new ideas.")*

### Corporate Vision
- As a Japan-origin IT company, aim to develop systems with the clever ingenuity (粋な計らい) of traditional karakuri
- Apply traditional Japanese engineering spirit to modern software development

## 9. Summary

4. **Young, Dynamic Workforce:** Average employee age of 33, flexible work arrangements including remote work support
