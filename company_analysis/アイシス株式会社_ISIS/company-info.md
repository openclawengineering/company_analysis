**Website:** http://www.isis.co.jp / https://www.isis.co.jp

### Company Name Origin
The company name "isis" comes from the musical term "double sharp" (ダブルシャープ) meaning "up and up" in German notation. The founder, who was a piano teacher, named the company with the wish that it would continuously improve and grow.
