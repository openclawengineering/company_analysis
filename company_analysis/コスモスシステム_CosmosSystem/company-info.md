## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Management Philosophy (経営理念):
The company pursues three core principles:

1. **「高い技術力と品質」** (High Technical Capability and Quality)
2. **「社員が働きやすい会社」** (A Company Where Employees Can Work Comfortably)
3. **「安定した経営と成長力」** (Stable Management and Growth Potential)

### Core Values:
- **"人が命" (People are the Life)** - A philosophy unchanged since the founding of the parent company
- Focus on nurturing employees and achieving fruitful work
- Belief that employee fulfillment and stable lives lead to company growth

### Employee Development:
- Strong emphasis on technical education and training
- Practical C/C++ training programs (even for new graduates and career changers)
- Balanced team composition: curious young engineers, capable mid-career staff, and technically mature veterans
- High client satisfaction with employee capabilities

### Work Environment:
- Focus on employee motivation through:
  - Welfare benefits (福利厚生)
  - Internal events (社内イベント)
  - Various club activities (各種部活動)
- Many initiatives born from employee suggestions
- Family-friendly and accommodating workplace culture

### Representative's Message (太田正三 - Ota Masamitsu):
> "Since our founding, control systems have been the main pillar of our system development business. Compared to those days, the needs of the IT industry have diversified, and there is no end to the new technologies that are emerging one after another. To carry out such a wide range of tasks, excellent engineers are essential, so we put particular emphasis on technical education for our employees. We have a well-balanced lineup of curious young engineers, mid-career staff who can lead the next generation, and technically mature veterans, and we are realizing that our employees' achievements are highly appreciated by our customers."

### Environmental Commitment:
- Achieved 100% renewable energy in 2016
- Operating renewable energy power generation business since 2015
- Member of CHO (Child-rearing/Housework/Office work) Concept Promotion initiative

### Recruitment Philosophy:
- Accepts new graduates, mid-career candidates, and career changers
- Strong track record of developing inexperienced candidates into capable engineers
- Even veteran engineers often started from non-IT backgrounds
- Retiree re-employment program available

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview:
株式会社コスモスシステム (Cosmos System Co., Ltd.) is a specialized software development company established on April 1, 2025, as a wholly-owned subsidiary of 株式会社コスモス through a corporate split. The company inherited approximately 30 years of technical expertise and know-how from its parent company, which was founded in 1996.

### Key Strengths:
1. **Technical Expertise:** Specializes in control system development with strong C/C++ capabilities (80% of work), particularly in image processing, embedded systems, and industrial control applications
2. **Diverse Portfolio:** Serves multiple high-tech industries including aerospace (satellite control), semiconductors, printing equipment, environmental monitoring, and precision machinery
3. **Human-Centric Culture:** Strong emphasis on employee development and work-life balance, with comprehensive training programs for both experienced and inexperienced engineers
4. **Stable Foundation:** Backed by 30 years of parent company history and stable client relationships

### Business Model:
- Contract software development
- Embedded software development
- System Engineering Services (SES)
- Worker dispatch services (licensed)

### Market Position:
- Small but specialized player in Kanagawa's IT industry
- Focus on technically challenging, niche control system applications
- Strong in B2B relationships with industrial equipment manufacturers
- Member of KIA (Kanagawa Information Services Industry Association)

### Notable Characteristics:
- Revenue of ~301 million JPY (FY2025)
- 39 employees (36 technical, 3 management)
- Capital of 20 million JPY
- Privacy Mark certified (inherited from parent)
- 100% renewable energy operation (since 2016)
- Comprehensive recruitment program (new graduates, mid-career, internships)

### Future Outlook:
As a newly independent company with a strong technical foundation and established client relationships, Cosmos System Co., Ltd. is positioned to continue its specialized work in control systems development while maintaining its employee-centric culture. The company's focus on challenging technical domains (image processing, satellite systems, precision equipment) and its comprehensive training programs suggest a strategy of sustainable growth through technical excellence and human resource development.
