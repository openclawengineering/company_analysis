# 株式会社新日本コンピュータサービス (Shin-Nippon Computer Service Inc.)

## Company Overview

株式会社新日本コンピュータサービス (Shin-Nippon Computer Service Inc.) is an information services company based in Yamato City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Established in 1971, the company has over 50 years of experience in providing IT services.

## Business Description

The company combines three businesses to guide customers to problem-solving:

### Human Resource Support Services (人材支援サービス)
Provides flexible business support according to busy/quiet periods. From a few people to a group of personnel, they respond flexibly to various needs, adapting to business volume changes such as seasonal variations.

### Data Entry Services (データ入力サービス)
Utilizing cultivated processing technology as a founding business, they perform rapid and accurate data processing. With proper security environment, they handle information safely so customers can entrust them with peace of mind.

### System Construction (システム構築)
From extensive experience in both open and host system software development to server, PC, and network internal infrastructure construction, they solve various customer problems related to information systems.

### One-Stop Services
By collaborating with specialized companies such as major call centers and printing companies, they can provide comprehensive one-stop services with rich experience and achievements.

## Services

The company addresses various customer concerns:

1. **No know-how, labor shortage** → Human Resource Support Services
   - From simple tasks to complex business, total support from business design

2. **Want to digitize large amounts of paper or PDF** → Data Entry Services
   - Supporting customer data digitization according to all requirements from paper media to PDF files

3. **Old systems, want to improve business efficiency** → System Development Services
   - Proposing business efficiency through business analysis and systematization

4. **Don't understand servers or networks** → IT Infrastructure Support Services
   - Solving all customer concerns about PC, server, and network from proposal to introduction to maintenance

5. **Can't fix Access, becoming complex** → Access Solution Proposals
   - Proposing alternative solutions through various services and tools

## Access Solutions

The company provides solutions related to Microsoft Access, including alternatives when Access systems become too complex or need modernization.
