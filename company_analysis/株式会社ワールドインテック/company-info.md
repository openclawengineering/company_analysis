# 株式会社ワールドインテック (World Intec Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

World Intec Co., Ltd. is a large-scale human resources dispatch and consulting company, founded in 2014. A 100% subsidiary of World Holdings Inc. (TSE Prime, Securities Code: 2429), the company provides manufacturing outsourcing, IT services, and staffing solutions across Japan. The company pursues "Co-Sourcing" — a new form of outsourcing that creates forms where people can thrive in the diversified working styles of the modern era.

The company operates multiple specialized divisions including manufacturing support, IT services (World Intec IT), R&D support (World Intec RD), and clinical development, with group companies spanning Japan, Taiwan, and the United States.

**Website:** https://www.witc.co.jp/
