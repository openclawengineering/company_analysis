# 株式会社情創 (Johso Co., Ltd.)

## Overview
株式会社情創 is a Japanese IT company specializing in system development and providing optimal solutions with courtesy. The company celebrated its 25th anniversary in August 2025, demonstrating long-term stability and experience in the IT industry.
