# Company Analysis Report: アマノ株式会社 (Amano Corporation)

## Executive Summary

**Company Name:** アマノ株式会社 / Amano Corporation  
**Website:** https://www.amano.co.jp/  
**Headquarters:** 〒222-8558 神奈川県横浜市港北区大豆戸町275番地 (275 Mamedacho, Kohoku-ku, Yokohama, Kanagawa 222-8558, Japan)

Amano Corporation is a leading Japanese multinational corporation specializing in time management systems, parking solutions, environmental equipment, and cleaning robot solutions. Founded in 1931, the company has grown into a global enterprise with over 5,500 employees and 32 consolidated subsidiaries worldwide.

---

## 1. Company History and Founding Date

### Founding and Early Years
- **Founded:** November 3, 1931 (昭和6年) by Shuichi Amano
- **Original Name:** 天野製作所 (Amano Manufacturing)
- **Purpose:** Manufacturing Japan's first time recorders (タイムレコーダー)
- **Location:** Originally established in Minami-Rokugo, Ota-ku, Tokyo

### Key Milestones
- **1939:** Reorganized as 株式会社天野製作所 (Amano Manufacturing Co., Ltd.)
- **1942:** Headquarters moved to Yokohama (Yokohama Factory)
- **1945:** Established "横浜機器株式会社" and resumed time recorder manufacturing (November 22, 1945)
- **1951:** Entered environmental business through partnership with US IVC Company for industrial cleaners
- **1956:** Changed name to 天野特殊機械株式会社 (Amano Special Machinery Co., Ltd.)
- **1961:** Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange Second Section
- **1966:** Renamed to current **アマノ株式会社** (Amano Corporation)
- **1967:** Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange First Section; Started parking business with "Parking Master"
- **2022:** Transitioned to Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market

### International Expansion
- **1964:** Established first US subsidiary "AMANO TIME SYSTEMS, INC." in New York
- **1972:** Established European subsidiary "AMANO EUROPE N.V." in Belgium
- **1994:** Opened offices in Shanghai and Seoul
- **Present:** Operates 25 overseas subsidiaries across North America, Europe, and Asia

## 5. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Philosophy System (基本理念体系)

Amano's corporate philosophy is built on the unchanging founding spirit expressed through three characters: **鈍・根・運 (DON・KON・UN)**

#### 社訓 (Company Motto) - The Three Principles

**1. 鈍 (DON) - Righteousness**
> "鈍とは、正しい行為をいいます。愚鈍の鈍ではなく正直、明朗、人格尊重、人の和、奉仕の気持といった行為にも通じます。"

**Translation:** "DON means righteous conduct. Not the 'dullness' of foolishness, but rather正直 (integrity), 明朗 (cheerfulness/candor), 人格尊重 (respect for human dignity), 人の和 (harmony among people), and 奉仕の気持 (spirit of service)."

**2. 根 (KON) - Perseverance**
> "根とは、たゆみない努力をいいます。仕事に対して愛情を持つとか、研究向上心を持つということにも通じます。"

**Translation:** "KON means tireless effort. It relates to having love for one's work and maintaining a spirit of research and improvement."

**3. 運 (UN) - Fortune**
> "運とは、恵まれる幸運をいいます。『鈍』『根』をよく実行した時に、『運』は開け、ゆたかな心、ゆたかな生活がみのります。『運』は寝て待てということではありません。"

**Translation:** "UN means blessed fortune. When 'DON' and 'KON' are well practiced, 'UN' opens up, and a rich heart and abundant life come to fruition. Fortune does not come to those who just sleep and wait."

### 行動規範 (Code of Conduct) - 12 Principles

1. **正直に、謙虚に生きる** - *Be honest and modest*
2. **基本に忠実、基本の徹底** - *"True to the Basics"*
3. **思いやりと感謝で人と社会に尽くす** - *Serve people and society with consideration and gratitude*
4. **時代の変化に心を開く** - *Be open to change*
5. **笑顔で挨拶、顔見て会話** - *Be pleasant and communicate*
6. **広く世界に目を向ける** - *Look out to the entire world*
7. **地道な努力を惜しまない** - *Be willing to put in the effort*
8. **お客様とともに成長する** - *Grow with our customers*
9. **有言実行、最後までやり抜く** - *Commit to and deliver on our goals*
10. **常に現場を見る** - *Always observe the worksite*
11. **心身ともに健康ありき** - *Engage in active and healthy lifestyles*
12. **一人ひとりがアマノブランド** - *Be a representative of the Amano Brand*

### Brand Statement

**"Time & Airの未来へ" (To the Future of Time & Air)**

> "時間を見える化し、安全で快適な社会の構築を。空気のチカラで、心地よい地球環境の創造を。アマノはこれからも、未来を良くする挑戦を続け、サステナブルな社会の実現に貢献していきます。"

**Translation:** "Visualize time and build a safe and comfortable society. Create a pleasant global environment through the power of air. Amano will continue to take on challenges to improve the future and contribute to realizing a sustainable society."

## 7. Conclusion

アマノ株式会社 (Amano Corporation) represents a remarkable example of Japanese industrial success. From its founding in 1931 as Japan's first time recorder manufacturer, the company has evolved into a diversified technology conglomerate with global reach.

**Key Strengths:**
- **Historical Legacy:** 95 years of continuous operation with founding values intact
- **Market Dominance:** Leader in time management systems in Japan
- **Innovation Culture:** Continuous adaptation from mechanical devices to AI-powered robotics
- **Global Footprint:** 25 overseas subsidiaries serving international markets
- **Balanced Portfolio:** Four major business segments reducing market risk
- **Strong Philosophy:** Clear corporate values guiding business decisions
- **Financial Strength:** Prime market listing with solid financials

**Strategic Focus Areas:**
- Digital transformation through cloud services and AI
- International expansion, particularly in Southeast Asia
- Automation solutions addressing labor shortages
- Environmental and sustainability solutions
- Integrated facility management services

The company's unique positioning at the intersection of **"Time & Air"** (時間と空気) - managing both time through attendance/parking systems and air through environmental/cleaning solutions - creates a distinctive market position. Combined with its strong corporate philosophy emphasizing righteousness, perseverance, and fortune, Amano is well-positioned for continued growth in its second century of operation.
