# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社リライフ・ジャパン (RLJ co.,ltd)

## Executive Summary

**株式会社リライフ・ジャパン** (RLJ co.,ltd) is a Japanese IT company established in 2000, specializing in financial system development, system consulting, and web application development. Headquartered in Shinagawa, Tokyo, the company has been providing high-level system development services primarily to the securities and financial industry for over 20 years. Since 2018, RLJ has been part of the CSS Group (シー・エス・エスグループ), leveraging the group's combined capabilities to expand its services.
