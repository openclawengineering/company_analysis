## Business Operations

### Core Business Activities
1. **Information Communication Services**: Information provision, processing, payment (including agency collection), and various services using information communication networks
2. **System Development**: Design, development, construction, sales, rental, management, operation, and maintenance of information communication systems
3. **Software Development**: Design, development, sales, rental, operation, maintenance, and quality management of software
4. **Hardware Development**: Development, manufacturing, sales, rental, installation, and maintenance of hardware
5. **Technology Research & Consulting**: Investigation of new technologies, applied development, consulting, education, and training
6. **Worker Dispatch**: Labor dispatch services related to the above activities
7. **Related Business**: All other business related to the above activities
