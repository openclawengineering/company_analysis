## Business Description

Sky株式会社 (SKY Perfect JSAT Corporation) is Japan's largest satellite communications operator and a leading multi-channel pay-TV broadcasting company. Formed through the merger of SKY Perfect Communications and JSAT Corporation, the company operates as an integrated space and communications enterprise.

### Core Business Areas

1. **Satellite Communications**
   - Operates the largest fleet of communication satellites in the Asia-Pacific region
   - Provides satellite transponder services for broadcasting, telecommunications, and data communications
   - Maritime and aeronautical connectivity solutions

2. **Pay-TV Broadcasting (SKY PerfecTV!)**
   - Japan's premier satellite broadcasting platform
   - Sports, music, anime, movies, drama programming
   - Multi-device viewing (TV, smartphone, tablet)
   - Content categories: Sports & live music, anime, drama, movies

3. **Space Business**
   - Satellite manufacturing and operations
   - Space-related technology development
   - Ground station services
