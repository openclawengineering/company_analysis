# Fabbi Japan株式会社 (Fabbi Japan)

## Company Overview

Fabbi Japan株式会社 is a software development company based in Tokyo, Japan, specializing in offshore development services and technology solutions. Founded in 2019, the company operates as part of Fabbi Holdings, leveraging Vietnamese technical expertise to serve the Japanese market. Fabbi Japan offers comprehensive software development, blockchain solutions, and digital transformation services to clients across various industries including education, finance, healthcare, and e-commerce. The company was selected as one of Japan's Best Venture 100 in 2023, being the only Vietnamese company to receive this prestigious recognition.

**Website:** [https://fabbiholdings.com/ja/](https://fabbiholdings.com/ja/)

- **2019/02** - 株式会社Fabbi Japan established in Tokyo, Japan as part of Fabbi Holdings
