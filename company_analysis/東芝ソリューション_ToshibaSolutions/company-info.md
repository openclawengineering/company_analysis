## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
Toshiba Solutions Corporation (東芝ソリューション株式会社) was a major Japanese IT services and solutions provider established in 2003 through the merger of Toshiba's e-Solutions Company and Toshiba IT Solutions Corporation. In July 2017, the company underwent a significant reorganization, merging with Toshiba's Industrial ICT Solutions Company to become **Toshiba Digital Solutions Corporation** (東芝デジタルソリューションズ株式会社).

### Key Strengths
1. **Deep Industry Expertise** - Leverages Toshiba Group's extensive knowledge across energy, infrastructure, manufacturing, and other sectors
2. **Advanced Technology Portfolio** - IoT, AI, quantum technology, blockchain, and XR solutions
3. **Comprehensive Service Offering** - From consulting to system integration to managed services
4. **Strong Manufacturing Focus** - Meister Series for smart manufacturing and supply chain management
5. **National Presence** - Offices across all major regions of Japan

### Strategic Focus
The company is positioned as Toshiba Group's digital solutions arm, driving:
- Digital transformation (DX) for enterprises
- Smart manufacturing solutions
- Quantum technology commercialization
- Sustainability and SDGs contribution

### Current Status
- **Capital:** 23.5 billion JPY
- **Employees:** ~3,000 (standalone), ~7,000 (consolidated group)
- **Leadership:** Shimada Taro (President & CEO)
- **Ownership:** 100% Toshiba Corporation subsidiary
