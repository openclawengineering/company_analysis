# 株式会社シンクタンクオブジャパン (Think Tank of Japan Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社シンクタンクオブジャパン (Think Tank of Japan Co., Ltd.) is a technology company aiming to become "Japan's brain trust" (日本の頭脳集団). Established in 1985, they celebrated their 40th anniversary in 2023.

## Business Description

### Network Construction Business
With the latest ICT technology, they handle everything in one package and assist in building networks with excellent operability and reliability. Based on extensive experience, they provide comprehensive full support from system planning and proposal to design, construction (introduction, evaluation testing, on-site adjustment), and maintenance.

Services include:
- Planning and Design
- Conformance
- Maintenance and Operation

### Product Development Business
Proposing optimal solutions from experienced technical developers who have accumulated extensive development experience and achievements. Particularly highly evaluated in multi-vendor environment application development, platform development, embedded and control system development.

Services include:
- IT Application Development
- Embedded Software Development
- LSI Development

### Evaluation Business
With digital technology and network technology advancement, systems are becoming more multifunctional and complex, while requiring high reliability. Experienced technical personnel respond to these needs with high-level skills.

Services include:
- Verification Evaluation Service
- Third-Party Evaluation
- Conformance Evaluation
