# あさかわシステムズ株式会社 (Asakawa Systems Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

あさかわシステムズ株式会社 (Asakawa Systems Co., Ltd.) is a Kanagawa-based software company specializing in back-office business solutions for the construction and civil engineering industries.
