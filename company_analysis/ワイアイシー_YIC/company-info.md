## 9. Summary

株式会社ビッツ (BITS CO., LTD.) is a well-established Japanese software development company founded in 1971, celebrating over 50 years of continuous operation. Headquartered in Tokyo's Shinagawa district with a significant presence in Yokohama, the company has evolved from minicomputer software development to become a comprehensive IT solutions provider.

The company's core competencies include:
1. **Contract Software Development** - Business systems, information processing
2. **Embedded Software** - Automotive, smartphone, and industrial applications
3. **Proprietary Products** - Wireless sensing systems (G-Link/WSDA), accessibility devices (eye棒), and mobility solutions (cyve)

With capital of 100 million JPY and approximately 279 employees, BITS serves diverse industries including automotive, aerospace, telecommunications, and public infrastructure. The company maintains a strong focus on social contribution through technology, developing systems that support critical infrastructure and improve quality of life.

BITS is recognized within the Japanese embedded systems industry as a member of JASA and regularly participates in industry exhibitions and technology showcases. Their 50+ years of experience, combined with continuous innovation in IoT and embedded systems, positions them as a reliable partner for organizations seeking specialized software development services.
