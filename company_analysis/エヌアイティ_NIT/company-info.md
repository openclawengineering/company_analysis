## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
**株式会社エヌアイティ (NTT IT Corporation)** was a prominent NTT Group company specializing in ICT (Information and Communication Technology) solutions. Founded in 1987 as NTT Intelligent Technology, the company established itself as a leader in multimedia and intelligent processing technologies.

### Key Strengths
- **Research Heritage**: One of the "研究所系4社" (4 research-related companies) with strong ties to NTT Research Laboratories
- **Diverse Portfolio**: Healthcare systems, voice/image processing, web conferencing, remote access solutions
- **Innovation Focus**: Pioneered technologies in AI, voice recognition, and media processing

### Current Status
The company merged with NTTソフトウェア on April 1, 2017, to form **NTTテクノクロス株式会社 (NTT TechnoCross Corporation)**. The Yokohama office at 横浜アイマークプレイス continues to operate as a major development center for the merged entity.

### Legacy Products
Many products developed by NTT-IT continue to be offered by NTTテクノクロス, including:
- MagicConnect (remote access VPN)
- MeetingPlaza (web conferencing)
- Healthcare management systems
- Voice and image processing solutions

### Industry Impact
NTT-IT played a significant role in Japan's ICT development, particularly in:
- Telemedicine and healthcare IT
- Distance learning and education technology
- Enterprise collaboration solutions
- Remote work infrastructure (predating the COVID-19 pandemic)
