# アドバンスドプランニング株式会社 (Advanced Planning Corporation)

## Company Overview

アドバンスドプランニング株式会社 (Advanced Planning Corporation) is a specialized software development company founded in 1994, headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. With over 30 years of experience, the company has established itself as a leading developer of control systems, network systems, video-related systems, and medical systems.

The company operates with the philosophy of being "strong rather than big" (大よりも強), focusing on providing reliable, high-quality software with the highest priority on safety and security. They serve a diverse range of industries including automotive control systems, medical equipment, and various industrial applications.

**Website:** https://www.apc-inet.co.jp

## History

- **1994/01** - Founded with 3 members in Yokohama, Aoba-ku
- **2006/04** - Relocated to Kita-ku, Shin-Yokohama for business expansion
- **2007/12** - Acquired Environmental Management System Eco-Stage 1 certification
- **2009/02** - Obtained ISMS (ISO/IEC 27001:2005) certification
- **2010/09** - Moved to current address in Shin-Yokohama for further business expansion
- **2011/03** - Changed fiscal year from July to March
- **2012/02** - Certified as Yokohama-type Regional Contribution Enterprise (highest level)
- **2015/02** - Transitioned to ISMS (ISO/IEC 27001:2013)
- **2017/03** - Obtained QMS (ISO9001:2015) certification
- **2018/02** - Expanded office space (moved from 8F to 2F within same building)
- **2023/05** - Further expansion due to business growth
- **2025/01** - Transitioned to ISMS (ISO/IEC 27001:2022)

## Philosophy

The company operates with the philosophy of being "strong rather than big" (大よりも強), prioritizing:
- **Safety and Security** - Always putting customer safety first
- **Quality Excellence** - Maintaining high standards through ISO 9001 certification
- **Information Security** - Strong commitment to protecting customer data through ISO 27001 certification
- **Environmental Responsibility** - Active participation in environmental initiatives
- **Regional Contribution** - Contributing to local economic and social development
