# 株式会社日本コンサルタントグループ (Nippon Consultants Group Inc.)

## Company Overview

Nippon Consultants Group Inc. (abbreviated "NICCON") is a comprehensive management consulting company founded in 1956. Headquartered in Shinjuku, Tokyo with offices in seven major cities across Japan (Sapporo, Sendai, Niigata, Tokyo, Nagoya, Osaka, Fukuoka), the company provides management consulting, corporate diagnosis, human resource development, market research, regional development, and publishing services. With 120 employees and the motto "More practical, more applied," NICCON serves diverse industries including construction, IT/telecommunications, retail, food/service, hotels, medical/welfare, and government agencies.

**Website:** https://www.niccon.co.jp/
