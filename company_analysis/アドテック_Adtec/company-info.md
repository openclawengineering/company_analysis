# 株式会社アドテック (Adtec Corporation)

## Company Overview

株式会社アドテック is a Japanese solution company with the mission "Creating new value through solutions." Founded in 1984, Adtec develops, manufactures, and sells a wide range of ICT products and services. The company specializes in industrial/embedded computing, IoT/M2M services, custom power supply development, and provides memory products for various industries including industrial, commercial, and general-purpose use.

**Website:** https://www.adtec.co.jp/
