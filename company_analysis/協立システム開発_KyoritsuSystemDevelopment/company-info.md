## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Motto (社訓)
> **「社は人なり、人は和なり」**
> *"A company is its people, and people are harmony"*

### Philosophy

Since its founding in 1979, Kyoritsu System Development has consistently upheld this corporate philosophy. As an independent company, it has accumulated system development know-how through relationships with diverse clients.

### Core Strengths
- **45+ years** of system development expertise
- **Human Capital:** Employees ("人財" - human assets) are considered the company's greatest treasure
- **Independence:** As an independent company, able to work with various clients flexibly
- **Trust:** Built on long-standing relationships with clients who describe KSD as "a company you can trust with peace of mind"

### Specialized Expertise
- **40+ years:** Production management systems for manufacturing, public sector infrastructure systems
- **25+ years:** Financial systems and medical system development/implementation support
- **Growing focus:** Embedded systems including automotive applications

### President's Message (Excerpt)
> "To our customers, the trust relationship we have built is a precious asset. We will continue to focus on developing our human resources, providing better systems and services to earn even greater trust."

— *Toshiro Takahashi, President & CEO*

## 9. Summary

**協立システム開発株式会社 (Kyoritsu System Development Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese IT services company founded in 1979 and headquartered in Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture. With over 45 years of experience in system development, the company has built a reputation as a trusted partner for enterprise software solutions.

### Key Highlights:

- **Established Legacy:** Operating continuously since 1979, demonstrating stability and reliability in the Japanese IT market
- **Strong Client Base:** Partners with major Japanese IT companies including Fujitsu, NTT Data group companies, and NEC
- **Diverse Portfolio:** Serves manufacturing, public sector, healthcare, financial, and telecommunications industries
- **Philosophy-Driven:** Corporate motto "社は人なり、人は和なり" emphasizes the importance of people and harmony
- **Growing Organization:** 57 employees with approximately ¥564 million in annual revenue
- **Strategic Positioning:** Became a wholly-owned subsidiary of Kyoritsu Holdings in May 2025, positioning for potential group synergies

The company's strength lies in its deep domain expertise—particularly in production management and public sector systems—combined with a culture that values human capital as its greatest asset. As part of the Kyoritsu Holdings group, the company is positioned for continued growth while maintaining its independent, client-focused approach to system development.
