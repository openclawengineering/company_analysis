# Company Analysis Report: 新明和ソフトテクノロジ株式会社 (ShinMaywa Soft Technologies, Ltd.)

## Executive Summary

**新明和ソフトテクノロジ株式会社** (ShinMaywa Soft Technologies, Ltd.) is the only IT company within the ShinMaywa Group, a major manufacturing group. Founded in 1986 as a subsidiary of ShinMaywa Industries, the company has nearly four decades of experience providing comprehensive digital solutions including production management, sales management, core system development, CAD-related systems, and infrastructure construction and operation. The company has expanded its capabilities by incorporating advanced technologies such as IoT and AI to support factory floor digitization while serving both ShinMaywa Group companies and external clients.
