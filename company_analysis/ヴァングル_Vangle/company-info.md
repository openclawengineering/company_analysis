## 4. Products and Services

### Core Philosophy
> "Making IT a true weapon for companies"

Vangle focuses on three key principles:
1. **全体最適 (Total Optimization)** - Medium to long-term holistic value perspective
2. **運用起点 (Operations-First)** - Design with operations in mind from the start
3. **体制設計 (Organizational Design)** - Balance quality with productivity

### Service Categories

#### A. IT Consulting (ITコンサルティング)
- Current status analysis and issue identification
- Strategic planning and roadmap development
- Offshore utilization support
- Project promotion support

#### B. System Development (システム開発)
- New development and reconstruction of business systems
- **Microservices implementation** using proprietary platform Axonium
- Offshore system development leveraging Vangle Tek subsidiary
- Architecture design, implementation, testing, and migration

#### C. System Operations & Infrastructure (システム運用・インフラ構築)
- **System Operations Service** - ITIL-based operations design, construction, and support
- **Cloud Construction Service** - AWS/GCP/Azure infrastructure including government cloud requirements
- **Cloud Migration Service** - Safe migration with comprehensive planning and data migration strategies
- **Kubernetes Construction Service** - Managed Kubernetes (EKS/GKE/AKS/OKE) cluster design, monitoring, and operations
- **BigData Environment Construction** - NoSQL design, Spark operations, full-text search infrastructure

#### D. Offshore Support (オフショアサポート)
- Vendor selection and project setup assistance
- Management improvement for QCD (Quality, Cost, Delivery) optimization
- Offshore marketing support for Japanese market entry

### Proprietary Product: Axonium (アクソニウム)

**Axonium** is Vangle's flagship development platform designed to lower barriers to microservices adoption.

#### Key Features:
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Skip Infrastructure Building** | Pre-built foundation allows focus on business logic |
| **Operations-Safe Design** | Pre-emptive solutions for operational scenarios prevent unexpected issues |
| **Reduced Coordination Costs** | Common functions reduce inter-team adjustments |

#### Technical Components:
- **Saga Orchestration** via Temporal for distributed transaction management
- **Event-Driven Architecture** using Debezium and Kafka for crash-resilient event handling
- **Multi-Tenant Support** with Keycloak for authentication/authorization
- **Kubernetes-Ready** with built-in operational settings

#### Target Use Cases:
- Frequently changing features (customer management, campaigns)
- Systems with uneven load distribution (search, recommendations, reporting)
- Complex integrated systems (core systems, payment, logistics)

## Summary

**ヴァングル株式会社 (Vangle Co., Ltd.)** is a Yokohama-based IT services company founded in 2018 with a mission to address Japan's IT talent shortage through optimized, operations-focused solutions. The company differentiates itself through:

1. **Holistic "Total Optimization" approach** - Medium to long-term value perspective
2. **Proprietary Axonium platform** - Lowering barriers to microservices adoption
3. **Vietnamese subsidiary (Vangle Tek)** - Enabling cost-effective offshore development with Japanese language support
4. **End-to-end service delivery** - From consulting through development to operations

The company targets both enterprise clients seeking legacy modernization and SaaS companies requiring scalable multi-tenant architectures, with a particular focus on making IT "a true weapon" for Japanese businesses facing talent constraints.
