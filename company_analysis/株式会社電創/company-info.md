# 株式会社電創 (Densou Inc.)

## Company Overview

Densou Inc. is an IT services company founded in May 1990, headquartered in Saiwai-ku, Kawasaki, Kanagawa. With approximately 30 employees, capital of 30 million JPY, and average age of 38, the company provides system development, server/network infrastructure construction, IT diagnostics, digital transformation services, and PC/smartphone/programming courses. The company has a subsidiary, Core Concept Technology Inc.
