## 6. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Philosophy (企業理念)

**"People-First Company" (人を大切にする企業)**

The company was founded in 1985 with "C&C" (Computer & Communication) as its banner. The guiding principle has always been: **"Value people, and as employees grow, the company grows."**

Key Philosophy Elements:
1. **People-Centric Growth:** Employee growth drives company expansion
2. **Fair Evaluation:** Performance-based personnel evaluation system that rewards motivation and effort
3. **Warm Atmosphere:** Active club activities, events, and a culture filled with smiles

### President's Message

> "To be close to people requires both 'strength' and 'kindness.' Without 'strength,' it becomes dependence; 'closeness' without 'kindness' cannot exist."
>
> "In our industry, 'strength' means 'technology.' We provide technology 'for customers' when 'customers need it.'"

**Vision for the Future:**
- Embrace AI and RPA evolution rather than resist it
- Develop flexibility and pattern-recognition capabilities
- Create new services in system operations
- Contribute to society through better solutions

### Health Management Declaration (健康経営宣言)

The company declared health management as a key management priority:

**Key Initiatives:**
1. **100% Health Checkup Rate** (2021 actual: 100%)
2. **Mental Healthcare** - 80%+ stress check participation; 24/7 consultation hotline
3. **Lifestyle Disease Prevention** - Educational activities and industrial physician guidance
4. **Anti-Smoking** - Outdoor smoking areas; free nicotine patch provision
5. **Flu Vaccination** - Full cost subsidy for employees
6. **Sleep Disorder Support** - Industrial physician consultation

## 9. Summary

**関越ソフトウェア株式会社 (Kanetsu Software Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese IT services company founded in 1985, celebrating its 40th anniversary in 2025. Headquartered in Kawasaki, Kanagawa with a branch in Niigata, the company employs over 500 professionals and provides comprehensive IT services including:

- **System Operations Support:** 24/365 data center and network operations
- **Software Development:** Application design, development, and maintenance
- **Engineer Dispatch:** Regular employment-based technical staffing

The company serves major Japanese corporations across 10+ industries, including major IT vendors (Fujitsu, Hitachi, NEC, NTT), financial institutions (Sumitomo Mitsui Bank, Mitsubishi UFJ), automotive (Toyota), and transportation (JR).

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **40-year track record** in the IT industry
2. **People-first culture** with emphasis on employee growth
3. **Health Management Declaration** - certified healthy workplace
4. **Stable employment model** - regular employees, not contractors
5. **Dual-location operations** - Kawasaki HQ and Niigata near-shore development
6. **Comprehensive certifications** - Privacy Mark, ISMS, JISA member

The company's philosophy centers on the belief that employee growth drives corporate growth, with fair performance-based evaluation systems. Recent initiatives include embracing AI/RPA transformation and developing flexible, adaptive capabilities for the changing IT landscape.
