# And One Software Engineering株式会社 (And One Software)

## Company Overview

And One Software Engineering株式会社 is a software development company based in Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture, specializing in sports scoring systems, public facility guidance displays, remote monitoring control, and AI systems. Established in 2019, the company has grown to become a provider of innovative solutions for various industries including sports, public facilities, and infrastructure monitoring.

The company is known for its ArenaScoreDisplaySystem, which is used in professional basketball, handball, and volleyball games, as well as in large gymnasiums for national sports events. They also provide services for public sports betting facilities, various public and private facility guidance displays, and AI-based image analysis and prediction systems.

## Services/Products

### Sports Scoring Systems
- **ArenaScoreDisplaySystem** - Indoor sports scoring system for basketball, handball, volleyball, table tennis, martial arts, futsal. Used in professional games and national events.

### Public Sports Betting Services
- **Audio guidance** - For horse racing, keirin, boat racing, auto racing venues
- **Information displays** - Odds/race results, special seat sales, entrance gates, point management, membership management, event coordination
- **Financial calculations** - Prize money calculations for athletes, salary calculations for staff

### Public Facility Guidance
- **Event guidance** - For public and private facilities
- **Signage control** - Airport flight schedules, baseball/soccer stadium scoreboards
- **Institutional guidance** - Hospitals, pharmacies, libraries, museums, wedding halls, funeral halls

### Remote Monitoring Control
- **Infrastructure monitoring** - Remote control of dams and water gates
- **Environmental monitoring** - River water levels, tidal levels, weather, temperature, rainfall, wind, earthquake intensity
- **Data management** - Central collection, monitoring, analysis, display, and report output of various measurement data

### AI Systems
- **AI Image Analysis** - Extracts and analyzes features from camera/sensor images to data information about color, shape, length, area, speed
- **AI Prediction** - Deep learning-based predictions using accumulated data
- **AIDream** - AI-powered boat race prediction system using 5 years of historical data
