## 3. Company History & Milestones

### Predecessor Era (1941-1998)
- **1941年7月 (July 1941):** Founded as ブラザーミシン販売株式会社 (Brother Sewing Machine Sales Co., Ltd.) - established as the domestic sales organization for Brother Industries products
- **1983年2月 (February 1983):** Changed company name to ブラザー販売株式会社 (Brother Sales, Ltd.)
- **1983年11月 (November 1983):** Listed on Nagoya Stock Exchange, Second Section (Securities Code: 8162)

### Current Entity (1998-Present)
- **1998年10月12日 (October 12, 1998):** Current entity established with initial capital of 30 billion yen
- **1999年4月1日 (April 1, 1999):** Became 100% subsidiary of Brother Industries, Ltd.
- **2001年6月 (June 2001):** Capital increased to 120 billion yen (shareholder allocation)
- **2001年12月 (December 2001):** Capital increased to 135 billion yen (shareholder allocation)
- **2003年4月 (April 2003):** Integrated industrial equipment sales from Brother Industries
- **2005年1月 (January 2005):** Capital reduced to 35 billion yen (covering capital deficit)
- **2005年3月 (March 2005):** Withdrew from cultural center business
- **2006年7月 (July 2006):** Headquarters relocated to current address in Nagoya
- **2007年1月 (January 2007):** Transferred DSM division to Yamano Group
- **2008年11月 (November 2008):** Obtained ISO14001 certification (integrated certification with Brother Industries)
- **2009年4月 (April 2009):** Transferred industrial equipment and industrial sewing machine businesses to Brother Industries
- **2014年4月 (April 2014):** Transferred laser printer OEM business to Brother Industries
- **2018年10月 (October 2018):** Integrated domestic sales of Brother Industries' garment printer business

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
Brother Sales, Ltd. (株式会社ブラザー販売) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Brother Industries, Ltd., serving as the primary domestic sales and marketing arm for Brother products in Japan. Established in its current form in 1998, the company traces its roots back to 1941 as the original sales organization for Brother sewing machines.

### Core Strengths
1. **Established Market Position:** Over 80 years of experience in the Japanese market with deep understanding of local customer needs
2. **Comprehensive Product Portfolio:** Wide range from consumer sewing machines to enterprise printing solutions
3. **Customer-Centric Culture:** "At your side." philosophy drives all business activities and customer interactions
4. **Strong Parent Company:** Backed by Brother Industries' global R&D capabilities and manufacturing excellence
5. **Extensive Network:** Nationwide presence with regional offices covering all major Japanese markets
6. **B2B Expertise:** Proven track record in serving corporations, government agencies, and system integrators

### Business Model
- **Primary Focus:** B2B sales and marketing of Brother products
- **Revenue Streams:** Product sales, after-sales services, consumables, and enterprise solutions
- **Channels:** Direct sales to enterprises, distribution through partners and SIers, retail partnerships for consumer products
- **Value Addition:** Custom solutions, technical support, and integration services

### Strategic Position
Brother Sales occupies a critical position in the Brother Group's value chain, serving as the interface between Brother's manufacturing capabilities and Japanese customers. The company's deep market knowledge and customer relationships enable Brother Industries to maintain strong market presence in Japan while focusing on global expansion.

### Recent Developments
- Expansion into digital marketing and demand generation
- Partnerships with leading IT companies for integrated solutions
- Focus on AI and cloud-based services
- Continued investment in B2B market development
