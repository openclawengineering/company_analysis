## 3. Company History & Milestones

### 1980s - Foundation
- **May 1983:** Founded as "Shirasaka Sangyo Co., Ltd." and opened "Soft Create" PC shop in Shibuya (Miyamasuzaka)
- **April 1985:** Started system integration (SI) services with custom software development
- **November 1985:** Moved registered headquarters from Yokohama to Shibuya, Tokyo
- **December 1985:** Changed company name from "Shirasaka Sangyo Co., Ltd." to "Soft Create Co., Ltd."

### 1990s - Business Evolution
- **April 1993:** Launched network construction and maintenance services
- **October 1998:** Closed PC retail shop due to market competition from large retailers
- **January 1999:** Launched e-commerce site "Tokka COM"
- **October 1999:** Started selling EC site construction package "ec-shop" (later renamed "ecbeing")

### 2000s - Expansion & Listing
- **March 2000:** Established data center in Ariake, Tokyo and started hosting services
- **April 2005:** Listed on Osaka Securities Exchange "Hercules" market
- **September 2005:** Obtained ISMS (Information Security Management System) certification
- **November 2005:** Launched unauthorized PC detection/blocking system "L2Blocker"
- **December 2008:** Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange Second Section

### 2010s - Growth & Restructuring
- **March 2011:** Listed on Tokyo Stock Exchange First Section
- **September 2012:** Launched cloud service "SCCloud"
- **October 2012:** Transitioned to holding company structure; changed to "Soft Create Holdings Corp." and transferred SI business to newly established "Soft Create Co., Ltd."
- **May 2013:** Celebrated 30th anniversary; formed capital alliance with Nihon Unisys

### 2020s - Digital Transformation
- **February 2021:** Opened "Sofuri 365 Club" community for Microsoft 365/Azure users
- **April 2022:** Launched "Fire Logic" network access control platform
- **November 2022:** Released "Survey Eyes" cloud service for PC/software asset management
- **March 2023:** Released "SCCloud 365" all-in-one Microsoft 365 service
- **February 2024:** Released "Safe AI Gateway" enterprise generative AI service
- **May 2024:** Released "Safe AI Bot" generative AI chatbot
- **February 2026:** Certified as "Great Place to Work" (働きがいのある会社)

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview:
株式会社ソフトクリエイト (Soft Create Co., Ltd.) is a comprehensive IT solutions provider and system integrator with over 40 years of history. Originally founded as a PC retail shop in Shibuya in 1983, the company has evolved into a major player in Japan's IT services industry, particularly known for its cloud solutions, security services, and e-commerce platform development.

### Key Strengths:
1. **Long-term Stability:** 40+ consecutive years of profitable, debt-free operations
2. **Technical Expertise:** Strong software development capabilities with Microsoft partnership and 7 consecutive Partner Awards
3. **Comprehensive Solutions:** Full-stack IT services from consulting to operation
4. **Market Leadership:** #1 market share in Japan's EC site construction market for 17 consecutive years
5. **Workplace Excellence:** Recently certified as "Great Place to Work" with excellent work-life balance
6. **Innovation Focus:** Proactive adoption of AI, cloud, and security technologies
7. **Customer Base:** Trusted by over 10,000 companies across diverse industries

### Business Model:
- System integration and cloud services (core business)
- Subscription-based cloud platform services (SCCloud series)
- AI and security solutions (growth areas)
- IT outsourcing and support services (SES)
- Software product sales (ecbeing, workflow systems)

### Strategic Position:
As a subsidiary of Soft Create Holdings (TSE Prime Market listed), the company combines the agility of a focused operating company with the financial strength and strategic resources of a listed holding company. The transition to a holding company structure in 2012 allowed for specialized focus on SI business while the EC business operates through sister company ecbeing inc.

### Future Outlook:
The company is actively expanding in:
- Enterprise AI solutions (Safe AI Gateway, Safe AI Bot)
- Cloud-native services (SCCloud 365, EaseBase)
- Security operations and monitoring
- Microsoft ecosystem integration (Copilot, Azure AI)
- DX support for mid-sized and large enterprises

With strong financial fundamentals, excellent workplace culture, and strategic focus on high-growth areas like AI and cloud, Soft Create is well-positioned for continued growth in Japan's competitive IT services market.

### Notable Differentiators:
- Rare combination of stability (40+ years profitable) and innovation (early AI/cloud adoption)
- Strong Microsoft partnership with co-sell status
- Balanced approach to growth with conservative financial management
- Genuine commitment to employee well-being (Great Place to Work certified)
- Comprehensive end-to-end service capability
- Active community building through owned media and events
