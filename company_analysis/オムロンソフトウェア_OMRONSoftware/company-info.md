## 9. Summary

OMRON Software Co., Ltd. (株式会社オムロンソフトウェア) was a Japanese software development company founded in 1976 with approximately 44 years of operation. As of 2019, the company had 428 employees and capital of 360 million yen, with annual revenue of 14.29 billion yen.

The company specialized in developing software solutions for social infrastructure, including railway systems, financial ATMs, and public infrastructure. Its business philosophy centered on "Creating Social Needs" rather than simply responding to them. The company had expertise in four core areas: Embedded systems, Control systems, Network/Communications, and System Integration, with a strong emphasis on Quality.

OMRON Software operated seven main business areas: FA (Factory Automation) Solutions, Mobile Solutions, Railway Solutions, EFTS (Electronic Funds Transfer) Solutions, Data Solutions, Health Solutions, and Financial Solutions. Key technologies included proprietary language processing (Wnn input method), image processing, and smartphone solutions.

The company maintained offices in Kyoto, Osaka, Tokyo, Shiga, and Aichi, with a subsidiary in Shanghai. It was part of the OMRON Group, leveraging the group's manufacturing and automation expertise.
