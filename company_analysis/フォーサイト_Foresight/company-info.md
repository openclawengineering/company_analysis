### Corporate Philosophy Name Origin
"FORESIGHT" means **先見性 (foresight), 洞察 (insight), 慎重さ (prudence), 用心 (caution), 見通し (outlook)** in Japanese. The company aims to correctly identify system requirements rather than developing blindly, creating new systems as an SE group that proposes solutions.

### Company Logo Symbolism
- **Outer pink ring:** 技術力 (Technical capability)
- **Inner white:** 提案力 (Proposal capability)
- The two overlapping eggs form an "eye" shape representing "先見性" (foresight)

### Specific System Development Projects

#### 予算管理システム (Budget Management System)
- **Target:** Companies with multiple factory locations
- **Features:** Database synchronization between factory servers, normally operates within each factory, connects to other factory servers during emergencies

#### 売上、在庫管理システム (Sales & Inventory Management System)
- **Target:** National apparel manufacturers
- **Features:** 
  - Barcode and handy terminal integration
  - Real-time inventory tracking
  - HQ purchasing/shipping operations
  - Store product management (daily sales, inventory counts)
- **Network:** Uses NTT Communications Group VPN closed network for secure connectivity

#### 資材業務管理支援システム (Materials Management Support System)
- **Target:** Printing industry
- **Features:** Internal materials management (quotation, ordering, delivery slip issuance, billing)

#### 金属卸売会社向け販売管理システム (Metal Wholesaler Sales Management System)
- **Features:**
  - Sales/purchase information management
  - Payment/receipt tracking
  - Employee sales performance management

## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Philosophy

#### 存在意義 (Purpose of Existence)
『価値』あるものを『創造』し、その成果を社員に還元することが使命と考えます。
*Creating "value" and returning the results to employees is our mission.*

#### 経営姿勢 (Management Approach)
1. 人（顧客）の満足を第一に考えます
2. 人（顧客）から信頼される人（社員）を育てます
3. 人（顧客・社員）との出会いを大切にします
4. 人（社員）が成長できる環境を与え、人とともに成長し続けます

#### 行動規範 (Code of Conduct)
1. 顧客がなにを望んでいるかを『考え』、その要望に応えます
2. 本質が何かを見極め、「これでいいのか？」を『考え』ます
3. 「無理です。できません。」とは言いません。「どうすればできるのか？」を『考え』ます
4. 良好な人間関係を構築します
5. 失敗を恐れません。『勇気』をもって『チャレンジ』します
6. 現状に満足せず、一歩上を目指します

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
株式会社フォーサイト (Foresight Co., Ltd.) is a small IT software development company established in 2000 with 500万円 in capital. The company specializes in custom software development, including budget management, sales/inventory systems, and industry-specific business applications.

### Strengths
1. **Oracle Database Expertise:** Strong focus on database-centered system development
2. **Industry Partnerships:** NTT Communications and Oracle partnerships
3. **Custom Solutions:** Tailored systems for various industries (apparel, printing, metal wholesale)
4. **Technical Foundation:** Experience with both legacy (Visual Basic 6) and modern (.NET) technologies

### Market Position
- Small-scale operation (6 employees as of 2015)
- B2B focused software development
- Regional presence in Yokohama (Kanagawa) with headquarters in Osaka
- Member of Kanagawa Information Service Industry Association

### Recruitment
The company offers both **新卒採用** (new graduate) and **中途採用** (mid-career) positions:
- **Position:** SE・プログラマー (System Engineer / Programmer)
- **Education:** High school graduate minimum
- **Training:** 1-2 month internal training with dedicated instructor
- **Benefits:** Social insurance, full transportation coverage, retirement allowance, company trips, qualification bonuses

### Business Philosophy
The company emphasizes creating value, customer satisfaction, and employee growth through a practical, challenge-embracing culture focused on finding solutions rather than making excuses.
