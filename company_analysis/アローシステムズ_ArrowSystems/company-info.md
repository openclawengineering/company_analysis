## 6. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Core Philosophy
**"企業は人なり" (Kigyou wa Hito Nari)**
Translation: "A company is its people" or "Enterprises are made of people"

### Corporate Values

**1. People-Centric Approach**
- Founded on the belief that "a company is its people"
- Emphasis on nurturing attractive talent and continuous evolution
- Focus on not being bound by single ideas or technologies, constantly pursuing better services

**2. Trust and Relationships**
- Success in SES (System Engineering Services) business depends not only on technology and knowledge, but also on trust and teamwork
- Strong belief in the power of human connections
- Gratitude for the people and relationships that enable success

**3. Employee Welfare & Growth**
- Creating environments where employees can work with peace of mind and grow
- Focus on individual lifestyle and career aspirations
- Belief that employees being themselves and demonstrating their abilities leads to value for customers and the entire company

### Company Culture Characteristics

**Supportive Environment:**
- Actively recruits liberal arts graduates for engineering positions
- Supports those with knowledge but limited experience
- Philosophy: "Don't give up - there's a chance to realize your dreams here"

**Health & Wellness Focus:**
- Certified as a "Health Excellent Enterprise" in Kanagawa
- Active participation in "Kanagawa Health Enterprise Declaration Project"
- Commitment to creating environments where employees can work healthily in both body and mind

**Transparent Management:**
- Regular face-to-face interactions with employees
- Management philosophy of looking each employee in the eye and checking on their physical and mental health
- Open communication about concerns and challenges

**Career Development:**
- Career paths tailored to individual aspirations (System Engineer, Project Manager)
- Support for continuous learning across various programming languages and industry knowledge
- Emphasis on logical thinking, communication skills, and persistence

### Work-Life Balance
- Comprehensive benefits package
- Telework accommodation (70% of clients offer remote work)
- Various leave types including summer, year-end, founding anniversary, and condolence leave
- Support for work-life integration

### Technological Philosophy
- Embracing technological evolution (including generative AI)
- Balance between innovation and timeless human values
- Recognition that even in rapidly evolving technology landscape, "people" remain the unchanging value

## Conclusion

Arrow Systems Co., Ltd. is a well-established Japanese IT services company with a 25-year history (as of 2025), specializing in enterprise system development across manufacturing and financial sectors. The company distinguishes itself through its strong people-first philosophy, commitment to employee welfare, and focus on building long-term trust relationships with both clients and staff. With recent leadership changes and continued growth (502 million yen revenue), Arrow Systems represents a stable, mid-sized Japanese IT firm that balances traditional values with modern technological adaptation.

The company's strength lies not just in technical capabilities spanning mainframes to modern web systems, but in its unwavering belief that human relationships and employee well-being are the foundation of business success - a philosophy that has guided them through two decades of industry evolution.
