# 株式会社シンクロジック (Think Logic Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社シンクロジック (Think Logic Co., Ltd.) is a manufacturing company specializing in online game development based in Yokohama Minato Mirai. The company develops online games and other software with a focus on creating products that both the company and customers can agree upon.

## Business Description

### Online Game Development
With years of development experience and achievements, they propose solutions that meet various needs. They accompany customers from initial development to subsequent operation, aiming for the same goal together.

### Other Development
Not limited to games, they have extensive development achievements in various fields:
- Web content
- Smartphone applications
- Business systems
- Apps and systems with entertainment elements

They can propose development that incorporates entertainment elements, so customers are encouraged to inquire.
