## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
富士通ビー・エス・シー株式会社 (Fujitsu BSC Ltd.) was a prominent Japanese IT company with nearly 60 years of history. Originally established in 1963 as Nissan Lease, the company evolved through multiple name changes before becoming a key member of the Fujitsu Group.

### Core Competencies
- **Software Development Excellence**: Over 55 years of experience spanning from early computing to modern embedded systems
- **Security Products**: FENCE series became one of Japan's leading enterprise security solutions
- **Embedded Systems**: Deep expertise in automotive, medical, and consumer electronics software
- **China Operations**: Early mover in offshore development with Beijing, Shanghai, and Dalian operations since 1992

### Business Impact
Before its absorption into Fujitsu Limited in 2021, the company:
- Employed approximately 1,700+ staff
- Generated ~8 billion yen in annual revenue
- Developed security products protecting countless Japanese enterprises
- Contributed to major government and private sector systems
- Maintained strong positions in embedded software and information security markets

### Legacy
As part of Fujitsu's 2021 reorganization to accelerate digital transformation, Fujitsu BSC's expertise, products, and talent were integrated directly into Fujitsu Limited. The FENCE security product line continues under Fujitsu's security solutions portfolio, and the embedded systems expertise contributes to Fujitsu's Future Mobility Accelerator initiatives.

### Key Dates to Remember
- **1963**: Founded
- **1975**: Joined Fujitsu Group
- **2000**: JASDAQ listing
- **2017**: Became wholly-owned Fujitsu subsidiary
- **2021**: Absorbed into Fujitsu Limited (company ceased to exist as separate entity)
