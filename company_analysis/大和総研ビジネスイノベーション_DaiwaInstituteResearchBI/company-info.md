## 3. Company History & Milestones

### Founding and Early Years
- **2008年（平成20年）:**
  - 大和総研 (Daiwa Institute of Research) was restructured and split into three separate companies:
    - 大和総研ホールディングス (DIR Holdings)
    - 大和総研 (DIR)
    - 大和総研ビジネス・イノベーション (DIRBI) ← **Founded**
  - This restructuring was part of the 大和証券グループ's strategic realignment

### Growth and Expansion
- **2011年（平成23年）:**
  - ファイテックフォース (FaitecForce) was established
  - ファイテックフォース was a subsidiary focused on IT services

- **2013年（平成25年）:**
  - **ファイテックフォース (FaitecForce) merged into DIRBI**
  - This merger expanded DIRBI's capabilities and resources

### Reintegration
- **2021年（令和3年）:**
  - **大和総研ホールディングス、大和総研、大和総研ビジネス・イノベーションの三社を統合**
  - The three companies were merged back into a single entity
  - The unified company name became "株式会社大和総研" (Daiwa Institute of Research Ltd.)
  - **DIRBI ceased to exist as a separate corporate entity**

### Legacy
- DIRBI operated as an independent subsidiary for approximately 13 years (2008-2021)
- Its business operations, employees, and assets were fully integrated into the parent company
- The expertise and capabilities developed by DIRBI continue to exist within the unified 大和総研

## 9. Summary

### Executive Summary
**大和総研ビジネス・イノベーション株式会社 (Daiwa Institute of Research Business Innovation Ltd., DIRBI)** was a Japanese IT services company that operated as a subsidiary of 大和総研 (Daiwa Institute of Research) within the 大和証券グループ (Daiwa Securities Group).

### Key Points
1. **Founded:** 2008 as part of a restructuring that split 大和総研 into three companies
2. **Merged:** 2021 back into parent company 大和総研, ceasing independent operations
3. **Operational Period:** Approximately 13 years (2008-2021)
4. **Location:** Yokohama, Japan
5. **Business:** Software development, package software, information processing services
6. **Status:** **No longer exists as a separate entity** - fully integrated into 株式会社大和総研

### Business Impact
- Provided IT services primarily to financial institutions and corporate clients
- Served both internal group companies and external clients
- Specialized in software development and information processing
- Merged with ファイテックフォース in 2013 to expand capabilities
- All operations successfully integrated back into parent company in 2021

### Current Status
- **DIRBI has been dissolved as a separate corporate entity**
- All employees, assets, and business operations now part of 株式会社大和総研
- Former DIRBI services continue under the parent company's "System" division
- Clients are now served directly by 大和総研
