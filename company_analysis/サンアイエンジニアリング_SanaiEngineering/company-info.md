## 9. Summary

**株式会社サンアイエンジニアリング (Sanai Engineering Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese IT services company headquartered in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Founded in 1978 and incorporated in 1991, the company has built a 45+ year history in system development and IT services.

### Key Strengths

1. **Deep Specialization in Core Business Systems**
   - Expert focus on enterprise mission-critical systems
   - Extensive experience in embedded systems (automotive: car audio, navigation)
   - Comprehensive service portfolio from development to verification to support

2. **Quality & Security Focus**
   - ISO 27001 certified for information security management
   - JSTQB-certified verification engineers for third-party testing
   - Rigorous quality assurance processes

3. **Strong Corporate Philosophy**
   - "Three Loves" philosophy: customer, partner, and employee focus
   - Emphasis on growing together with all stakeholders
   - Specialization strategy as competitive advantage

4. **Employee-Centric Culture**
   - Recent certifications as Health Excellence Company (2024) and Health Management Excellent Corporation (2025)
   - 100% participation rates in health programs
   - Comprehensive work-life balance initiatives
   - No transfer policy, extensive training programs

5. **Sustainable Business Practices**
   - Kawasaki SDGs Partner registration
   - Commitment to sustainable development goals
   - Environmental and social responsibility focus

### Business Model
The company operates as a specialized IT services provider focusing on:
- **System Development:** Custom enterprise applications, web systems, e-commerce, embedded systems
- **Third-Party Verification:** Quality assurance and security testing services
- **Support Services:** System deployment, operation, and office work outsourcing

### Market Position
- Small-medium enterprise with 37 employees
- Unlisted private company
- Strong regional presence in Kanagawa/Kawasaki area
- Member of key industry associations
- Focus on long-term B2B partnerships

### Recent Achievements
- Health Excellence Company certification (2024)
- Health Management Excellent Corporation 2025
- Kawasaki SDGs Partner registration
- Continuous ISO 27001 compliance

The company demonstrates solid fundamentals with a clear specialization strategy, strong employee focus, and commitment to quality and security standards. While maintaining a relatively low public profile, Sanai Engineering has built a reputation for reliability and expertise in core business system development and IT services in the Japanese B2B market.
