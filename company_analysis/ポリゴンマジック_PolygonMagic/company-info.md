## 3. Company History & Milestones

### Founding (1996)
- **April 3, 1996:** Founded as a spin-off from Visual Science Laboratory Co., Ltd. (株式会社ビジュアルサイエンス研究所), alongside companies like Digital Hollywood, Digital scape, and I.M.J.
- **Original Focus:** 3DCG-based computer game development company
- **Founding Philosophy:** "世界中の子どもたちを魔法にかける" (Cast a spell on children around the world using the power of 3DCG/polygons)
- **First Representative Director:** 吉岡賢 (Masaru Yoshioka)

### Key Milestones

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1996 | Founded; released first arcade game "Fighter's Impact" (タイトー) |
| 1999 | Released "Incredible Crisis" (とんでもクライシス!) and "Galerians" for PlayStation |
| 2004 | Developed "Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune" arcade racing game |
| 2006 | Developed "Silent Hill: The Arcade" with Konami |
| **2008** | **鶴谷武親 becomes Representative Director & President (January 1, 2008)** |
| 2008 | BlendMagic team spins off as Matchlock Corporation (now sells "BISHAMON" via Silicon Studio) |
| 2008 | Helped develop "Tsurista" (釣りスタ) fishing game for GREE - deepening mobile social game relationship |
| **2010** | **Became a group company of Future Institute Corporation (フューチャーインスティテュート株式会社)** |
| 2011 | Acquired game information portal Gpara.com Co., Ltd. (株式会社ジーパラドットコム) |
| **2012** | **Established joint venture G-PLA Inc. with GREE Corporation** |
| 2012 | Acquired Bull's Eye Corporation stock from Heiwa Group fund; made it a group company |
| 2014 | Established Space Magic Inc. for restaurant business; opened "Torizono" |
| 2014 | Acquired Hippo Lab Inc. from Square Enix Holdings (later transferred to United Inc.) |
| 2014 | Established Medical Fitness Laboratory Inc. with medical corporation NAIZ for preventive healthcare |
| 2015 | Opened "Data Fitness Roppongi" - medical-linked data-driven fitness club |
| 2015 | Started telemedicine support, stress check, and health checkup data management businesses |

### Business Evolution
- **Phase 1 (1996-2007):** Console/Arcade game development (PlayStation, PlayStation 2, Arcade, GameCube, DS, Wii)
- **Phase 2 (2008-2014):** Mobile social games (GREE, Mobage, Facebook) + Pachinko/Pachislot video production
- **Phase 3 (2015-Present):** Diversified entertainment conglomerate:
  - 2.5D Musicals (ヘタリア, 明治東亰恋伽)
  - Movie distribution (Asian films)
  - VR solutions (VOOR platform)
  - Incubation business
  - Education and healthcare ventures

## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Mission Statement
**「笑顔の創造」(Creating Smiles)**

> 笑顔には大きな力があります。笑顔は周囲の人を楽しくし、自分も楽しくなります。私たちは、仕事を通じて、一人でも多くの人の笑顔を誘い、幸せな気持ちを広げていきたい。
> 
> *Smiles have great power. A smile makes those around you happy, and makes you happy too. Through our work, we want to bring smiles to as many people as possible and spread feelings of happiness.*

### Core Values & Culture

1. **「遠慮禁止」(No Hesitation)**
   - Open communication culture
   - Rejecting the idea that "superiors are always right"
   - Creating an environment where people can work without holding back

2. **「良い意味でミーハーであれ」(Be a "fan" in a good way)**
   - Embrace new technologies with curiosity
   - Experience content as users do before creating
   - Balance customer perspective with business viability

3. **「ムダを許容するココロ」(Tolerance for "waste")**
   - Allow experimental projects that may not be immediately profitable
   - Focus on long-term (10-20 years) over short-term gains
   - Some "wasteful" projects lead to unexpected breakthroughs

4. **「打席数を増やして」(Increase your at-bats)**
   - Take more chances
   - Aim for "sudden greatness or spectacular failure" rather than "consistent mediocrity"
   - Current reputation: "Stable company that consistently passes" - but seeking transformation

5. **「マイナスの少ない会社」(A company with few negatives)**
   - Focus on eliminating barriers and frustrations
   - Not about flashy benefits, but about removing obstacles
   - Create an environment for spontaneous growth

### Recruitment Philosophy
- Seeks people who can "criticize cheerfully and comfortably"
- Values "自発的な人" (spontaneous/proactive people)
- Believes lucky people create their own fortune through perspective
- Looks for diverse personalities to enrich company culture

### Work Environment
- Average age: ~30 years old (relatively young workforce)
- 師匠弟子制度 (Mentor-mentee system) for new graduates
- 部活動制度 (Club activity system) - sports, literature, games, design
- LGBTQ-friendly workplace
- SDGs commitment

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
株式会社ポリゴンマジック (Polygon Magic Inc.) is a **comprehensive digital entertainment company** founded in 1996 in Tokyo. Originally established as a 3DCG-focused game development studio, the company has evolved into a diversified entertainment conglomerate with operations spanning:

- **Game Development** (Arcade, Console, Mobile, Social)
- **Pachinko/Pachislot Content** (High-quality 3DCG video)
- **Live Entertainment** (2.5D Musicals and stage productions)
- **Film Distribution** (Asian cinema in Japan)
- **VR Technology** (VOOR platform and solutions)
- **Incubation & Investment**

### Key Strengths
1. **Technical Expertise:** Nearly 30 years of 3DCG and game development experience
2. **Major Client Relationships:** Deep partnerships with Japan's leading game publishers (Capcom, Konami, Sega, Square Enix, Bandai Namco, etc.)
3. **Diversified Revenue Streams:** Not dependent on single business segment
4. **Mobile Social Game Pioneer:** Early partnership with GREE and success in the social game boom
5. **Content Creation Capability:** From game development to stage production to film distribution

### Financial Health
- Solid capital base of 325 million yen
- Profitable operations (20.4 million yen net income FY2025)
- Total assets of 1.77 billion yen
- Part of the Future Institute Corporation group since 2010

### Corporate Culture
The company operates under the mission of **"Creating Smiles"** with a culture that emphasizes:
- Open communication (no hesitation)
- Embracing new technologies with enthusiasm
- Long-term thinking over short-term profits
- Tolerance for experimental projects
- Focus on eliminating workplace frustrations

### Future Direction
Under President 鶴谷武親 (Takechika Tsurutani, since 2008), the company is transforming from a "stable but predictable" organization to one that takes more bold risks - aiming to create content that can "suddenly become huge or spectacularly fail" rather than consistently meeting minimum standards.

### Notable Legacy
- **Galerians Series:** Cult classic survival horror PlayStation games
- **Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune:** Popular arcade racing series
- **Incredible Crisis:** Unique PlayStation game with cult following
- **Extensive Arcade Portfolio:** Worked on major Konami, Namco, and Sega arcade titles
