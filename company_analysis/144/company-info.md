# 株式会社シスメトン (CISUMETON Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

株式会社シスメトン (CISUMETON Co., Ltd.) is a company based in the Kanto region that focuses on connecting people through "eni" (fate/connection). They provide system engineering services, IT solutions, and unique "eni" (connection) business services.

## Business Description

### SES (IT Personnel Support Service)
Specialized technical personnel support projects from upstream to downstream processes. System engineers and programmers provide project support across a wide range.

**Skills:** ASP, C#, VB.net, JAVA, PHP, PL/SQL, RPA, ERP packages, etc.

### IT Consulting
Careful business requirements hearing, solution plan proposals, business process design and improvement, business efficiency using IT, and proposing new ideas for further development.

### Eni (Connection) Business
Valuing connections between people and various industries. Regardless of administration, enterprise, or industry, they develop connections with all people and assist in regional, social, corporate, and human activation.

## Corporate Philosophy

### 人の真心 (Human Sincerity)
"いかなる技術躍進の先にも、必ず最後には人の手が、心があります。主体業務が機械的な業種だからこそ、真心を持たなければ良い仕事ができません。お客様は当然ながら、社員にも社会にも真心を持って向き合います。"

Translation: "Beyond any technological advancement, there are always human hands and hearts in the end. Because our core business is a mechanical industry, we cannot do good work without sincerity. We treat customers, employees, and society with sincerity."

### 情熱と発想 (Passion and Ideas)
"情熱は物事を動かす原動力です。そして発想からあらゆるイノベーションの可能性が産み出されます。類を見ない情熱と発想で、かつて無い事業やサービスを創生します。"

Translation: "Passion is the driving force that moves things. And innovation possibilities arise from ideas. With unparalleled passion and ideas, we create unprecedented businesses and services."

### 道徳と倫理 (Morality and Ethics)
"良い仕事は正しい道徳観や倫理観の基でなされるものです。観念は人それぞれとは言え、良好な人間関係を築く観念を信念としております。社員一人ひとり、活きた観念・社会通念を持って仕事に取り組んでおります。"

Translation: "Good work is done based on correct moral and ethical views. Although views vary among people, we hold as a belief the views that build good human relationships. Each employee works with live views and social common sense."

## Company Name Origin

The name "CISUMETON" is a created word combining "music" and "note" in reverse:
- music → cisum
- note → eton

Combined: "cisum" + "eton" = "Musicnote" (meaning sheet music)

The company views itself as sheet music, and all elements related to the company as musical notes. They aim to connect customers, employees, and even unrelated industries to create diverse, harmonious, unique new music (work) together.
