# ディエスソリューション株式会社 (D-Solution Inc.)

## Company Overview

D-Solution Inc. is a comprehensive medical and healthcare consulting firm that provides innovative solutions to healthcare institutions and professionals. The company focuses on fundamental problem-solving and creating social value through innovative healthcare approaches, with a philosophy of "understanding essence, communicating essence, building the world with essence."
