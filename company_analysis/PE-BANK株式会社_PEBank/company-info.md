# 株式会社PE-BANK (PE-BANK, inc.)

## Company Overview

PE-BANK is a comprehensive IT freelance/engineer services company founded in 1989. The company specializes in IT freelancer agent services, engineer dispatch, HR education, seminars, and welfare benefits for IT professionals. With a mission to "create a society where the social status of IT freelancers is further elevated," PE-BANK has built a 36+ year track record of supporting both freelance engineers and client companies.

Operating 12 offices across Japan from Hokkaido to Kyushu, PE-BANK manages approximately 100,000 registered engineers and has 2,279 active freelance IT engineers as of August 2025.

**Website:** https://pe-bank.co.jp/

The company's symbol mark features three cherries: the center represents PE-BANK, and the two connected cherries represent "IT freelancers" and "enterprises," expressing the good relationship and partnership between all three parties.
