## Company Philosophy & Culture

### Management Philosophy
- 人と人、人と社会の繋がりを大切に (Value connections between people and society)
- 各々にとって本当に良い関係や状態で生きていけるよう応援・教育し、共存していく (Support, educate, and coexist to enable everyone to live in truly good relationships and conditions)
- アットホームな会社 (At-home/family-like company atmosphere)

### Growth Philosophy
- 焦らずに、良い仲間を増やしていく (Don't rush; increase good colleagues steadily)
- 企業成長の速度や売上に拘らず、焦らず前向きに (Not fixated on growth speed or sales; proceed positively without rushing)
- "ずっと黒字でやれております" (Continuously profitable operation)

### Employee Relations
- 会社事由での解雇を一人もださず (No company-initiated terminations)
- Employees with 20+ years tenure present
- Many employees with 10+ years tenure
- Active recruitment and development of younger staff
