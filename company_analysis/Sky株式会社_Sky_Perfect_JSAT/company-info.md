# Sky株式会社 (Sky Perfect JSAT Corporation / スカパーJSAT株式会社)

## Overview

Sky Perfect JSAT Corporation is a leading Japanese telecommunications and space industry company operating multi-channel broadcasting and streaming video services. The company provides satellite communications, broadcasting services, and space business solutions under the "スカパー！" (SKY PerfecTV!) brand.

**Website:** 
- Main: https://www.skyperfectjsat.co.jp
- Broadcasting Service: https://www.skyperfectv.co.jp
- Brand Slogan: "未知を、価値に。" (Turning the unknown into value)
- Brand Identity: "通信 宇宙実業社" (Communications & Space Enterprise)

## History

Sky Perfect JSAT has established itself as Japan's largest multi-channel broadcasting service provider. The company operates satellite communications and has evolved into a comprehensive space industry enterprise, providing both broadcasting and telecommunications services.
