## Business Description

enefla株式会社 is a Japanese startup focused on **EV (Electric Vehicle) charging infrastructure**. Founded in December 2023, the company aims to contribute to carbon neutrality by solving infrastructure challenges. The name "enefla" combines concepts related to energy and flat/flow.

### Core Business Areas

1. **EV Charger Sales & Installation**
   - **Normal Chargers (普通充電器)**: Optimal for long-term parking at homes and workplaces; cost-effective charging
   - **Fast Chargers (急速充電器)**: Optimal for en-route charging and time-constrained situations; higher cost per charge
   - Both residential and commercial installations

2. **EV Charging Billing System "enefla Charge" (エネフラチャージ)**
   - Billing system for chargers used by unspecified individuals (apartments, commercial facilities)
   - **No membership registration required** - scan QR code to start
   - Single screen for charging and billing
   - Developed in-house
   - Available for normal chargers only
   - QR code is a registered trademark of Denso Wave Inc.

### Pricing Plans (enefla Charge)
- **Monthly Plan**: Base fee 2,970 JPY/month + 10% payment processing fee
- **Campaign (April-July 2026)**: Base fee reduced to 990 JPY/month for first 3 months
- Billing system is optional - customers can choose without it
