## 12. Summary

**テクノブレーン株式会社 (Techno Brain Co., Ltd.)** is a specialized engineer recruitment and career consulting company with nearly 30 years of experience, operating as a 100% subsidiary of Techno Pro Holdings. The company provides headhunting and RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing) services focused on high-end engineers and technical talent.

### Key Strengths:
1. **30-Year Track Record** - Extensive experience since 1992
2. **Engineer Specialization** - Focused exclusively on technical recruitment
3. **High-End Focus** - Targeting top-tier engineering talent
4. **Dual Services** - Both headhunting and RPO capabilities
5. **Strong Network** - Extensive connections with companies and engineers

### Service Portfolio:
1. **Headhunting** - Executive and senior engineer recruitment
2. **RPO Services** - Complete recruitment process outsourcing
3. **Career Consulting** - Professional guidance for engineers
4. **Scout Services** - Proactive talent identification

### Competitive Advantages:
- Deep understanding of technology industry evolution
- Specialized knowledge in engineer quality requirements
- Sincere and careful approach to matching
- Extensive human network built over decades
- Large corporation capabilities with boutique service quality

### Business Philosophy:
- Responding sincerely to both corporate needs and engineer passion
- Continuous evolution: "deeper, broader, and more cutting-edge"
- Making engineers' careers shine
- Creating the future that engineers will build

### Market Position:
- Part of Techno Pro Holdings group
- Located in prime Tokyo locations (Gotanda and Roppongi Hills)
- Privacy Mark certified
- Fully licensed for employment agency and worker dispatching services

### Industry Challenges Addressed:
- Declining birth rate and talent shortage
- DX talent scarcity
- Globalization of recruitment
- Increasing engineer quality requirements
- International competition for technical talent
