### Specialized Solutions for Bus & Transportation Industry

#### Driver Management Systems
- **乗務員・運転者台帳システム (Crew/Driver Ledger System)**
  - Manages crew and driver data from headquarters to branch offices
  
- **教育管理システム (Education Management System)**
  - Tracks training history: when, where, and what training employees received

- **点呼支援システム (Roll Call Support System)**
  - Digitizes roll call records for secure storage
  - Supports safe operations through various options

- **TENCHOL® (Alcohol Detector Measurement Check Tool)**
  - Uses alert screens and audio to prompt alcohol testing
  - Ensures reliable roll calls and alcohol testing management

#### Operations Management Systems
- **ダイヤシステム (Dia/Schedule System)**
  - Creates and manages data for stops, routes, distance-based fares, and distances
  
- **運行(勤怠管理)システム (Operation/Attendance Management System)**
  - Manages crew work plans, vehicle assignment, and actual performance
  - Checks compliance with "Improvement Standards Notification"

- **ZENkeeper® (Improvement Standard Notification Check Tool)**
  - Checks crew working hours for compliance with "Improvement Standards Notification" before and after operations

#### Passenger & Revenue Systems
- **乗降Compass® (OD System - Origin-Destination System)**
  - Analyzes passenger boarding/alighting data (OD data)
  - Supports efficiency in schedule composition and operation guidance

- **RAKUPA® (IC Backend System)**
  - Collects IC card sales and usage amount information
  - Manages boarding/alighting information
  - Tracks sales and usage status

#### Vehicle Management
- **車両管理システム (Vehicle Management System)**
  - Properly manages vehicle information
  - Prevents oversights such as expired vehicle inspections

#### Accounting Systems
- **営業所会計システム (Branch Office Accounting System)**
  - Streamlines accounting operations for "Sales & Taxes," "Bus Revenue Management," and "Inventory Management (IC cards, etc.)"

#### Other Products
- Custom solutions tailored to customer needs (non-proprietary products)

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
株式会社神奈中情報システム (Kanachu Information System Co., Ltd.) is a specialized IT services company established in 2002 as a spin-off from Kanagawa Chuo Kotsu. With 70 million yen in capital and 50 employees, the company serves as the IT backbone of the Kanachu Group, one of Japan's major transportation conglomerates.

### Core Strengths

1. **Industry Specialization**
   - Deep expertise in bus, passenger, and freight transport systems
   - Over 20 years of experience in transportation industry IT solutions
   - Comprehensive understanding of regulatory requirements (improvement standards, safety management)

2. **Complete Solution Provider**
   - End-to-end services from consulting to development, operation, and maintenance
   - Wide range of specialized products covering all aspects of transportation operations
   - Cloud-based services for modern, scalable solutions

3. **Quality & Trust**
   - ISO 9001:2015 certified system development processes
   - Privacy Mark certified for data protection excellence
   - Strong track record with proven products (trademarked solutions)

4. **Group Synergy**
   - Integral part of Kanachu Group's ecosystem
   - Serves 20+ related companies across transportation, real estate, and services
   - Benefits from deep domain knowledge within the group

### Business Model
The company operates on a B2B model, primarily serving:
- Kanachu Group companies (internal IT services)
- External bus operators and transportation companies
- Passenger and freight transport operators nationwide

### Competitive Position
- **Niche Leader:** Specialized in transportation industry IT solutions
- **Regional Base:** Strong presence in Kanagawa Prefecture, particularly Hiratsuka area
- **Innovation Driver:** Continuous development of new solutions (TENCHOL®, ZENkeeper®, RAKUPA®, 乗降Compass®)
- **Group Support:** Stable business foundation as part of large transportation group

### Future Outlook
With the transportation industry undergoing digital transformation (DX), Kanachu Information System is well-positioned to:
- Expand cloud-based services
- Develop solutions for emerging needs (autonomous vehicles, on-demand transport)
- Support safety management and compliance requirements
- Grow with the increasing need for operational efficiency in transportation

### Key Differentiators

- **Comprehensive Portfolio:** Complete suite of transportation management systems

- **Stable Foundation:** Part of established Kanachu Group
