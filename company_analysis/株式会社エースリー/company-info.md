# 株式会社エースリー (Acely Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Acely Co., Ltd. is an IT services company founded in 1990, headquartered in Kanagawa Prefecture. With approximately 180 employees, the company provides system development, infrastructure development and operations, and various IT services across a wide range of industries including finance, telecommunications, government/public sector, distribution, and manufacturing. The company combines fundamental technologies (hardware, software, communications, operations) to deliver comprehensive IT solutions.

Their brand "A-Thily" represents the company's recruitment and corporate identity, with the vision of becoming "the most continuously sought-after team of aces in the world."
