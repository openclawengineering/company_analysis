# ナレッジビーンズ株式会社 (Knowledge Beans Corporation)

## Company Overview

ナレッジビーンズ株式会社 (Knowledge Beans Corporation) is an IT company specializing in embedded Linux development, mobile application development, IoT/AI/cloud solutions, and IT human resource development. The company focuses on career development for engineers and aims to be "a company you want to stay with for life."

**Website:** [https://www.knowledgebeans.co.jp](https://www.knowledgebeans.co.jp)

## History

The company has been providing IT solutions with a focus on embedded systems and mobile application development. They have expanded their services to include IoT, AI, and cloud development, as well as IT education services.

## Capital & Employees

- **Headquarters:** Tokyo, Minato-ku, Toranomon (1 minute walk from Kamiyacho Station)

## Services/Products

### AI/Web/App Development
- Smartphone application development (entertainment, education, SNS, games)

- Cloud AI system development, operation, and maintenance
- Content production

### IoT/Embedded Systems
- Embedded/control system development
- Android OS/driver/application development
- Information appliance development
- In-vehicle information equipment
- Medical systems
- Energy-saving IoT platform development

### Human Resource Development Services
- Technical training for young inexperienced employees
- Linux, application development, and IoT technology training for immediate on-site effectiveness

## Philosophy

- 改善 (Improvement) - Customizing career paths based on individual goals and existing careers
- 価値創造 (Value Creation) - Turning career paths into fixed assets and increasing value
- "Building a company you want to stay with for life"
