## 3. Company History & Milestones

### Founding & Early Years
- **June 1, 1990:** Established in Nakahara-ku, Kawasaki City with a focus on firmware and hardware development

### Expansion & Development (2001-2010)
- **November 2001:** Relocated Kawasaki office to Kawasaki-ku, Kawasaki City due to business expansion
- **April 2010:** Joined Kanagawa Prefecture Information Service Industry Association (社団法人 神奈川県情報サービス産業協会)

### SES & Business Growth (2011-2018)
- **December 2011:** Started Specific Worker Dispatching Business (特定労働者派遣事業) - Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare notification (特14-304733)
- **March 2013:** Certified as "Kanagawa Child-Rearing Support Group" (かながわ子育て応援団)
- **November 2017:** Increased capital to 48 million yen
- **November 2018:** Changed from Specific Worker Dispatching to General Worker Dispatching Business (一般労働者派遣事業) - License: 派14-302492

### Recent Milestones (2020-2022)
- **June 1, 2020:** Celebrated 30th anniversary
- **June 1, 2020:** Website renewal
- **January 1, 2020:** Declared "SECURITY ACTION" two-star rating for information security measures
- **April 15, 2022:** Certified as Kawasaki SDGs Gold Partner (かわさきSDGsゴールドパートナー)

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
**株式会社サザン・エージェンシー (Southern Agency Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese IT services company founded in 1990 in Kawasaki, Kanagawa Prefecture. With over 30 years of experience, the company has built a strong reputation in embedded systems, software development, and hardware design.

### Key Strengths
1. **Deep Technical Expertise:** Extensive experience in firmware, embedded systems, and hardware-software integration
2. **Diverse Service Portfolio:** Covers embedded systems, business applications, web development, hardware design, and network operations
3. **Breadth of Industries:** Serves consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, public sector, and manufacturing
4. **Quality Certifications:** SDGs Gold Partner, security certifications, and child-rearing support recognition
5. **Established Presence:** Two office locations (Kawasaki and Kanazawa) with strong regional business network

### Business Model
- **Contracted Development:** Custom software and hardware solutions
- **SES (System Engineering Service):** Technical staffing services (General Worker Dispatching License)
- **Consulting & Support:** From small-scale projects to enterprise-level systems

### Competitive Position
- Mid-sized Japanese SIer (System Integrator) with specialized embedded systems expertise
- Strong in both legacy technologies (COBOL, mainframe migration) and modern platforms
- Regional focus on Tokyo-Kanagawa area with secondary presence in Ishikawa Prefecture
- Family-friendly and sustainability-focused corporate culture

### Recent Focus
- Modernizing recruitment (year-round hiring for new graduates)
- SDGs commitment and local community engagement
- Information security enhancement
- Expanding business application development alongside traditional embedded systems

### Financial Standing
- Solid capital base of 48 million JPY (increased from previous levels in 2017)
- Stable operation for over 30 years
- No publicly disclosed revenue or employee count figures
