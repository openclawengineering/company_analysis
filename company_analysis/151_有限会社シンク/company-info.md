# 有限会社シンク (Shink)

## Company Overview

有限会社シンク (Shink) is a diverse service company providing graphic design, web production, UI design, digital signage, drone photography, and SI (System Integration) services. Established in 2003, the company is based in Yokohama.

## Business Description

### Graphic Design
Design and planning including web/user interface (UI), digital signage content, and printed materials.

**Printed materials include:** business cards, envelopes, menus, pamphlets, flyers, posters, leaflets, logos, etc.

### Web Production
Supports web production and operation based on customer requirements, considering the user's perspective.

### User Interface (UI) Design
Proposes optimal UI designs considering user convenience.

### Digital Signage Content
Handles everything from a single still image. Proposes and produces digital signage content according to customer requirements and usage environment.

### Drone Photography (Video/Still Image)
Provides aerial photography, PR videos, and inspection services using drones.
- Specialist photographer still photography also available
- Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism Aviation Bureau Permission Obtained
- Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism Drone Body Registration Completed
- Liability Insurance Enrolled

### SI Business
Provides IT equipment introduction and system proposals for SOHO and small offices.
- Comprehensive support from proposal to design, construction, introduction, operation, and maintenance
- PC maintenance and management for SOHO and small offices
  - PC troubleshooting
  - Security measures
  - Virus removal and other troubleshooting
  - Network troubleshooting
  - etc.
