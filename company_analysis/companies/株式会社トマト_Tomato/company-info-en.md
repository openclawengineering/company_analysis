# 株式会社トマト（Tomato）

## Company Overview

Tomato Co., Ltd. is an IT company specializing in web application development. Its motto is "A breeding ground for cutting-edge technology born from communication and trust." It operates in three business areas: web system development, smart app development, and AI & Cloud Computing. It supports B2C/B2B app development and Android/iOS/HTML5 hybrid app development. It also handles IoT system construction through cloud environments such as AWS. Its clients include leading companies.

Tomato Co., Ltd. **Website:** https://www.jptomato.com/
