# 株式会社フロンティアポート（現：株式会社サテライト・ソリューションズ）

## Company Profile

Frontier Port Co., Ltd. is an independent system integrator headquartered in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. It is currently operating its business as Satellite Solutions Co., Ltd. The aim is to contribute to the information society by utilizing cutting-edge information and communication technology and software technology. Our customer base is centered on companies listed on the First Section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange, and we have a proven track record in a wide variety of industries, including major electronics manufacturers, banks, life insurance, and hospitals. In addition to focusing on training engineers and establishing an OJT system, we are also developing a programming education business for outsiders.
