# 株式会社セカンド・ウェーブ

## Company Profile

Second Wave Co., Ltd. is a human resources and career consulting company established in 2014. The representative is Takako Tanida. With the theme of "The world changes with just one choice," the company offers diverse work style proposals, talent placement and recruitment consulting, personal branding, career checkups (periodic career health checks), title creation services, and wellness businesses. The company actively practices side hustles and workation, proposing new ways of working and lifestyles.

## Business Activities

### Recruitment and Consulting

- Introduction of job opportunities for full-time and contract employees
- Consultation and introduction of diverse work styles such as side jobs, part-time work, outsourcing, entrepreneurship, and overseas work
- Personal branding support

### Recruitment Consulting and Recruitment Agency

- Proposal of personnel that match the company's strategy and culture
- Recruitment plan development and recruitment agency

### Career Dock

- Training and 1-on-1 consulting to regularly review careers and goals
- From individual career docks to corporate welfare training

### Title Craftsman

- Clarifying individual strengths and aspirations, and collaboratively creating a professional title (branding)

### Career x Wine Salon

- A salon-style event where participants casually discuss themes and goals while enjoying wine.

### Wellness Business

- Introduction and development of services and products for maintaining beauty, health, and well-being.

## Features

- Companion-type consulting starting from discovering "life's purpose"
- Fundamental career design support, not just job introductions
- Real-world advice from practitioners of side hustles and workation
- Support for diverse working styles (side hustles, entrepreneurship, overseas work, etc.)
- Providing career training as employee benefits for corporations
