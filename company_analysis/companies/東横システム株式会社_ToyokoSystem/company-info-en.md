# 東横システム株式会社

## Company Profile

Toyoko System Co., Ltd. is an independent IT solutions company based in Kohoku-ku, Yokohama City. Its main businesses are e-commerce site construction (Shopify), website creation, and branding support. Its mission is "To create creators who will change the world." It is a certified Shopify Expert Partner and has a strong track record in supporting the launch of D2C brands.

Toyoko System Co., Ltd. **Website:** https://www.toyoko-sys.co.jp/
