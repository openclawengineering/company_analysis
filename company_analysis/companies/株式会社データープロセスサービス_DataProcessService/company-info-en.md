# 株式会社データープロセスサービス（Data Process Service）

## Company Profile

Data Process Service Co., Ltd. (DPS) is a system development company headquartered in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture. It operates on three main pillars: control system development, business system development, and IT solutions. Since its founding, DPS has specialized in the development of control systems for communication networks and railways/transportation, boasting approximately 40 years of experience in communication infrastructure development, from public telephone networks to NGN (Next Generation Network). Its proprietary packaged products include the IC card-compatible attendance management software "Dakoku-chan Touch" series, the childcare attendance management software "Touen-chan Touch," and the cloud-based transportation and warehouse management system "Mercury." The company is certified as a Health and Productivity Management Outstanding Organization 2026 (Large Corporation Category) and a Sports Yell Company.

**Website:** https://www.dps-net.co.jp/
