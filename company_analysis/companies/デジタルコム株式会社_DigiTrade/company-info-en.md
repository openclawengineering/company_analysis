# デジタルコム株式会社 (DigiTrade Corporation)

## Company Overview

デジトレード株式会社 (DigiTrade Corporation), operating under the brand DGT, is a digital solutions company focused on distributing overseas media and digital platforms to solve social issues in Japan. The company has extensive experience operating international media brands and digital platforms.

**Website:** [https://dgt.co.jp](https://dgt.co.jp)

## History

- **September 20, 2019:** Founded on the day of the Rugby World Cup opening when Japan's national team made significant progress
- Since founding, the company has been exploring "high-performance digital" solutions to address social issues in Japan
- Currently in its third fiscal year, focusing on providing unique value that only DGT can deliver

## Capital & Employees

- Established: September 2019
- Currently in 3rd fiscal year

## Services/Products

### Overseas Media Operations
The company has Japan's largest track record in operating overseas media, including:
- The Independent (UK quality newspaper) - Exclusive distribution
- Goal.com (World's largest soccer site) - Exclusive distribution

### Digital Product (Platform) Sales Agency
- Distribution of advanced overseas digital platforms
- Sports-focused digital platform distribution (e.g., DAZN, Wyscout)
- "Spotter" system to identify cutting-edge digital services from overseas

### SNS Operations & Activation Support
- Operation of SNS accounts (Japanese versions) for major overseas companies
- Content translation and live streaming
- Influencer assignment for content diffusion
- Contract negotiations with celebrities (brand ambassadors)
- Supported SNS operations in 6 languages for global sponsors during World Cup

## Clients

- Sports industry clients
- European football clubs (SNS localization)
- Japanese companies (sponsorship activation support)
- SONY (Global SNS site operations during World Cup)

## Philosophy

**Mission:** To distribute "overseas media" and "digital platforms" to solve social issues

**Vision for 2030:** As the world undergoes major digital transformation, DGT is committed to increasing the "digital value" that clients can receive, with sincere dedication to supporting business transformation and growth.
