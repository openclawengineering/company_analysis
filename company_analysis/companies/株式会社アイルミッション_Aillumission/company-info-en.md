# 株式会社アイルミッション (Aillumission Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Isle Mission Co., Ltd. is an ICT solutions provider that solves customer challenges through information and communication technologies such as mobile networks, infrastructure construction, and DX (Digital Transformation). Since its establishment in 2013, the company has leveraged the high technical skills of engineers from major telecommunications carrier vendors, and driven by a sense of mission to support social infrastructure, continuously striving to solve customer problems and create new value.

The company's strength lies in its mobile infrastructure business, primarily focusing on 4G/5G base station construction, and it has numerous direct transactions with major telecommunications carriers. The company's business split in 2014 brought in many highly skilled engineers, giving it some of the leading telecommunications technology capabilities in Japan. In 2024, it also launched a cybersecurity business, expanding its operations into the ICT and software development fields.

**Website:** [https://www.aillumission.co.jp/](https://www.aillumission.co.jp/)

## History

- June 27, 2013: Established as Critic Mission Japan Co., Ltd. (CMJ)
- 2014: Business split implemented, many highly skilled engineers joined
- May 12, 2017: Obtained ISO/IEC 27001 certification
- June 21, 2024: Registered as a registered telecommunications carrier
- 2024: Started cybersecurity business
- Company name changed to "Aillumission Co., Ltd." (Date unknown)
