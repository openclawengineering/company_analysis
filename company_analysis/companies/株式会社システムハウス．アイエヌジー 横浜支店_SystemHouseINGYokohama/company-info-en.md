# 株式会社システムハウス・アイエヌジー 横浜支店（System House I.N.G.）

## Company Profile

System House I.N.G., Ltd. is an IT company established in February 1985. Its headquarters are located in Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, with a Yokohama branch (Takashima, Nishi-ku, Yokohama). Capital: 49 million yen. Its main businesses include software development, network construction and operation, IT consulting, and information security services. It holds the Privacy Mark certification.

**Website:** http://www.s-ing.co.jp/
