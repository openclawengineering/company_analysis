# 株式会社悟空テクノロジーズ（GOKU TECHNOLOGIES）

## Company Overview

Goku Technologies Co., Ltd. is an IT venture company established on October 5, 2021. With the vision of becoming "Japan's number one venture company that enriches people's lives," the company operates on four pillars: IT solutions, DX support, IT support (SES), and training consulting. As of September 2024, it is a rapidly growing company with 190 employees (including contract employees and part-timers). 99% of its employees started with no prior industry experience, and the company has a track record of creating over 200 inexperienced individuals in the IT market. Its motto is "Big Family!", aiming to create Japan's largest family, where people are connected by heart, even if not by blood. The company is ISMS (ISO27001) certified.

**Website:** https://www.goku-tech.jp/
