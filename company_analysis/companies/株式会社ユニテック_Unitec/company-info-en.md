# 株式会社ユニテック (Unitec Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Unitech Co., Ltd. is a system development company established in November 2001. With a mission to provide the best solutions for various industries, including construction, distribution, logistics, and manufacturing, we develop custom software, packaged software, and information processing services. Our head office is located in Kanda Higashi-Matsushita-cho, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo.

We are a small to medium-sized IT company with approximately 35 employees. We specialize in solutions for the construction industry and handle business system development for various other industries.
