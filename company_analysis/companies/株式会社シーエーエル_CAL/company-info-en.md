# 株式会社シーエーエル (CAL CO., LTD.)

## Company Overview

CAL CO., LTD. is an information systems company headquartered in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Established in 1979, it started as a computer application laboratory. We provide a wide range of information services, from legacy systems to web systems, offering comprehensive services from system planning and development to maintenance. Our goal is to become a 50-year, 100-year company, aiming for long-term business succession.

**Website:** [www.cal.ne.jp](https://www.cal.ne.jp)

## Philosophy

Since its establishment in 1979, CAL Corporation has been involved in a wide range of information services, from legacy systems to web systems. We accurately analyze the needs of customers in various industries and provide consistent services from system planning and development to maintenance.

Aiming to become a rare 50-year and 100-year company, we will pass on our business to the next generation. We strive to provide the latest information services to meet customer demands in all aspects, including quality, technology, and price.
