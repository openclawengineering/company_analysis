## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Philosophy (経営理念)
> **「人にやさしい情報社会を実現する」**
> 
> *"Realize a friendly information society for people"*

### 2030 Vision
> **「人と技術で、信頼されるITコンサルティングパートナーとして、社会に "ちょっと好い未来" を広げていく」**
>
> *"With people and technology, as a trusted IT consulting partner, spreading a 'slightly better future' to society"*

### Corporate Values

1. **People-Centric Growth**
   - Company growth stems from individual employee growth
   - Belief that "corporate growth comes not from systems or institutions, but from people's growth"

2. **Culture of Challenge**
   - Both young and veteran employees can raise their hands for new challenges
   - Trial and error is encouraged as a path to growth
   - No barriers between experience levels

3. **Evolution Mindset**
   - Transforming from a "company that builds systems" to a "company that solves problems"
   - Growing into an IT consulting partner that co-creates customer growth

### Leadership Message (From CEO Kiyoshi Okada)
> "We believe that such a culture - where young and veteran employees alike can raise their hands for new challenges, growing through trial and error - is the driving force that will open up our future."

## 9. Summary

### Company Overview
株式会社カールビット (Karbit Co., Ltd.) is a mid-sized Japanese IT solutions provider headquartered in Yokohama, established in July 2006. The company specializes in telecommunications infrastructure development, embedded software, and comprehensive IT consulting services.

### Key Strengths
1. **Deep Telecommunications Expertise** - Strong relationship with NTT DoCoMo group, recognized multiple times for technical contributions
2. **Steady Growth** - Revenue growth from ¥1.71B (FY2023) to ¥1.78B (FY2025)
3. **Quality Certifications** - Privacy Mark (2010) and ISO 27001 (2014) demonstrate commitment to security
4. **People-Centric Culture** - Strong focus on employee growth and team development
5. **Diversified Client Base** - Spans telecommunications, manufacturing, financial, and government sectors

### Business Focus
- Mobile network system development
- Enterprise software development
- IT infrastructure construction and operations
- Project management consulting
- Cloud service system integration

### Strategic Direction
The company is evolving from a traditional system development firm into an IT consulting partner, with a 2030 vision focused on creating "slightly better futures" through the combination of people and technology. Their emphasis on employee growth and collaborative culture positions them as a stable, relationship-oriented partner in Japan's IT services market.
