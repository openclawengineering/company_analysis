# 株式会社横浜電算

## Company Profile

Yokohama Densan Co., Ltd. is an IT company headquartered in Yokohama City. With the slogan "Creating the future together with you," its main business is the development of software packages for local governments and public institutions. It offers a wide range of package products, including comprehensive computer systems for labor insurance administrative associations, library lending management systems, cemetery management systems, childcare support center reception systems, consultation management systems, and park ledger management systems. In recent years, it has also expanded into cutting-edge technology areas such as RPA solutions, AI-OCR services, and ultra-high-speed data processing systems. It also operates the comprehensive childcare support system "Kid'sView" and the e-book subscription service "Dokushokan."

**Website:** https://yokohamadensan.com/
