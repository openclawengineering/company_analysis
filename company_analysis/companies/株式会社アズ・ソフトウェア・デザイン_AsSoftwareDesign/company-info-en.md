# 株式会社 アズ・ソフトウェア・デザイン (As Software Design Inc.)

## Company Overview

Az Software Design Co., Ltd. is an IT company headquartered in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Established in September 1990, its main businesses are the design and development of open-source system software, data warehouses, and communication and control systems. We provide system development services in a wide range of fields, including embedded systems for automobiles and business systems for government agencies. We also handle system consulting for small and medium-sized enterprises and provide various development engineer personnel services.

Az Software Design Co., Ltd. is an IT company headquartered in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture. **Website:** [www.asd-net.co.jp](http://www.asd-net.co.jp)
