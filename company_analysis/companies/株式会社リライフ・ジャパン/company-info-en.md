# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社リライフ・ジャパン (RLJ co.,ltd)

## Executive Summary

**RLJ Co., Ltd.** is a Japanese IT company established in 2000, specializing in financial system development, system consulting, and web application development. Headquartered in Shinagawa, Tokyo, the company has been providing high-level system development services primarily to the securities and financial industry for over 20 years. Since 2018, RLJ has been part of the CSS Group, leveraging the group's combined capabilities to expand its services.

---

## 1. Company History and Founding Date

### Timeline of Development

- **January 12, 2000**: Company established
- The company operated for nearly 20 years under founder Ozawa as CEO

- **June 2018**: Became part of CSS Group, entering a new stage of challenges

- **July 2024**: Founder and CEO Chairman Ozawa passed away
- The company continues under CEO Manabu Sagawa, inheriting the founder's vision.

RLJ has a strong foundation in securities system development, having built extensive experience and expertise over two decades of operation in the financial technology sector.
