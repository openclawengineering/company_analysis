# ミツイワ株式会社 横浜営業部 (Mitsui & Co., Ltd. - Yokohama Sales Department)

## Company Overview

Mitsuiwa Co., Ltd.'s Yokohama Sales Department is a sales base of Mitsuiwa Co., Ltd., located in Kitakou, Nishi-ku, Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Mitsuiwa's main business is the sale and repair of office automation equipment. The company's headquarters are located in Toranomon, Minato-ku, Tokyo, and it is a mid-sized general trading company established in 1964. Mitsuiwa Co., Ltd. handles the sale of information equipment, software and product development, IT infrastructure construction services and operation support, electronic device solutions, smart factories, IoT solutions, and 3D printers, conducting business across a wide range of fields from IT to energy and environment.

In 2020, the company launched "Foresight Innovating Company" as its brand concept, clearly demonstrating its commitment to supporting digital transformation (DX). The Yokohama Sales Department is responsible for the sales and repair of office automation equipment in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. As of April 2025, in addition to the Yokohama Sales Department, the company has multiple other locations in Kanagawa Prefecture, including the Yokohama First and Second Managed Service Departments and the Kanagawa Field Service Department. Mitsuiwa Corporation, a mid-sized general trading company with 970 employees and a capital of 4.09 billion yen, contributes to the advancement of IT in social infrastructure.
