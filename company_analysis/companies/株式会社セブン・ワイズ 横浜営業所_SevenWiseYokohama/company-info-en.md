# 株式会社セブン・ワイズ 横浜営業所 (SevenWise Co., Ltd. - Yokohama Office)

## Company Profile

Seven Wise Co., Ltd., Yokohama Branch, is a system integrator specializing in specialized services, headquartered in Nagoya City, Aichi Prefecture. Established in October 2000, it has grown from a capital of 770 million yen through multiple capital increases to a medium-sized company with 40 employees (as of April 1, 2019). The head office is located in Meieki, Nakamura-ku, Nagoya City, and the Yokohama branch is located in Minami-Saiwai, Nishi-ku, Yokohama City.

Seven Wise's mission is to provide customers with the highest quality service, focusing on its specialties (information technology, network infrastructure, and system integration). With its information technology and network divisions at its core, the company has built a business model that comprehensively supports the entire IT infrastructure lifecycle, including system development, network construction, system maintenance and operation, and cloud compatibility.

## Business Description

### Specialized Business

Seven Wise provides services to customers under the business category of "Specialized Business." Focusing on information technology and network infrastructure, system integration is a key strength.

#### Information Technology Department

- **Customer System Integration** — Integration, optimization, and migration of existing systems
- **System Development** — Planning, design, development, testing, implementation, and maintenance of various business systems
- **Network Construction and Operation** — Design, construction, and maintenance of LAN, WAN, and internet connections
- **Security Measures** — Firewalls, IDS/IPS, antivirus, and security policy formulation
- **Server, Storage, and Backup** — Server implementation, storage environment construction, and data backup
- **Cloud Support** — Design, migration, and operation of public and private clouds

#### Network Department

- **Network Monitoring and Management** — Implementation of monitoring tools, operational status monitoring, and fault response
- **Wireless LAN Construction** — Design and implementation of in-house wireless LANs and external wireless APs
- **Network Equipment Sales** — Sales and implementation of routers, switches, wireless APs, etc.

#### System Integration Department

- **System Integration** — Integration and data integration between different systems
- **System Implementation Support** — System requirements definition, implementation planning, and implementation support
- **Customer Training** — System operation training, user manual creation
