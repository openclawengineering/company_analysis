# 株式会社ＹＳＬソリューション（YSL Solution）

## Company Profile

YSL Solutions Co., Ltd. is an IT company established in July 1991. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of YSG Holdings Co., Ltd. As of March 2025, consolidated sales for subsidiaries are projected at 6.75 billion yen, and as of April 2025, it is a mid-sized IT company with 384 employees. It owns the Kannai Building in Naka-ku, Yokohama City, which serves as its headquarters. It has offices in Tokyo, Sendai, Osaka, and Fukuoka, and also has overseas bases in China (Hangzhou) and Vietnam (Hanoi). It holds a general temporary staffing business license. It also has a subsidiary, Sogo System Information Co., Ltd. (established in 1986, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo).

**Website:** https://www.ysl.co.jp/
