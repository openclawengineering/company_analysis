# 朝日ソフトウェア開発株式会社

## Company Profile

A software development company founded in 1979 (Showa 54). A long-established company celebrating its 48th anniversary. Leveraging human and technical expertise, we challenge ourselves to solve problems together with our clients, always striving to be a "trusted best partner" that our clients need. Our head office is in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture, and we also have a Sendai office (Sendai City, Miyagi Prefecture).

In February 2026, Shinobu Taniya assumed the position of Representative Director and Chairman, and Yumiko Takeda assumed the position of Representative Director and President.

**Website:** [https://www.asdweb.co.jp](https://www.asdweb.co.jp)

## Location

- Head Office: 15F, Parare Mitsui Building, 8 Higashida-cho, Kawasaki-ku, Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture 210-0005

## Business Activities

We develop and provide comprehensive software development services, from system analysis and design to development and manufacturing. We offer a wide range of expertise, including hardware construction and operational support.

### Head Office

- Development, implementation, and maintenance of electronic medical record and in-hospital departmental systems
- Development, operation, and maintenance of financial information systems
- Development of open systems for business applications and communication/control systems
- Development of embedded applications for electronics products

### Sendai Office

Primarily engaged in contract development, implementation, and maintenance of medical information systems.

- Development, implementation, and maintenance of medical accounting systems
- Development, implementation, and maintenance of in-hospital departmental systems
- Development, implementation, and maintenance of electronic medical record systems
- Development of life insurance systems

## Features and Strengths

- 48 years of experience and trust
- Extensive development experience in medical, financial, and embedded systems
- Flexible working hours (core time 10:00-15:00)

## Management Philosophy

We always view things from the same perspective as our customers, share problems with our business partners, and share both the joys and sorrows with our employees. We prioritize establishing and maintaining trusting relationships, aiming to be our customers' trusted best partner, and contributing broadly to society by always providing the optimal solutions.
