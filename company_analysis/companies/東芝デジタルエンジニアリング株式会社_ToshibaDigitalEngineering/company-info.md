# 東芝デジタルエンジニアリング株式会社 (Toshiba Digital Engineering Corporation)

## Company Overview

Toshiba Digital Engineering Corporation, a member of the Toshiba Group, is an IT solutions company that contributes to society through system integration activities. Headquartered in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture, we act as our customers' ICT partner, providing optimal solutions to enhance corporate competitiveness based on our long-cultivated business know-how. These solutions include improving core systems to increase operational efficiency, reducing ICT costs through virtualization and integration, and accelerating services through cloud utilization.

**Website:** https://www.toshiba-tden.co.jp/

## History

- **October 1, 2021**: Toshiba Digital Engineering Corporation established
- **Predecessor**: Integration and reorganization of system integration businesses within the Toshiba Group

## Services/Products

### Engineering IT Business

One-stop service to realize your needs

- Flexible development system including waterfall development, agile development, and DevOps
- Wide range of development from business systems to cloud-based systems
- Contributing to solving business challenges and improving the quality of product development and manufacturing

### Government IT Business

Solutions for realizing a digital society

- Digitalization of administrative procedures (online processing of various applications, etc.)
- Centralized data management, low-code development
- Integration with common authentication platform (g-Biz), integration with import/export port-related information (NACCS)
- Public infrastructure (LGWAN integrated administrative network, government common network, etc.)
- Security measures (personal information leakage prevention, tampering detection, vulnerability assessment)

### Package and Infrastructure IT Business

Supporting DX with cutting-edge technology

- Digital marketing, migration
- AI/data analysis and utilization, security
- Operation management/ITSM, data protection
- Application platform, IT infrastructure design and construction services

### Semiconductor IT Business

Accelerating the future of semiconductors with IT

- Development of automatic semiconductor cell placement and wiring tools
- Large-scale distributed processing DB, yield improvement simulation using machine learning
- Automatic production plan formulation using mathematical optimization
- PLM package products, document/BOM integration
- Production Management Systems (MES), Work Support Systems, Real-Time Dispatchers
- Technical Analysis Systems, FDC Monitoring and Analysis Systems
- Semiconductor Equipment Engineering Systems (EES), Equipment Online Systems
