# 株式会社 ユーズウェア (USEWARE Co., Ltd.)

## Company Profile

USEWARE Co., Ltd. is an independent software house established in May 1983 (Showa 58) by six engineers. The company name originates from the idea of ​​"providing customers with 'usable technology' (USE WARE)." Its main business is contract development of business applications, and it boasts the top market share in Japan for building business systems for joint venture factories in the feed industry. As its own packages, it develops and sells "Optimal Solution," an optimization simulation software based on feed formulation design know-how, and "BASEBALL CHART," information analysis software for professional baseball. It is a Privacy Mark certified company and also operates a temporary staffing business.

**Website:** https://www.useware.jp/
