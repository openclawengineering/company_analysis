## 9. Summary

**株式会社システムアイ (System I Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established independent software development company founded in 1996 and headquartered in Yokohama Landmark Tower. As part of the SHIFT Group since 2019, the company has experienced significant growth, doubling its revenue from approximately 2.2 billion JPY (FY2020) to 4.4 billion JPY (FY2023).

The company specializes in **custom software development, cloud solutions, and system operations** with a strong foundation in financial-grade quality systems. Their core strengths include:

1. **Financial Industry Expertise** - Decades of experience with banking and financial systems
2. **Cloud Excellence** - AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud partnerships with certified specialists
3. **Full-lifecycle Support** - End-to-end services from consulting to managed operations
4. **Modern Technology Stack** - Active adoption of AI, Kubernetes, and modern development frameworks

System I gained significant media attention for its innovative **"Bandman Recruitment" (バンドマン採用)** program, which recruits musicians as IT engineers, recognizing transferable skills like creativity, collaboration, and dedication. This unique approach has been featured on NHK and in major newspapers.

With 376 employees (as of April 2025), offices across Japan (Yokohama, Tokyo/Akihabara, Osaka, Fukuoka) and internationally (USA), and strong partnerships with Fujitsu, Red Hat, and major cloud providers, System I is positioned as a growing player in Japan's IT services industry.

The company's philosophy centers on **"Flexibility, Customer Orientation, and Technical Excellence"** - delivering "order-made optimal solutions" tailored to each client's unique business needs.
