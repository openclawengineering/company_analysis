# 株式会社ティー・エム・シー（TMC）

## Company Profile

TMC Inc. is an IT company headquartered in Yokosuka City, Kanagawa Prefecture. It operates on three main pillars: DX (Digital Transformation) projects for childcare facilities, system and application development, and website construction and operation. Its proprietary products include "Mimamori Kids," a QR code-based entry/exit management system for after-school kids' clubs, and "Newtus," a childcare facility management system. The company also has a track record of unique solutions, such as a uniform management system utilizing RFID and a Square-based web payment system.

**Website:** https://www.tmc-world.co.jp/
