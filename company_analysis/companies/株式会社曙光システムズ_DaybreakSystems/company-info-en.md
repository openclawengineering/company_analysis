# 株式会社曙光システムズ（Daybreak Systems）

## Company Profile

Shoko Systems Co., Ltd. is a system development company established in April 2004. It is a small system development company (25 employees) headquartered in Urawa Ward, Saitama City, Saitama Prefecture. We handle everything from modifying existing public and private systems to building and developing systems using the latest technologies. We specialize in the development of banking and securities systems, and have extensive experience in development across various industries including public sector, insurance, distribution, and service sectors. We hold the Privacy Mark certification and a temporary staffing business license. We are a KIA member company.

**Website:** http://daybreak-systems.com/
