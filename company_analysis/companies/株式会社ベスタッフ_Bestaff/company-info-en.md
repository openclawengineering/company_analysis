# 株式会社ベスタッフ（Bestaff Corporation）

## Company Profile

Bestaff Co., Ltd. is a system development company headquartered in Kitakou, Nishi-ku, Yokohama City. Its mission is "to build systems that delight our customers." Its main businesses include the construction of core and business support systems, consumer-oriented web system development, on-site/remote system development and operation support, and systemization planning and implementation consulting. It has a proven track record in a wide range of industries, including telecommunications, electricity/gas, media, real estate, medical/nursing care, insurance, education, and logistics. It supports technology stacks such as Java, JavaScript/TypeScript, PHP, C#, Vue.js, and AngularJS, and also handles AWS and Microsoft Azure cloud environments.

**Website:** https://www.bestaff.co.jp/
