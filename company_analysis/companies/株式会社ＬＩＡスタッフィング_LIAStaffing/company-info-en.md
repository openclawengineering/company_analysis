# 株式会社ＬＩＡスタッフィング（LIA Staffing）

## Company Profile

LIA Staffing Co., Ltd. is an IT personnel recruitment and staffing company headquartered in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. With the slogan "The world class for IT human resources," it specializes in IT industry personnel services. Its main businesses include permanent employee placement to foreign-owned IT companies and major Japanese IT companies, SES (System Engineering Services), and paid job placement. It is a member company of KIA (Kanagawa Information Service Industry Association).

**Website:** https://lia-staffing.co.jp/

## Business Activities

- Permanent employee placement of IT engineers (career change support)
- SES (System Engineering Services)
- IT personnel dispatch and temp-to-perm placement

## Features

- Specialization in IT industry personnel services
- Extensive track record of placements to foreign-owned and major Japanese IT companies
- KIA member company
