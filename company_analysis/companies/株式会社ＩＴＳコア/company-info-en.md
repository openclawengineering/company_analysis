# 株式会社ＩＴＳコア (ITS Core Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

ITS Core Co., Ltd. is a system development company based in Shin-Yokohama, Yokohama City, established in February 2004. We develop websites, business systems, and packaged software, and provide contract development software, packaged software, information processing services, and other internet-related services.

Our corporate philosophy is "As a professional group, we combine technical skills, teamwork, and human skills to provide valuable IT services that align with our clients' visions." We value interaction and communication among employees. Since our founding in 2004, we have developed our business with the two keywords "trust" and "growth" as our goals.

**Website:** [https://www.its-core.co.jp](https://www.its-core.co.jp)

## History
