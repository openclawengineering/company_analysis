# 株式会社ステップ (Step Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Step Co., Ltd. is a software development company founded in October 1986 by three engineers who focused on the potential of mobile communication networks. Building on its core business of developing mobile communication programs such as mobile terminals and communication control software, the company has expanded into fields such as web system development, AI, IoT, and data analysis, leveraging its accumulated technology and know-how.

Major clients include NTT Docomo, NTT TechnoCross, Docomo Technology, Comsys Information Systems, and Yokosuka City. Its main business is base station software, mobile terminal software, and support software. The company has 128 employees, is ISO 27001 certified, and is a Health and Productivity Management Outstanding Organization 2026 certified company.

**Website:** [https://www.step-japan.co.jp](https://www.step-japan.co.jp)
