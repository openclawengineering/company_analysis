# 株式会社テクノリサーチ（Techno Research）

## Company Profile

Techno Research Co., Ltd. is an IT company established in April 1988 (Showa 63) in Odawara City, Kanagawa Prefecture. The representative director is Nobuo Sugino. With a capital of 10 million yen and 34 employees (as of 2020), the company operates in four divisions: software development, internet business, hardware development and sales, and content business. It developed and began selling its own product, "DigiTen," in 1990. Major clients include PS Mitsubishi, Hitachi Systems & Services, Anritsu, Fujifilm, and NCR Japan. The company owns its own office building in Kōzu, Odawara City, and conducts business with Yokohama Bank, Sagami Shinkin Bank, Shizuoka Bank, and Shizuoka Chuo Bank.
