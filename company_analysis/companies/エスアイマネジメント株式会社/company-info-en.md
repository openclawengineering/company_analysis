# エスアイマネジメント株式会社 (SI Management Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

SI Management Co., Ltd. is an IT consulting and system development company established in November 1994. We handle a wide range of services, from computer system implementation planning, operation support, and consulting, to custom software development, package software development, sales, and maintenance. We also hold registered trademarks for "Cemetery User Information Management System," "Digital Worship," "Smart Worship," and "Smart Cemetery," and focus on digital solutions for temples and cemeteries.

Our staff consists of consulting staff (management consultants, social insurance labor consultants, tax accountants), PMs, SEs, PGs, web designers, and personal information protection specialists, enabling us to provide diverse IT services. We hold the Privacy Mark certification. We are a member of the Kanagawa Information Service Industry Association. We operate from two locations: our Shibuya headquarters and our Sendai Tohoku branch.

**Website:** [https://www.si-m.com](https://www.si-m.com)
