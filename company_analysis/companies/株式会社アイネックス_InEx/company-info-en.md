# 株式会社アイネックス (InEx Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

AINEX Corporation is a comprehensive corporate group committed to sustainable local communities. It operates in a diversified range of businesses, including construction, real estate, environmental consulting, waste secondary resource recovery, climate change countermeasures, food service, welfare services, agriculture, transportation, advertising production, and call center operations.

With the vision of becoming "a company that stands by the community," the company is committed to solving social issues in each region. While environmental businesses are at its core, the company contributes to the development of the entire region by handling a wide range of businesses, from construction and real estate to food service and welfare.

**Website:** [https://in-ex.co.jp/](https://in-ex.co.jp/)

## History

- May 2002: Incorporated as Kensou Kogyo Co., Ltd.
- 2007: Reorganized into a joint-stock company
- 2012: Changed company name to the current AINEX Co., Ltd., and constructed the head office building in Atsugi City
- 2010: Obtained a real estate license in parallel with the construction business, and began developing the real estate business
- 2024: Began mutual capital participation with Eco Creative Co., Ltd.
- Present: Promoting diversified management, developed into a group company with numerous subsidiaries
