## 3. Company History & Milestones

### Predecessor Company (Cosmos Co., Ltd.) History:

- **1996.05:** Founded in Yokohama Midori-ku with capital of 10 million yen as Cosmos Co., Ltd.
- **1997.04:** Joined Midori Corporate Association
- **1997.10:** Acquired internet domain
- **1997.11:** Launched company website
- **1998.04:** Joined Kanagawa Prefecture Information Processing Industry Health Insurance Association
- **1999.10:** Obtained Special Worker Dispatch Business License
- **2000.04:** Joined Kanagawa Information Services Industry Association (KIA) - General Incorporated Association
- **2000.07:** Moved to POTR2 Building, 308-11 Nakayama-cho, Midori-ku, Yokohama
- **2001.09:** Obtained General Worker Dispatch Business License
- **2003.04:** Joined Kanagawa SME Entrepreneurs Association
- **2003.05:** Increased capital to 20 million yen
- **2004.05:** Increased capital to 30 million yen
- **2006.10:** Obtained Privacy Mark Certification
- **2007.03:** Increased capital to 40 million yen
- **2008.10:** Moved to current location at Trust One Building 22, 4-21 Hirodaiota-cho, Kanagawa-ku, Yokohama
- **October 2008:** Joined Kanagawa Corporate Association
- **April 2009:** Developed and released package software
- **September 2010:** Launched "C Programming Course" as Emergency Personnel Training Project
- **April 2015:** Started renewable energy power generation business
- **March 2019:** Registered as CHO Concept Promotion Office (00372)
- **April 2019:** Declared achievement of 100% renewable energy since 2016
- **April 2023:** New Representative Director appointed
- **April 2025:** **Established Cosmos System Co., Ltd. through company split, inheriting software development business from parent company**

### Current Company (Cosmos System Co., Ltd.) Establishment:

- **April 1, 2025:** Established as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Cosmos System Co., Ltd. Through corporate split (newly established company)
- Inherited approximately 30 years of technology and know-how from parent company
- Specializes in image processing and other technical domains
