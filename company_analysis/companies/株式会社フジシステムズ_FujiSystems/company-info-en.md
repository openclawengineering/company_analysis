# 株式会社フジシステムズ (Fuji Systems Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Fuji Systems Co., Ltd. is a system integrator established in April 1982 as an IT company within the Fuji Sankei Group. Aiming to establish its own unique software technology, the company operates a wide range of businesses including control system engineering, communication network development, surveillance camera system manufacturing, embedded system development, system management, and online shop construction.

As a core IT company within the Fuji Sankei Group, including Fuji Television, it consistently promotes software development as a proactive system integrator, focusing on areas such as the internet, e-business, smartphones, car navigation, and digital broadcasting systems. It has approximately 108 employees.

**Website:** [https://www.fujisystems.co.jp](https://www.fujisystems.co.jp)

## History

- **April 1982** — Established as part of the Fuji Sankei Group
