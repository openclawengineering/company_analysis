# 株式会社日本マネジメントシステム（Japan Management System）

## Company Profile

Japan Management Systems Co., Ltd. is a company whose main business is consulting to support the acquisition of ISO management system certifications (ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO 45001, etc.). They provide support for the construction of various management systems, documentation support, internal audits, training, and registration audit support. They also handle support for obtaining ISMS (Information Security Management System) and Privacy Mark certifications.

**Website:** http://www.j-ms.biz/

## Business Activities

- ISO Management System Construction Support Consulting
- Support for Acquisition of Quality (ISO 9001), Environment (ISO 14001), Information Security (ISO 27001), and Occupational Safety and Health (ISO 45001) Certifications
- Internal Audit Support
- Training
- Documentation Support
