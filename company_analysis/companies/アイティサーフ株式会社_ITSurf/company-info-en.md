# 株式会社アイティサーフ (IT Surf Inc.)

## Company Overview

IT Surf Co., Ltd. is a system development company established in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture in 2006. He has been involved in web systems since the dawn of the Internet, developing e-commerce sites, business web applications, and mobile applications. We work as a group of engineers who are familiar with the latest technologies, including supporting migration from on-premises environments to cloud environments such as AWS/Azure, and building container technology using Docker.

The company also offers a unique service called "The Machines" platform solution that uses its own data center platform and provides a development environment free of charge for a certain period of time to web service and application development companies. In 2022, sales will reach 120 million yen. We also offer ``Machi Support,'' a DX service for neighborhood associations and neighborhood associations, and also make IT contributions to local communities.

**Website:** [https://it-surf.co.jp](https://it-surf.co.jp)

## History
