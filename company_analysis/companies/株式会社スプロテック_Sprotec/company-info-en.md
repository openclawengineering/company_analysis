# 株式会社スプロテック

## Company Profile

Sprotec Co., Ltd. is a software development company established in November 1985 (Showa 60) in Odawara City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Its name is derived from the three words "Special," "Professional," and "Technical." It handles all fields without distinction between personal computers, workstations, and embedded systems, and handles a wide range of projects, from creating spreadsheet macros to assembler work for single-chip computers, development using C++ and .NET on Windows, website creation, and web application development. The company practices advanced work styles, such as introducing a telecommuting system (virtual office) in 1996. In 2016, it opened the "Sprotec Store" on Yahoo! Shopping to sell its own products.

**Website:** https://www.sprotec.com/
