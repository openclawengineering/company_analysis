# 株式会社リアルソフト (Realsoft Inc.)

## Company Overview

Realsoft Co., Ltd. is a Yokohama-based software company established in April 2005. Under the philosophy of "Make Everyone Happy with IT Solutions," the company primarily develops business systems for enterprises. They offer one-stop services for ERP package implementation consulting and add-on development. Their proprietary production management package, "Mister," supports Chinese and English, and they have a sales record in China through their Shanghai subsidiary.

The company has 83 employees and a capital of 33 million yen. Their head office is located on the 5th floor of the Chuo 6th Kannai Building in Kannai, Naka-ku, Yokohama. They provide consulting, business system development and implementation support, and maintenance services to manufacturing and distribution industries. They employ an agile development process.

**Website:** [https://www.realsoft.co.jp](https://www.realsoft.co.jp)
