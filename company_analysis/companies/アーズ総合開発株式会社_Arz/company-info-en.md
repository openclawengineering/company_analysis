# アーズ総合開発株式会社 (Arz Co., Ltd.)

## Overview

Aars General Development Co., Ltd. is an IT company headquartered in Shinagawa-ku, Tokyo. Registered in 2004, it is a stable company with 21 consecutive years of profitability. It holds ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 certifications.
