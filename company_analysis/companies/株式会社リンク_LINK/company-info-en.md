# 株式会社リンク (LINK, INC.)

## Company Overview

LINK Co., Ltd., established on November 18, 1987, is a comprehensive IT company engaged in cloud hosting, cloud-based telephony, security platform, and agricultural businesses. Headquartered in Kita-Aoyama, Minato-ku, Tokyo, it also has branches in Osaka and Fukuoka. It maintains a privately held company structure, employs all employees as full-time staff, and has unique internal systems such as a variable retirement age.

Its main services include the server hosting service "at+link," the cloud-based telephony service "BIZTEL," the email relay service "Bearmail," PCI DSS-compliant cloud services, payment gateway services "Pay TG" and "Smart TG," and the operation of the agricultural production corporation "Nakahora Farm."

**Website:** [https://www.link.co.jp](https://www.link.co.jp)

## Business Activities

### Cloud Hosting Business

- **at+link** — High-quality server service
- **at+link Remote PC Service** — Desktop environment service
- **Bare Metal Cloud** — On-demand service for physical servers
- **Bare Mail** — Mail relay service achieving high mail deliverability
- **Bare Support** — Infrastructure operation support
- **Bare Care** — System Life Extension Support After EOL/EOSL

### Cloud-Based Telephony Business

- **BIZTEL** — Cloud-based call center service (20th anniversary in 2026)
- **BIZTEL shouin** — Smaller version of BIZTEL

### Security Platform Business

- **PCI DSS Ready Cloud** — Cloud service specialized for the credit industry
- **Cloud Token for Payment Card** — Tokenization service
- **Pay TG** — Card information non-retention service for telephone/postcard/fax orders
- **Smart TG** — Store subscription payment service

### Agricultural Business

- **Nakahora Farm** — Sustainable dairy farming, manufacturing and sales of milk and dairy products
