# 株式会社システムズナカシマ 東京支店（Systems Nakashima）

## Company Profile

Systems Nakashima Co., Ltd. is an IT company established in 1960. Headquartered in Matsuyama City, Ehime Prefecture, it also operates a Tokyo branch. Its main businesses are information processing services and the research, development, and sales of information processing software. A long-established company founded in 1960, it operates nationwide from its base in Shikoku.
