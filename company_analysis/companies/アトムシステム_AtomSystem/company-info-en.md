# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社アトムシステム (Atom System Co., Ltd.)

## Executive Summary

**株式会社アトムシステム** (Atom System Co., Ltd.) is a well-established Japanese independent SI (System Integration) company with over 40 years of experience in the IT industry. Founded in 1981 and headquartered in Fujisawa City, Kanagawa Prefecture, the company has built a strong reputation as a reliable IT partner serving major Japanese corporations with comprehensive system development, maintenance, and advanced technology solutions.

---

## 1. Company History and Founding Date

### Timeline of Development

- **December 1981 (Showa 56)**: Founded as "株式会社湘南三川" (Shonan Sansen Co., Ltd.)
- **June 1982 (Showa 57)**: Changed company name to "株式会社スリー・エス" (Three S Co., Ltd.)
- **May 1983 (Showa 58)**: Renamed to current name "株式会社アトムシステム" (Atom System Co., Ltd.)
- **March 2000 (Heisei 12)**: Certified as SI enterprise by Ministry of International Trade and Industry (now METI)
- **May 2008 (Heisei 20)**: Licensed for labor dispatching business (License No. 派14-300735)
- **August 2018 (Heisei 30)**: Acquired Privacy Mark certification (Registration No. 17003379)
- **September 2020 (Reiwa 2)**: Relocated headquarters to current address at Fujisawa City, Kanagawa

The company has grown from its humble beginnings to become a recognized independent system integrator with a track record spanning over four decades, primarily serving the Tokyo metropolitan area.

The company operates with a unique vision called the **"Three Happy"** philosophy:

1. **Customer Happy** - Customers are satisfied
2. **Colleagues Happy** - Team members and partners are fulfilled
3. **Family Happy** - Families of employees are happy

President Tetsuya Hosono emphasizes that true business success cannot be achieved unless all three parties are happy. The company believes that successful enterprises of the future must achieve all three "Happy" outcomes to be widely recognized by society.
