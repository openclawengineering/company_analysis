# サンネット株式会社 (SUNNET Corporation)

## Company Overview

SUNNET Corporation is a comprehensive information services company founded in 1969 (Showa 44), headquartered in Odawara, Kanagawa. With 233 employees and capital of 32 million JPY, the company provides information system services spanning the entire computer system lifecycle — from planning and design through development and maintenance. The company operates a dual-headquarters structure with the main office in Odawara (serving Kanagawa/West-Shonan area) and a Tokyo HQ (serving primarily Tokyo clients). SUNNET also operates a workation office in Naganuma, Hokkaido.
