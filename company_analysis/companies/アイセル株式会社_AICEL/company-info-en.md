# 株式会社アイセル (AICEL Inc.)

## Company Overview

Aisel Co., Ltd. is an IT company founded in 1989 as "System Technology Research Institute," specializing in building business systems for corporations. In 2000, through a merger with ITEC, it expanded its business into an IT company with two main pillars: embedded software development and embedded systems development. Currently, it has offices in Tokyo (Ueno, Taito-ku), Osaka, and Saga, employing a total of 156 engineers.

With the vision of "Going one step beyond customer satisfaction," the company operates three businesses: business solutions, security solutions, and cloud services. Its "Aisel brand solutions," known for their high quality in web-based business system construction, have earned the strong trust of many customers. It is a certified member of the Apple Consultants Network and has strengths in iPhone/iPad app development. It also has extensive experience in developing inventory management systems utilizing RFID and AI, and has a proven track record in a wide range of industries, including manufacturing, distribution, wholesale, finance, and service industries.

**Website:** [https://www.aisel.ne.jp](https://www.aisel.ne.jp)

## History

- January 1989 — Established as a system technology research institute for building business systems for corporations
- 2000 — Merged with ITEC, expanding business to include embedded software development
- 2000s — Focused on web-based business system development, launched "Aisel brand solutions"
- 2010s — Expanded into cloud system development and smart device application development
- 2020s — Developed inventory management DX solutions utilizing RFID/AI, certified as an Apple Consultants Network member

## Services/Products

### Business Solutions

- **Business System Development** — One-stop service from planning and design to development, testing, operation, and maintenance
- **iOS App Development** — Design and development of iPhone/iPad applications (Apple Consultants Network member)
- **RFID Inventory Management System** — DX transformation of inventory management using RFID readers/tags
- **AI Image Recognition System** — Efficiency improvement of inventory management and inspection operations using AI-based image recognition
- **ECM Document Management System** — Construction of a document management system using Oracle packages
- **Migration** — Migration of aging systems to the latest environment

### Security Solutions

- **FinalCode** — File Encryption Software Implementation Proposal
- **AppGuard** — Endpoint Security Product Implementation Proposal

### Cloud Business
