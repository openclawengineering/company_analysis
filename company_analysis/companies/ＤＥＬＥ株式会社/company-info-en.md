# ＤＥＬＥ株式会社 (DELE Inc.)

## Company Overview

DELE Co., Ltd. is a platform company specializing in data "deletion." We operate a data erasure traceability business, a data erasure platform business, sales and rental of data erasure equipment, and data erasure services (on-site and mail-in). Our mission is to eliminate information leaks and risks at their root by securely erasing data, thereby alleviating people's anxieties.

Our headquarters are located in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture. We hold ADEC erasure process certification, a Privacy Mark certification, a secondhand goods dealer license, and an industrial waste collection and transportation permit. We are a "Kawasaki☆Elboshi" and "Kawasaki SDGs Gold Partner" certified company. We serve as the accounting director of the Data Proper Erasure Execution Certification Council.
