# TERAPOC株式会社

## Company Overview

TERAPOC Corporation is a software development company specializing in industrial machine control systems. The company develops software that connects machines and people by understanding the status and operations of various industrial machines used in different environments. With comprehensive capabilities ranging from factory in-line systems to remote monitoring and wireless systems, TERAPOC provides flexible, customer-tailored solutions.
