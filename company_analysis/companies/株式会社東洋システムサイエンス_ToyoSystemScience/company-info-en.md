# 株式会社東洋システムサイエンス

## Company Profile

Toyo System Science Co., Ltd. is a software development company based in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Its main products are nutrition management and calculation software, and it provides solutions specializing in the field of school lunch and meal management.
