# 株式会社CIJ（Computer Institute of Japan）

## Company Profile

CIJ Corporation is a publicly listed IT company established on January 7, 1976 (Showa 51). It is listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market (Securities Code: 4826). The company name is an abbreviation of "Computer Institute of Japan." Its headquarters are located on the 17th floor of the Yokohama Gate Tower in Takashima, Nishi-ku, Yokohama City. Its main business is system development and related services, providing a wide range of solutions including DX, AI/robotics, industry-specific solutions, business process improvement, system operation, development support, and research and development support. For the fiscal year ending June 2025, consolidated sales were 26,899 million yen, and it is a major SIer with 1,677 consolidated employees.

**Website:** https://www.cij.co.jp/
