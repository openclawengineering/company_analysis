# 株式会社神奈中情報システム (Kanachu Information System Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Kanachu Information Systems Co., Ltd. is a system integrator established in April 2002 as the IT division of the Kanagawa Chuo Kotsu Group. It builds and operates the information systems for the Kanachu Group, centered around Kanagawa Chuo Kotsu Co., Ltd. (a bus operator), and also provides IT consulting, system development, maintenance and operation, and IT training services to external clients.

The Kanachu Group operates a wide range of businesses, including not only bus services but also taxis, real estate, automobile sales, leisure and sports, and hotels, playing a key role in supporting the digitalization of the entire group. Its headquarters are located in Hiratsuka City, Kanagawa Prefecture. It is certified as a Health and Productivity Management Outstanding Organization 2026.

**Website:** [https://www.kanachu-it.co.jp](https://www.kanachu-it.co.jp)
