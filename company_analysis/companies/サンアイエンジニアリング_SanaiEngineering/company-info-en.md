# 株式会社サンアイエンジニアリング (Sanai Engineering Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

San-ai Engineering Co., Ltd. is a software development company based in Saiwai-ku, Kawasaki City, established in October 1991. With over 30 years of history, the company's main businesses include software development, testing/verification, and implementation/operation support. 90% of its projects are direct contracts, giving it a stable business foundation. The company specializes in developing business systems for companies, particularly third-party verification services, focusing on core business systems such as attendance management, sales management, and payroll systems.

Its clients include companies well-known from TV commercials. The company is certified as a Health and Productivity Management Outstanding Organization 2026 (Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Category) and is a Kawasaki SDGs Partner. It also made the Kanagawa Healthy Company Declaration (September 2020).

**Website:** [https://www.sang.co.jp](https://www.sang.co.jp)
