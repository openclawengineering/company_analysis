# インフォテックサーブ株式会社 (Infotech Serve Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Infotech Serve Co., Ltd. is an IT company based in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture, established in 1998. It handles information processing services, software development, network construction, and website creation and operation. With a small, highly skilled team of 15 people, the company is characterized by its close-knit, meticulous service delivery to clients.
