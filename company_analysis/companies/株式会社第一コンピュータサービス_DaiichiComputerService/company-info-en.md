# 株式会社第一コンピュータサービス（DCS）

## Company Profile

Daiichi Computer Service Co., Ltd. (DCS) is a long-established IT company with over 50 years of history. Its philosophy is "Contributing to the information society by providing optimal systems." In addition to its activities in the IoT-centric solutions field, the communications field supporting mobile phone networks, and the development field as a new business, it has also been developing and operating financial systems since its founding. In May 2025, the representative director changed (Yoichi Masuda, Chairman → Jun Ayuzawa, Representative Director).
