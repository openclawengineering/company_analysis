# 株式会社サプライム (Suprime Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

Supreme Co., Ltd. is a newly established IT company founded in 2024 as a subsidiary of Hodaka Electronics Co., Ltd. With the philosophy of "creating the future and new value through free thinking and contributing to society," the company handles IoT-compatible design, system efficiency improvements, and embedded software development for the automotive and manufacturing industries.

Business commenced on October 1, 2024. Inheriting the technological capabilities and know-how of Hodaka Electronics (listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Standard, stock code: 6669), the company is headquartered in Shin-Yokohama. It is a newly established corporation that started with approximately 10 employees.

**Website:** [https://www.suprime.co.jp](https://www.suprime.co.jp)
