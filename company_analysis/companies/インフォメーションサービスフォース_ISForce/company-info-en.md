## 4. Products and Services

### 4.1 System Product Business (システム・プロダクト・ビジネス)
Development, sales, maintenance, and customization of proprietary software products.

#### Mimasaka Series (みまさかシリーズ)
A core business software package for small and medium-sized enterprises with approximately **1,500 companies** as customers and **20+ years** of market presence.

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **みまさか販売 (Mimasaka Sales)** | Multi-dimensional sales, purchasing, and inventory management system with integrated support for estimates, orders, shipping, billing, and collection |
| **みまさか会計 (Mimasaka Accounting)** | Financial accounting system that streamlines financial processing and facilitates management decision-making |

**Key Features of Mimasaka Products:**
- Developed using proprietary 4GL (Fourth Generation Language) called "Mimasaka Tool" (美作ツール)
- Programming possible in Japanese
- Supports multi-dimensional analysis
- Available for direct sales and through authorized resellers
- Help desk support for system operation guidance
- Remote operation support
- Customization services available

## 6. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Management Philosophy (経営理念)

**Core Mission:**
> "Through system engineering, we contemplate the essence of social contribution, aim to improve engineer mindsets and the social status of engineers, and provide customers with excellent services and high-quality products."

**English Translation of Philosophy:**
> システムエンジニアリングを通じて社会貢献の本質を考え、エンジニアマインドの向上とエンジニアの社会的地位向上を目指し、以ってユーザに充実したサービスと高品質な製品を提供する

### Core Values

1. **Best Solutions for Society** (最適なソリューションで社会貢献を)
   - IT as critical infrastructure for modern society
   - Focus on reliability, availability, and confidentiality of information systems
   - Acting as IT consultants ensuring healthy information systems

2. **Engineer Mind Development**
   - System engineering reflects engineers' abilities and personalities in products
   - Continuous learning of latest technologies
   - Uncompromising effort until go-live
   - Thoughtful and detailed engineer mindset cultivation

3. **Proposal-Based Service**
   - Going beyond immediate customer requests
   - Generating value-added ideas
   - Creating new business opportunities through innovative proposals

### Engineer Mind Principles (エンジニア・マインド)

The company has established 15 core principles for engineers, declared on July 31, 2009:

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Project Success** | The most important job for SEs is to lead projects to success; IT is merely a means |
| **Contribution Focus** | Engineers are evaluated by their contribution to project success, not just technical skills |
| **Win-Win Philosophy** | Project success means achieving a 1:1 benefit for both the company and customer |
| **Triple Benefit** | Excellent projects benefit the company, customer, and society equally (1:1:1) |
| **Ethical Responsibility** | Never participate in anti-social projects |
| **IT as a Tool** | IT is a powerful tool that moves people and changes society; project success justifies its use |
| **Information Security** | Protecting confidential information from leakage, tampering, and loss is an engineer's duty |
| **Shared Responsibility** | Everyone involved in a project shares responsibility regardless of position |
| **Equal Task Importance** | All project tasks carry equal responsibility regardless of difficulty |
| **Best Solution** | Finding compromises between incompatible challenges (quality vs. productivity) |
| **Moving People** | Best solutions drive project members, departments, and even customers and third parties |
| **Positive Thinking** | Believing there's always a way to succeed, with continuous effort, produces best solutions |
| **Customer Listening** | Listening sincerely to complaints without excuses helps engineers grow |
| **Broad Knowledge** | Not being fixated on specialties but being knowledgeable helps engineers grow; those who grow others grow themselves |

### Company Culture

**Work Style:**
- Proposal-based service approach rather than simple order-taking
- Global perspective on how each project impacts society
- Value-added engineering through innovative thinking

**Organizational Characteristics:**

**Quality Assurance:**

## Summary

Information Service Force Co., Ltd. (ISForce) is a well-established Japanese IT company that has grown through strategic M&A to combine over 30 years of technical expertise. The company offers a comprehensive range of IT services including:

- Proprietary business software (Mimasaka series) serving 1,500+ companies
- System integration and engineering services for major enterprises
- Mainframe solutions for legacy system support
- Partnership programs connecting skilled engineers with projects

With offices in Tokyo and Osaka, serving 200+ clients across finance, manufacturing, healthcare, and government sectors, ISForce maintains a strong commitment to engineering excellence, ethical business practices, and social contribution through technology.
