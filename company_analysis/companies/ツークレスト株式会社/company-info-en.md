# ツークレスト株式会社 (Two Crest Co., Ltd.)

## Company Overview

TwoCrest Co., Ltd. is an IT company based in Yokohama, established in January 2013. Its main businesses include the proposal, design, development, and maintenance of information systems; the development and sales of POS systems; and the construction and operation of servers, networks, and firewalls. With a small, highly skilled team of 11 people, the company is characterized by its flexible and close-knit approach to clients. It holds a Privacy Mark certification. The company also offers comprehensive employee benefits, including full social insurance, iDeCo+ (with a portion of contributions covered by the employer), and permission to work remotely.
