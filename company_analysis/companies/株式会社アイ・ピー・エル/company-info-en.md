# 株式会社アイ・ピー・エル

## Company Overview

IPL Co., Ltd. is an IT company specializing in the development and provision of the video distribution system "Classtream." Since its founding in 1992, the company has been dedicated to the development of video distribution technology for many years, and employs many highly skilled information processing engineers (national qualification holders).

## Business Description

IPL Co., Ltd. provides video distribution services for businesses through its flagship video distribution platform, "Crastream." The company specializes in secure video distribution technology, including live video streaming and limited distribution of recorded videos.

In November 2025, it became a wholly owned subsidiary of J-Stream Corporation, and as a member of the J-Stream Group, aims to create new business models through the integration of IT, entertainment, and AI technologies.

Major clients include large corporations such as Japan Post, Nissan Motor, Sony, NEC, Mitsubishi Chemical, Ricoh, Mitsubishi Motors, Tokyo Gas, Taisei Corporation, and Yamato Transport.

## Key Characteristics

- **Specialization:** Development and provision of the video distribution system "Classtream"
- **Technology:** ISO27001/27017 certified, high-security video distribution platform
- **Innovation:** Development and provision of the Vtuber-focused platform "V-TIPS"
- **Partnership:** Partnership with a pioneer in streaming technology as part of the J-Stream Group
- **Scale:** Over 1 million cumulative users (as of 2024)
