# 株式会社システムクルー

## Company Profile

System Crew Co., Ltd. is a system integrator based in Nishi-ku, Yokohama City. Founded in 1986, it is a long-established IT company that describes itself as a "pilot and IT coordinator in the information society." It handles a wide range of services, from software development for mainframes, UNIX, and PC servers to game software planning and development. It also develops web systems, provides system consulting, and sells products (POS systems, office automation equipment, etc.). It has commercialized its own web-POS system, "Web-POS." It has obtained the Privacy Mark certification.

**Website:** https://www.syscrew.co.jp/
