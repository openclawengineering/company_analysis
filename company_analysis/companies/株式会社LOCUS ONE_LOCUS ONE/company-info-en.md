# 株式会社LOCUS ONE（ローカス・ワン）

## Company Profile

LOCUS ONE Co., Ltd., established on December 2, 2024, is an IT company engaged in ICT consulting, system engineering, web production and maintenance, telecommunications business, and recruitment outsourcing. Its headquarters are located in Chigasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture, with a Yokohama office in Nishi-ku, Yokohama City.

The company name "LOCUS ONE" is a coined word combining "LOCUS" (important place) and "ONE" (unique), reflecting the desire to "create a unique and important place for employees and customers."
