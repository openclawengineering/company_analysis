# 株式会社ＴＳＵＮＡＧＵ

## Company Profile

TSUNAGU Co., Ltd. is an IT company headquartered in Minato Mirai, Nishi-ku, Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. Established in November 2017, its main businesses are web and mobile application development, system development, and UI/UX design. As its name suggests ("Tsunagu" meaning "to connect"), the company provides solutions that connect people to people, people to technology, and companies to users. Its office is located in the Yokohama Landmark Tower.
