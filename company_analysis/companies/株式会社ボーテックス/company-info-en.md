# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社ボーテックス (VORTEX CO., LTD.)

## Executive Summary

**株式会社ボーテックス** (VORTEX CO., LTD.) is a Japanese IT company established in 1986, specializing in system development, network construction, system migration, hardware/software sales, and comprehensive consulting services. Headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, the company provides IT solutions from hardware procurement to software development and system operation, serving clients with a focus on quality and customer satisfaction.
