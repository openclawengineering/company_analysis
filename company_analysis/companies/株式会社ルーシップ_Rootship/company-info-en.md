# 株式会社ルーシップ（Rootship）

## Company Overview

Luship Co., Ltd. is a system development company established in September 2013. In October 2024, it established and launched Citeras Co., Ltd. as a group company. Specializing in the design and development of core business support systems, the company operates with three main pillars: system integration, Salesforce implementation support, and contract development. Utilizing the latest technology stacks such as Golang, AWS, and Salesforce, it has a wide range of experience in areas including advertising distribution, payment processing, government My Number compliance, and SNS integration systems. As of 2025, the organization is primarily composed of young professionals, with over half of its employees in their 20s.

**Website:** https://www.rootship.co.jp/
