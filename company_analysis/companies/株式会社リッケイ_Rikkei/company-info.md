# 株式会社リッケイ

## Company Overview

Our company is a DX/AI development partner with over 2,000 IT engineers and over 13 years of experience supporting the DX (Digital Transformation) of Japanese companies. Leveraging our hybrid development system, a collaboration between Japan and Vietnam, we provide smooth communication in Japanese, ensuring a secure and reliable environment for DX implementation. We support a wide range of industries, primarily manufacturing, logistics, retail, and finance, with the use of cloud technology and AI for operational efficiency improvements, modernization, and system integration, driving value creation through cutting-edge technologies such as generative AI.
