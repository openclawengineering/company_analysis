# 株式会社新日本コンピュータサービス (Shin-Nippon Computer Service Inc.)

## Company Overview

Shin-Nippon Computer Service Inc. (SNCS) is a Kanagawa-based information services company founded in 1971 (Showa 46), headquartered in Chuo-Rinjan, Yamato City. With 92 employees (including contract workers) and capital of 20 million JPY, the company operates three core business areas: human resources support, data entry services, and system development/operations. The company also has an office in Sagamihara.
