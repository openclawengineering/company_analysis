# ノックスデータ株式会社 (KNOX DATA Inc.)

## Company Overview

Knoxdata Co., Ltd. is a software development company established in May 1974. It is a group company of ISB Co., Ltd. (Securities Code: 9652), a company listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Section. Since its founding, the company has been engaged in software development, primarily in communication infrastructure, and undertakes contract software development for 5G optical transmission equipment and broadcast video.

With the spread of IoT, the company is adapting to the changing environment where all "things" are connected to the internet, leveraging its agility to flexibly respond to customer needs. It has approximately 179 employees and annual sales of 2.05 billion yen. Its head office is located in Minami-Ooi, Shinagawa-ku, Tokyo, and it has a Nagoya office (Meieki). It is ISO 9001 certified.

**Website:** [https://www.knox.co.jp](https://www.knox.co.jp)

## History

- **May 1974** — Established (Omori Kita, Ota-ku, Tokyo)
- Started development of communication infrastructure software
- Moved to Minami Ooi, Shinagawa-ku due to business expansion
- Increased capital to 45 million yen
- Opened Nagoya office (expanded business in the Chubu region)
- Obtained ISO 9001 certification
- Present — Operating as a member of the ISB Group
