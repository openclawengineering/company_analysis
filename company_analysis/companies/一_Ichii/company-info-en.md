# 株式会社一 (Ichii Corporation / 1corp) - Company Analysis

## 1. Company Name

**Japanese:** 株式会社一 (Kabushiki-gaisha Ichi)

**English:** Ichii Corporation / 1corp

**Meaning of "一" (Ichi):**
The company name "一" (meaning "one" in Japanese) embodies three core principles:
- **Number One:** Striving to be the best (一番優れていることを目指す)
- **Only One:** Conducting unique activities unlike any other (他に類のないユニークな活動をする)
- **Next One:** Serving as a driving force for the next generation (次世代に向けた牽引役を果たす)

## 6. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Core Philosophy

**On Software and the Modern Era:**
The company believes that in today's knowledge-driven society, software is the engine that generates wealth. They argue against approaches based on:
- Human wave tactics (人海戦術)
- Ad hoc responses (場当たり的な対応)

**Modern Software Challenges:**
They emphasize that future software requires approaches different from traditional problem-solving, encompassing:
- Deep theoretical foundations (深い理論)
- Life-like and complex behaviors (生命的で複雑な振る舞い)
- Business integration (ビジネスとの連動)
- Social mechanisms (社会的な機構)
- Creative design (創造的なデザイン)

**Broader Definition of Software:**
The company views software not just as computer programs, but as anything that incorporates knowledge and "executes," including:
- Operational processes
- Organizational human activities
- Knowledge-embedded systems

### Cultural Values

**Client Relationships:**
- Trust is the most important element in client relationships
- Trust is cultivated through daily human interactions, not just consulting work
- Initial contact often begins through:
  - Publications (books, papers, blogs)
  - Lectures and seminars
  - Community activities
  - Mutual understanding and empathy

**Engagement Approach:**
- Start with casual discussions on topics of interest
- Build relationships through seminars and lectures before formal consulting
- Offer light analysis and advisory services as entry points
- Encourage informal contact to explore potential collaboration

### Research and Intellectual Pursuits

**Knowledge Exploration:**
- Active engagement with the Agile Process Council / Chidoka Research Association
- Pursuit of wide-ranging research themes beyond software
- Focus on philosophical questions and deep inquiry
- Exploration of topics not widely investigated in society

**Thought Leadership:**
- Regular symposium organization examining contemporary issues
- Focus on paradigm shifts in industry and society
- Critical examination of AI trends and their implications
- Commitment to "Chidoka" (知働化 - intellectual labor/knowledge work) research

### Vision

**"The True Era of Software":**
The company believes we are entering the genuine age of software, where traditional approaches must evolve to meet the complexities of modern knowledge-driven society.
