# 株式会社ディーインクリエイティブ

## Company Profile

DIN CREATIVE Co., Ltd. is an IT solutions company based in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. As a partner to small and medium-sized enterprises, we specialize in problem-solving utilizing cutting-edge AI and web technologies. We handle a wide range of services, from server infrastructure to web application development, including cloud VPS, private cloud, dedicated server provision, colocation services, SSL certificate sales, and email relay services. We also have a track record in developing our own AI parking management system, "AI-Parking," and smartphone applications.
