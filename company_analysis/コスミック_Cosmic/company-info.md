## 7. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Management Philosophy: "おかげさまの精神" (The Spirit of Gratitude)

**Core Values:**
The company operates on the fundamental principle of "Okagesama" (gratitude/interdependence), which manifests as:

1. **Customer Focus:** "A company that cannot be trusted by employees cannot be trusted by customers"
2. **Responsibility:** Taking full responsibility for work entrusted by customers
3. **Sustainability:** Profits support company operations and protect employees' livelihoods

### Corporate Philosophy (企業理念):

1. **Service Excellence:** Provide high-productivity and high-quality services to earn customer satisfaction and trust
2. **Employee Respect:** Respect each employee, prioritize health, provide opportunities for challenge and success, and aim for stable work and personal life
3. **Capability Development:** Enhance individual employee abilities and strengthen corporate power through unity and coordination
4. **Security Awareness:** Maintain high security awareness and comply with laws protecting personal and customer information
5. **Regional Contribution:** Focus on community-based services and contribute to local society
6. **Total Solution:** Accurately understand customer operations and provide total system integration from planning and problem solving to system construction and operation

### CEO Message:
Representative Director Yoshiki Nara emphasizes:
- Partnership built with numerous customers since founding in 1988
- All achievements built on the "Okagesama" spirit
- Commitment to leveraging AI, IoT, and high-speed communication technologies
- Creating services needed by society through IT technology as both company growth and social contribution

## 9. Summary

**株式会社コスミック** is a well-established regional IT system development company based in Sagamihara, Kanagawa Prefecture. Founded in **1988**, the company has over **35 years** of continuous operation in the Japanese IT industry.

**Key Strengths:**
- **Regional Advantage:** Strategic location enabling easy on-site support in Kanagawa prefecture center and West Tokyo areas
- **Technical Expertise:** High technical capability combined with strong human skills
- **Industry Specialization:** Deep domain knowledge in insurance, railway, medical, automotive, and financial sectors
- **Philosophy-Driven:** Strong "Okagesama" (gratitude) culture emphasizing mutual trust with employees, customers, and society

**Business Model:**
The company operates primarily as a **SES (System Engineering Service)** provider and **contracted software developer**, serving major IT vendors and end-user companies. Rather than developing its own products, Cosmic Inc. provides skilled technical resources and development services to client projects.

**Current Focus:**
- Actively recruiting embedded system engineers
- Embracing new technologies including AI, IoT, and advanced communication systems
- Maintaining strong presence in financial, healthcare, and transportation sectors
- Contributing to regional development through community-focused services

**Company Size:**
With 28 employees and 10 million yen in capital, Cosmic Inc. represents a typical successful mid-sized Japanese IT services company - large enough to handle significant projects while maintaining the personalized service and agility of a smaller organization.

The company's longevity and continued growth in a competitive industry demonstrates successful execution of its customer-centric philosophy and strong regional positioning in the Greater Tokyo Area.
