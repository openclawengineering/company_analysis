## Business Description

株式会社エハラタカオ provides IT services to clients in the Kanagawa and greater Tokyo area. As a KIA member, the company operates within the regional IT ecosystem, which includes collaboration with other member companies, participation in industry events, and access to Kanagawa Prefecture's IT promotion programs.

The Kanagawa IT Industry Association (KIA) was established to promote the information service industry in Kanagawa Prefecture. Member companies benefit from networking opportunities, industry-government collaboration, recruitment support, and business matching services.
