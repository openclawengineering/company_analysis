# 株式会社インタースペース (Inter Space Inc.)

## Company Overview

株式会社インタースペース (Inter Space Inc.) is a comprehensive IT solutions company based in Kobe, providing software development, internet solutions, security camera systems, and electrical construction services. The company focuses on bridging digital solutions with on-site implementation.

**Website:** [https://www.isf.co.jp](https://www.isf.co.jp)
