# ニューテック株式会社 (Newtech Corporation)

## Company Overview

株式会社ニューテック (Newtech Corporation) is a specialized IT infrastructure company focusing on storage solutions, data replication, backup systems, and high-performance computing solutions. The company was listed on the stock exchange but announced delisting in March 2026.

**Website:** [https://www.newtech.co.jp](https://www.newtech.co.jp)

## History

- Established as a storage solution provider
- Expanded into AI/deep learning solutions
- Listed on the stock exchange
- **March 2026:** Announced stock delisting
