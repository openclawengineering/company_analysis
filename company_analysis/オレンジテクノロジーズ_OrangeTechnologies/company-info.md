# 株式会社オレンジテクノロジーズ (Orange Technologies Inc.)

## Company Overview

Orange Technologies Inc. is a Yokohama-based software development company established in 1986, specializing in custom software development and embedded systems. The company handles control systems, business systems, DX/AI utilization, and embedded software development across diverse industries including automotive, railway, mobile phones, LCD panels, and logistics. The company is a member of the Kanagawa IT Industry Association (KIA) and Yokohama Chamber of Commerce.

**Website:** http://www.orangetech.co.jp/
