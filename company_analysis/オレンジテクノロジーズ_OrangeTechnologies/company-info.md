# 株式会社オレンジテクノロジーズ (Orange Technologies Inc.)

## Company Overview

Orange Technologies Inc. is a Yokohama-based software development company specializing in custom software development and embedded systems. The company is a member of the Kanagawa IT Industry Association and is located in the Shin-Yokohama area.

## Business Description

Orange Technologies develops software for a wide range of applications. According to the KIA member directory, they contribute to fields including automotive, railway, mobile communications, LCD panels, and material handling systems. Beyond software requirements definition and system proposals, the company is also involved in chip and hardware selection, development, and manufacturing.

## Key Characteristics

- **Hardware-software integration** — Unusual capability to handle chip selection, hardware development, and manufacturing alongside software
- **Diverse industry exposure** — Automotive, railway, telecommunications, and manufacturing sectors
