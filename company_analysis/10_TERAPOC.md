## Business Description

TERAPOC株式会社 is a Japanese software development company specializing in **industrial machine control software**, **IoT monitoring systems**, and **broadcasting equipment control software**. The company also develops its own IoT products incorporating AI and big data technologies.

### Core Business Areas

1. **Industrial Machine Control Software Development (産業機械制御ソフト開発)**
   - Develops software connecting industrial machinery and human operators
   - Handles factory inline systems, industrial equipment operation management, and wireless systems
   - Capable of flexible response to diverse equipment and machinery types
   - Deep understanding of industrial machine characteristics for optimal system design

2. **IoT Monitoring Software Development (IoTによる監視ソフト開発)**
   - Remote operation management for measuring instruments, welding machines, and mobile equipment
   - Position management and monitoring systems
   - Small-to-medium scale IoT systems developed end-to-end (server-side to client applications)
   - Business efficiency improvement and labor shortage mitigation through IT

3. **Broadcasting Equipment Control Software (放送機器制御ソフト)**
   - Specialized in broadcasting equipment control, including non-linear editing systems
   - News production support and production efficiency applications
   - Video material management, ingest processing, server transfer handling
   - Comprehensive software development for broadcasting equipment control

4. **In-House Product Development (自社開発)**
   - IoT-based proprietary product development
   - AI and big data utilization
   - Targeting industries facing labor shortages and slow IT adoption
   - Research and development in smart glasses and drone technologies
