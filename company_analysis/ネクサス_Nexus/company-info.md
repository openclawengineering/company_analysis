## 9. Summary

**株式会社ネクサス (Nexus Co., Ltd.)** is a well-established Japanese software development company founded in 1990 and headquartered in Osaka Prefecture. With over 35 years of history, the company has built its reputation on:

- **Core business systems development** - Specializing in enterprise-level software for mission-critical operations
- **Legacy system expertise** - Strong capabilities in mainframe-based systems (COBOL/CICS) that remain vital to Japanese enterprises
- **Financial sector focus** - Extensive experience serving banks, insurance companies, and securities firms
- **Utility industry relationships** - Long-standing partnerships with electric and gas utilities
- **Regional presence** - Strong base in Kansai (Osaka area) serving major corporations

The company operates as a traditional Japanese IT contractor (SES/受託開発), providing both project-based development services and engineer staffing. Their client roster includes major system integrators (SCSK, TIS) and utility company subsidiaries (Kanden Systems), indicating they serve as a reliable technical partner for large-scale enterprise projects.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Long-standing expertise in legacy/mainframe systems that few companies maintain
- Deep relationships with regulated industries requiring stable, reliable technology partners
- Focus on mission-critical business systems where continuity and reliability are paramount
