# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社ＩＳＴソフトウェア (IST Software Co., Ltd.)

---

## Executive Summary

**IST Software Co., Ltd.** (株式会社ＩＳＴソフトウェア) is an established independent IT company based in Tokyo, Japan, with nearly 50 years of operational history. As a wholly-owned subsidiary of I-NET Corporation, the company specializes in software development, embedded systems, cloud services, and digital transformation (DX) solutions. With over 500 employees and annual revenues exceeding ¥8 billion, IST Software serves diverse industries including government, healthcare, insurance, financial services, and manufacturing.

> "We will continue to stand from a 'customer-first' perspective, based on 'respect for people,' and strive to create an organization rich in ideas and action through 'human resource development.'"
