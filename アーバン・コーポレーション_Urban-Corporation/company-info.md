# Company Analysis Report

## アーバン・コーポレーション株式会社 / Urban Corporation Co., Ltd.

---

## Executive Summary

**Urban Corporation** is a Japanese IT services company established in 1992, specializing in system development, accounting outsourcing, and BPO services. With over 30 years of operation, the company has expanded from Yokohama to multiple offices across Japan and has established international operations in Vietnam.
