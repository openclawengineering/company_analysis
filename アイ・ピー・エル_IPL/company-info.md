# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社アイ・ピー・エル (IPL Co., Ltd.)

## Executive Summary

**IPL Co., Ltd.** (株式会社アイ・ピー・エル) is a Japanese software development company headquartered in Atsugi City, Kanagawa Prefecture.
