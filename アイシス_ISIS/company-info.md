## Executive Summary

ISIS Co., Ltd. is a well-established Japanese IT services company specializing in business software support and system development. Founded in 1983 and incorporated in 1988, the company has built a solid reputation over nearly four decades, serving more than 7,000 clients across various industries.

### Origin of Company Name
The name "isis" derives from the musical term "double sharp" (ダブルシャープ) in German notation, meaning "up and up" (上へ上へ). The founder, who was a piano teacher, chose this name with the aspiration that the company would continuously improve and ascend. This reflects the company's philosophy of constant growth and development.
