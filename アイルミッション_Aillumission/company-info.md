# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社アイルミッション (Aillumission Co., Ltd.)

---

## Executive Summary

**株式会社アイルミッション** (Aillumission Co., Ltd.) is a Japanese ICT solutions provider headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. The company specializes in business consulting, ICT environment construction, system development, and security services. With a strong corporate philosophy centered around contributing to people's happiness through technology, Aillumission has grown steadily since its founding in 2013.

## 5. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Message

> **「人の幸せのすぐそばに。」**
> **"Close to people's happiness."**

This message reflects the company's commitment to not only creating value through ICT but also being mindful of potential negative effects of technology and working to mitigate them.

### Management Philosophy (経営理念)

> **ICTによる『顧客課題の解決』と『新しい価値創造』を通じて、豊かで幸せな未来の実現に貢献する。**
>
> *"Contributing to the realization of a rich and happy future through 'customer problem solving' and 'new value creation' through ICT."*

### Management Vision (経営ビジョン)

> **ICT技術に精通し、高いプロ意識、豊富な経験、最高のチームワークを発揮する事で、お客様から信頼され期待され続ける、業界トップレベルのICTソリューションプロバイダーになる。**
>
> *"To become a top-level ICT solutions provider that is trusted and expected by customers, by demonstrating ICT expertise, high professionalism, abundant experience, and the best teamwork."*

### Core Values (コアバリュー)

1. **Customer Focus** - Focus on solving customer problems and achieving customer satisfaction
2. **Innovation** - Continue challenging new value creation and create "new common sense in ICT"
3. **Pride & Responsibility** - Take pride and responsibility in working with ICT as critical social infrastructure
4. **Teamwork & Individuality** - Pursue working styles where individuality can shine while aiming for the best teamwork
5. **Growth Opportunity** - Aim to be a company that offers the greatest growth opportunities in the world
6. **Social Responsibility** - Fulfill social responsibility to all stakeholders while building good relationships
7. **Enjoy the Journey** - Challenge, fail, grow, overcome difficulties, walk with colleagues, and enjoy work itself!

### Key Company Saying

> **「仕事はお客様の為にある。仕事は自分の為になる。」**
> *"Work exists for the customer. Work becomes for oneself."*

This principle is emphasized every year during new employee orientation, reinforcing that customer needs are the starting point, and through sincere dedication to work, employees gain livelihood, job satisfaction, and personal growth.
