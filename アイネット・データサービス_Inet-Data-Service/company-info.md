# Company Analysis Report

## 株式会社アイネット・データサービス (Inet Data Service Co., Ltd.)

---

## Executive Summary

Inet Data Service Co., Ltd. is a Japanese IT services company operating as a special subsidiary (特例子会社 - Tokurei Kogaisha) of I-NET Corporation, focused on promoting employment opportunities for people with disabilities while providing essential data processing and IT support services.

---

## 1. Company History and Founding

### Founding Details
- **Established:** April 1, 2009
- **Special Subsidiary Certification:** August 3, 2009
- **Certification Authority:** Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare (厚生労働省)
- **Historical Significance:** Certified as the 36th special subsidiary company in Kanagawa Prefecture

### Background
The company was established by I-NET Corporation (株式会社アイネット), a major Japanese IT services provider founded in 1971, with the specific purpose of promoting employment for people with disabilities. This follows Japan's "Law for the Promotion of Employment of Persons with Disabilities," which encourages companies to create specially designated subsidiaries that provide supportive work environments.

### Parent Company Context
I-NET Corporation (the parent company) is a publicly traded company on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market ( Securities Code: 9600), founded in 1971 as Fuji Consult Co., Ltd. The parent company operates data centers, cloud services, and system development businesses, serving over 4,300 corporate clients.

## 5. Company Philosophy and Culture

### Corporate Philosophy
**"自立そして自律" (Independence and Self-discipline)**

The philosophy is built on three pillars:

1. **自立 (Independence):** Aiming for employees to stand on their own as members of society
2. **成長 (Growth):** Encouraging personal development through striving toward individual goals
3. **自律 (Self-discipline):** Enabling employees to act according to their own thinking

### Mission Statement
> "We support employees with disabilities to become independent through their own power and to act based on their own thinking."

### Corporate Culture

#### Commitment to Inclusion
- Primary purpose is promoting employment for people with disabilities
- Provides supportive work environment tailored to individual needs
- Focus on job development and skill building

#### Health and Wellness Focus
The company has received multiple certifications demonstrating its commitment to employee well-being:

1. **Health & Productivity Management Outstanding Organization 2025** (健康経営優良法人2025)
   - Small and Medium Enterprise Category
   - Certified for 3 consecutive years (2023-2025)

2. **Yokohama Health Management Certification - Class AAA** (横浜健康経営認証【クラスAAA】)
   - Highest certification level
   - Certified twice (initially in 2022, renewed in 2024)

3. **Kanagawa Treatment and Work Balance Promotion Company** (かながわ治療と仕事の両立推進企業)
   - Platinum Company certification (2020)

4. **CHO (Chief Health Officer) Concept Promotion Business** (CHO構想推進事業所)
   - Registered in October 2017

Its multiple health management certifications reflect a genuine commitment to employee well-being beyond mere regulatory compliance.
