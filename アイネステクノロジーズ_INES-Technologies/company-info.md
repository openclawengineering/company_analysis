# Company Analysis Report

## 株式会社アイネステクノロジーズ / INES Technologies Co., Ltd.

---

## Executive Summary

INES Technologies Co., Ltd. is a Japanese IT infrastructure and cloud services company headquartered in Yokohama, Japan. Established in 1999 as a Hitachi Solutions group company, it has evolved into a specialized provider of cloud computing, IT lifecycle services, and digital solutions. The company is a subsidiary of INES Corporation (株式会社アイネス), a publicly traded system integrator listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime Market.

| Year | Milestone |

| **1999** | Founded as **株式会社日立ソフトサービス** (Hitachi Software Service Co., Ltd.) - spun off from the data center division of Hitachi Solutions (formerly Hitachi Software Engineering) |

| **2010** | Started SaaS-based JENNIFER service; Launched "VM Rental Economy" |

| **2014** | Started offering "FogosPro" Windows PC virtual thin client solution |

## 9. Summary

株式会社アイネステクノロジーズ (INES Technologies Co., Ltd.) is a well-established Japanese IT infrastructure company with over 25 years of experience. Originally founded as a Hitachi Solutions data center subsidiary, it has transformed into a comprehensive cloud and IT services provider within the INES Corporation group.

**Key Strengths:**
- Deep expertise in cloud computing and IT infrastructure
- Strong focus on security and reliability (Privacy Mark certified)
- Comprehensive IT lifecycle services
- Supportive company culture with low workplace stress
- Part of a publicly traded group with significant resources
- Quick, responsive customer service

**Target Market:**
- Small to medium enterprises without dedicated IT staff
- Companies requiring secure, reliable cloud infrastructure
- Organizations needing flexible IT lifecycle management
- Government and public sector entities

The company's evolution from Hitachi's data center operations to a modern cloud services provider demonstrates its ability to adapt to technological changes while maintaining its core values of reliability, security, and customer service.
