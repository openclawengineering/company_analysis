## Executive Summary

**株式会社アクロイト** (Acroit Corporation) is a Japanese IT services company specializing in software development, technical support, and system engineering services (SES). Established in 2019 as a spin-off from a larger engineering conglomerate, Acroit has positioned itself as a flexible, software-focused subsidiary serving various industries with custom development and technical staffing solutions.

## 2. Company History & Founding

### Background

Acroit Corporation was established on **July 1, 2019**, through a corporate spin-off (新設分割) from its parent company, 株式会社アド・ソアー (Ad Soar Co., Ltd.). 

**Historical Context:**
- Prior to the spin-off, the parent company operated as a comprehensive engineering firm covering mechanical, electronic, and software systems
- The division was created to **specialize exclusively in software development** (business applications, web systems, embedded server systems, and network solutions)
- The separation enabled faster, more flexible management decisions and the ability to explore new business domains

### CEO Background

**田村 孝章 (Takaaki Tamura)**
- Born: 1973, Chiba Prefecture
- 1997: Joined 株式会社アド (now Ad Soar Co., Ltd.) as a mechanical design engineer
- Later transitioned to ICT department management, leading department launch and operations
- 2019: Became Representative Director & President of Acroit Corporation upon spin-off

**External Appointments:**
- Vice Chairman, Kanagawa Information Service Industry Association (multiple committees: Corporate Management, Industry-Academia Collaboration, Diversity)
- Committee Member, Kanagawa Prefectural Yokohama Sakae High School School Management Council
- Committee Member, Yokohama System Engineering College
- Committee Member, Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare (Workplace Adaptation Support Working Group)
