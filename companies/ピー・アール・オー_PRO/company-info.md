# 株式会社ピー・アール・オー / P.R.O Inc.

## Overview
P.R.O (Professional Progress Produce) is a Tokyo-based IT company with three core business segments: SI (system integration), solutions, and digital content. They develop smartphone apps including the hit "Solitaire V" series (7.5M+ cumulative downloads), game platforms like "Bushimo" (3M users), and enterprise solutions including Cloudinary (image optimization) and ScoreBase (blockchain project evaluation). They also release LINE mini-apps.

## Key Details
- **Website:** https://www.pro-japan.co.jp/
- **Philosophy:** "Professional Progress Produce — Realize 'wouldn't it be nice if'"

## Business Areas
- **SI Business:** End-to-end from planning/proposal through operation — one-stop development
- **Solution Business:** Cloudinary (Israel-originated image/video optimization), ScoreBase (digital asset/blockchain evaluation platform)
- **Digital Content Business:** Smartphone games — Solitaire V, Princess Solitaire, Trump Card Collection, Big Rich, LINE mini-apps
- **Works:** Game platforms (Bushimo — avatar, gacha, chat for 3M users), web+mobile multi-platform development
