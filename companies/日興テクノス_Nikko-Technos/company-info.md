# 日興テクノス株式会社 / Nikko Technos Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Nikko Technos is a Yokohama-based IT company founded in 1946 (80 years of history) providing comprehensive IT and network solutions — from planning and design through construction, operation, and maintenance. A Fujitsu partner, they serve as a regional Yokohama contributor with strong focus on healthcare/welfare systems and network integration. Certified as a Healthy Management Excellent Corporation (Bright 500) for 5 consecutive years.
