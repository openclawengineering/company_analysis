# 株式会社メタテクノ / Metatechno Inc.

## Overview
Metatechno is a Kawasaki-based software development company with 40+ years of history, offering embedded systems, cloud/web/mobile development, AI/ML, and specialized solutions across printing, healthcare, education, construction, automotive, and IoT. They have a Sri Lanka offshore subsidiary (Metatechno Lanka) and develop their own products including worker scheduling app "MinSche," walking posture service "Alukuno," and bathtub monitoring system.

## Business Areas
- **Embedded Systems:** Firmware development, device drivers, communication software, OS porting (μITRON/Linux)
- **Cloud/Frontend:** AWS infrastructure, web apps, mobile apps (Android/iOS)
- **Expert Services:** Printing industry systems, medical cloud systems, digital textbooks, educational materials
- **Sustainable:** AI/ML, information security, offshore development (Sri Lanka), XR system development
- **Industries:** Printing (office printers, commercial printing DX), healthcare, education, construction/smart buildings, automotive, IoT

## Own Products
- **MinSche (みんスケ):** Worker scheduling/assignment smartphone app for construction and industries
- **Alukuno (アルクノ):** Walking posture analysis and improvement service (wellness management, skill succession)
- **Bathtub Monitoring System:** Sensors detect breathing, pulse, and movement; alerts family via smartphone
- **HotFax Series:** FAX system products
- **EMLinux:** Embedded Linux platform
