# 株式会社図研プリサイト / Zuken Presight Inc.

## Overview
Zuken Presight is a Yokohama-based PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) and knowledge management software company. A subsidiary of Zuken Inc. (a leading EDA/PCB design software company), Presight develops and sells products including Visual BOM, Knowledge Explorer, Qualityforce, and XVL Studio Z for the manufacturing industry.

## Key Details
- **Address:** 224-0032 Yokohama, Tsuzuki-ku, Koshigaya-Chuo 32-11
- **Website:** https://www.presight.co.jp/
- **Recruitment:** New graduate, mid-career

## Business Areas
- **PLM Solutions:** Visual BOM — BOM/drawing/3D data management for product lifecycle, recently added AI capabilities (v6.2, June 2026)
- **Project Management:** Visual BOM/Project — Gantt chart-based project management with Visual BOM integration
- **Knowledge Management:** Knowledge Explorer — AI-powered automatic knowledge discovery and management
- **Quality Management:** Qualityforce — Past failure/defect information utilization for recurrence prevention
- **3D Design Verification:** XVL Studio Z — Ultra-lightweight 3D model verification for electrical/mechanical hybrid design (distributed with Zuken)
