# 株式会社ハイマックス / HIMACS Ltd.

## Overview
HIMACS is a publicly traded Japanese IT company with 50+ years of history (50th anniversary celebrated). They provide system solutions, reform services, and middleware products including ASTERIA (data integration platform). Listed company with IR information, CSR/TCFD reporting, and quality management certifications.
