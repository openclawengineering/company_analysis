# CLINKS株式会社 / CLINKS Co., Ltd.

## Overview

AI-driven IT company (branded as "AI DRIVEN COMPANY") founded in 2002, headquartered in Tokyo. With 1,291 employees, CLINKS is one of the larger IT service providers in the list, specializing in generative AI development, DX support, system development, IT outsourcing, and cloud infrastructure — with a particular emphasis on Oracle engineering services.
