# メディノス 横浜営業所 / Medinos Yokohama Office

## Overview

Medinos is an Osaka-headquartered IT company established in 1998, providing software and hardware design/development as well as R&D services. The Yokohama office serves as a secondary location for development and R&D activities. Medinos has expanded into FA (Factory Automation) solutions and AI-powered image analysis services.

## Key Details

- **Founded:** April 1998 (as Yugen Kaisha; incorporated as Kabushiki Kaisha November 1999)
- **Headquarters (Osaka):** Osaka, Chuo-ku, Honmachi 2-5-7, MetLife Honmachi Square 9F
- **Yokohama Office:** 220-0004 Yokohama, Nishi-ku, Kita-saiwai 1-11-15, Yokohama ST Building 6F
- **Capital:** ¥30 million
- **President:** Koji Yamada (山田浩司)
- **Website:** https://medinos.co.jp/

## Business Areas

- Software development (embedded, applications, control systems)
- Hardware design and development
- R&D services
- FA (Factory Automation) system solutions
- Custom-made image analysis software (AI-powered)
- Online image analysis subscription service
