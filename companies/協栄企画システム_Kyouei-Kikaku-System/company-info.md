## History

- 1971: Kyouei Shoji starts printing business in Minato-ku, Tokyo
- 1973: Incorporated as Kyouei Shoji Co., Ltd.
- 1976: Renamed to Kyouei Kikaku Printing Co., Ltd.
- 2000: Started DTP operations; established system development division
- 2001: Opened Sagamihara office
- 2003: Sagamihara office spun off as Kyouei Kikaku System Co., Ltd.
- 2008: Moved HQ to Shinagawa, Tokyo
- 2012: Opened Kikuna office in Kohoku-ku, Yokohama
- 2017: Obtained general worker dispatch license
- 2022: Obtained ISO/IEC 27001 certification
