# 湘南技術センター株式会社 / Shonan Technology Center, Inc.

## Overview
Shonan Technology Center (STC) is a Yokohama-based multi-disciplinary technology company celebrating its 50th anniversary. It uniquely combines engineering services (mechanical/electrical/control design) with IT system integration, training/development, and environmental energy services.

- **Recruitment:** New graduate, mid-career, dispatched/contract

## Business Areas
- **Engineering Services:** Contract design (mechanical, electrical, control, residential equipment), technical staff dispatch, design/manufacturing, structural/thermal/fluid analysis, PCB defect identification/repair
- **System Integration:** On-premise and cloud system development, website construction, CAD customization, design automation, IoT (self-developed), AI utilization
- **Education/Development:** Instructor dispatch, implementation support, R&D, industry-academia collaboration
- **Environmental Energy:** Solar power generation, O&M, electricity supply agency
