# テクノスクエア株式会社 / Tec Square Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Tec Square is a Kawasaki-based technology company founded in 2002 by 4 engineers, now with 10x+ that workforce. They operate software, hardware, and consulting business divisions. The president is himself an engineer, creating a culture that values individual engineers. Certified as a Healthy Management Excellent Corporation (2026) and JIS Q 15001 (privacy standards).
