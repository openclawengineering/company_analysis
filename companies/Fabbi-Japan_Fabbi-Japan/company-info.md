# 株式会社Fabbi Japan / Fabbi Holdings

## Overview

Fabbi Holdings (Fabbi Japan) is a technology company operating from Tokyo with development centers in Vietnam. Selected as one of Japan's "Best Venture 100" in 2023, Fabbi provides end-to-end software development, AI solutions, CRM consulting, and blockchain development. The company has completed 350+ projects for 90+ clients with a reported 95%+ customer satisfaction rate.
