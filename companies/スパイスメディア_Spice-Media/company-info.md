# 有限会社スパイスメディア / Spice Media Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Spice Media is a small Yokohama-based web design and production company founded in 2004. They provide website creation, operation/maintenance, and print material design services. Celebrated 20th anniversary in 2024.

## Key Details
- **Address:** 241-0821 Yokohama, Asahi-ku, Futamatagawa 2-82-5-204
- **Website:** https://spice-media.com/
- **Founded:** June 25, 2004
- **President:** Mihoko Takazutsu

## Business Areas
- **Website Construction:** New website creation, renewal, design, and development from zero
- **Operation & Maintenance:** Content updates, planning proposals, and ongoing site maintenance
- **Print Design:** Business cards, flyers, brochures, envelopes, DM, and other print materials coordinated with web design
- **Consulting:** Internet-based information services

## Notable
- Founded 2004, celebrated 20th anniversary in 2024
- Member of Kanagawa Information Service Industry Association (KIA)
- Philosophy: "Create Together" — websites should be nurtured and promoted, not just built
- Banking: Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation
