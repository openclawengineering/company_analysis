# コーエイシステム / Koei System Inc. (KSI)

## Overview

Specialized system integrator founded in 1986, focused on disaster information, monitoring/control, and measurement systems for civil engineering, electric power, and telecommunications infrastructure. Part of the ID&E Holdings group since 2023.

## Business Areas

### System Development
- Disaster information systems
- Substation monitoring & control systems
- Dam monitoring systems
- Telecommunications equipment management systems
- ICT, monitoring, control, and measurement systems

### System Operation & Maintenance
- Ongoing operation and maintenance support for developed systems

## Corporate History

- 1986: Founded in Yokohama (Kohoku-ku)
- 1991: Expanded to general market; registered for worker dispatch
- 2001: Capital increased to ¥30M
- 2003: Capital increased to ¥90M; relocated to Tokyo (Chiyoda-ku)
- 2009: Obtained ISO/IEC 27001 certification
- 2012: Became wholly-owned subsidiary of 日本工営 (Nihon Koei)
- 2023: Transferred to ID&E Holdings group
