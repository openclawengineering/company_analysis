# モトミ株式会社 / Motomi Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Motomi is a Yokohama-based IT company operating closely with the local community while also expanding domestically and internationally. They offer software development, electronic product R&D support, comprehensive computer system design/development, large-scale database construction, bioinformatics data processing, DNA analysis system R&D, and large-scale nucleotide sequence analysis.
