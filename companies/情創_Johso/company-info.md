# 株式会社情創 / Johso Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Johso Co., Ltd. is a Tokyo-based software development company that has shifted its focus toward AI utilization while maintaining its manufacturing industry roots. Celebrated its 25th anniversary in August 2025. Operates from both Tokyo and Yokohama offices.

- **Recruitment:** New graduate, mid-career, internship, dispatched/contract

## Business Areas
- **Custom Software Development:** Wide-ranging project development with specialized knowledge in manufacturing
- **Manufacturing Industry Solutions:** Production line efficiency, inventory management, supply chain optimization
- **Education Sector Solutions:** DX solutions for education using open-source technology
- **AI & Trend Technology Solutions:** Business efficiency tools, modern frameworks, ICT-based support services
- **Network/Communication Systems**
- **Portal/Site Operation, SES, ASP Services**

## Notable
- Founded in 2000, celebrated 25th anniversary in 2025
- Originally manufacturing-focused, now emphasizing AI utilization
- "Gorikai" program — young engineers develop small-scale projects in Java/Python at affordable rates for hands-on experience
- Philosophy: "Coexistence and co-prosperity"
- Active business partner recruitment program
