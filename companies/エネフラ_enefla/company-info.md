## Business Lines

enefla is a startup focused on carbon neutrality through EV charging infrastructure:

- **EV Charger Sales & Installation** — Normal chargers and rapid chargers for homes, workplaces, and commercial facilities
- **EV Charging Billing System "enefla Charge"** — Self-developed payment system for EV chargers (QR-code based, no membership required)
- **V2H (Vehicle-to-Home) Sales & Installation**
- **Subsidy Application Support** — Assisting customers with national and local government subsidies for EV charger installation

## Key Differentiators

- One-stop service: from proposal to site survey, quotation, subsidy application, and installation
- Group company with electrical equipment firm ensures reliable construction quality
- Self-developed billing system (no third-party dependency)
- Campaign pricing: base fee 2,970 JPY/month + 10% payment processing fee
