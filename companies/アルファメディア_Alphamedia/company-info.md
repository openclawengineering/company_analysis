## Notes

Founded in 1992, Alphamedia Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive IT company based in Kawasaki, Kanagawa, with offices in Kobe and Nagoya. The company offers hardware development, software development, web system/site development, IT education (through Fujitsu Open College partnership), and staff dispatch services. They hold Privacy Mark certification and Kanagawa/Kawasaki city enterprise certifications. Their related company, Alphamedia Technology, is based in Kobe.
