## Business Lines

Fnet is an independent software development company based in Yokohama, operating for over 30 years:

- **Web System Development** — Financial industry web systems, digital appliance embedded control systems
- **Embedded Software Development** — Automotive and digital electronics
- **Software Engineering Services (SES)** — Engineer dispatch (licensed: 派14-300475)
- **Broad-domain Development** — From financial web systems to digital home appliance embedded control systems

## Key Differentiators

- "Exploration-type" engineering group — not just "proposal-type" but works alongside clients to discover root causes and solutions
- Close client relationships built on trust
- Over 30 years of accumulated knowledge and experience
- ISO 27001 certified for information security
- Member of Kanagawa Information Services Industry Association (KIA)
