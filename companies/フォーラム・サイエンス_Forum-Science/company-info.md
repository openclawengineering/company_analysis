# 株式会社フォーラム・サイエンス / Forum Science

## Overview

Forum Science is a Tokyo-based IT services company founded in December 1996. Specializing in both mainframe (COBOL) and open-system development, the company serves financial institutions, manufacturing firms, NTT, and government agencies. Forum Science also provides infrastructure construction, BPO services, and IT staffing dispatch.

## Business Areas

- **Web system development** — Java and web technology-based application planning, development, and operations
- **Mainframe systems** — COBOL-based development for banking, securities, insurance, and credit card systems
- **Infrastructure construction** — network and server monitoring, operations management
- **BPO services** — technical support, testing/verification, system maintenance, help desk

## Development Track Record

**Mainframe systems:**
- Banking: core accounting, information systems, network systems
- Insurance: policy management, premium management, agent management
- Securities: information and accounting systems
- Manufacturing: process management, production management
- NTT: billing systems, customer management

**Open systems:**
- Banking: loan management, lease management
- Publishing: product management, sales management
- Medical: receipt processing, pharmaceutical purchasing
- E-commerce: screening systems, real estate systems
