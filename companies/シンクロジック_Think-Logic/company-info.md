# 株式会社シンクロジック / Think Logic Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Think Logic is a Yokohama-based game development company headquartered in Yokohama Landmark Tower. They specialize in online game development for PC, smartphone, and browser platforms, with an impressive portfolio including titles like My Hero Academia: Ultra Rumble, Tekken 8, Fate/Grand Order, Granblue Fantasy: Relink, and Dragon Ball: The Breakers. Also offers web/app development and UI/UX design.
