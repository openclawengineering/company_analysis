## Business Lines

### Contract System Development
- IoT system development (manufacturing to service industries)
- Big data / AI analysis for business DX
- Image and video analysis solutions
- IT-based business efficiency improvement

### Product Development
- **Steach** — AI-powered presentation/speaking analysis app that evaluates eye contact, posture, speaking speed, and speech content from smartphone/PC recordings. Used for interview practice and communication training.
- **Uniform Resale Prevention AI Engine** — Joint patent with Kawasaki Frontale (J-League) for detecting unauthorized merchandise resale
- **Job-hunting AI** — Co-developed with JICK Inc.

### SES (System Engineering Services)
- Engineer dispatch services from Yokohama to nationwide
- Expanding to Shonan and Ken-o (south-central Kanagawa) areas
- Focus on matching skills and personality, not just technical fit
