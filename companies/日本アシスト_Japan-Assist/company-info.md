# 株式会社日本アシスト / Japan Assist Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Japan Assist is a Yokohama-based ESG company providing IT solutions, device kitting/repair services, and personnel dispatch. With 170 engineers and 20+ years of repair experience (1,000万+ devices repaired), they offer a full lifecycle approach — from system design/development through device configuration, repair, refurbishment, and reuse. They are an official partner of Yokohama Excellence basketball club.

## Business Areas
- **System Solutions:** System design/development, SES, contracted development, on-site assignments, Atlassian product sales/operation/maintenance, software testing
- **PC/Smartphone Kitting:** Patent-holding proprietary RPA tools for device configuration; carrier/agency/manufacturer back-office support; MDM operation outsourcing
- **Repair Services:** Smartphone, PC, home appliance, and robot repair (20+ years experience, 10 million+ devices)
- **Rework/Grading/Terminal Buyback:** Device refurbishment services
- **Crushing/Data Erasure:** Secure data destruction
- **Light Worker Dispatch:** Personnel dispatch services
- **Facility Management:** Facility support services
