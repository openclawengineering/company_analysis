# ネットリサーチ株式会社 / Net Research Corp. (NRC)

## Overview
Net Research is a Kawasaki-based IT company with a dual focus: professional embedded/web software development and children's programming education. They operate the "Tamiya Robot School" programming classrooms and emphasize "customer first" philosophy with thorough reporting/communication.

## Business Areas
- **Embedded Systems Development:** Android apps, communication protocol conversion, various firmware development
- **Web System Development:** Point management systems, material management systems for enterprises/factories
- **FA Systems (Production Automation):** Man-machine interfaces, handlers, efficient data collection/control mechanisms
- **Tamiya Robot School:** Children's programming education through robot assembly and coding (武蔵小杉教室 and others)
- **IT Education:** Programming education for both employees and children

## Notable
- Dual business model: enterprise IT + children's education
- Operates Tamiya Robot School franchise locations
- Philosophy: "Customer first" with thorough reporting, communication, and consulting
- Focus on developing "people who will be active in the future" through programming education
