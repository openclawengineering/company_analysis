# 日本テクノストラクチャア株式会社 / Nihon Technostructure (TESCO)

## Overview

Nihon Technostructure (TESCO) is a major independent Japanese software company founded in 1975, celebrating its 50th anniversary in 2025. With 277 employees and 7 offices across Japan, TESCO develops systems that support critical social infrastructure — from security and healthcare to cashless payments, IoT, automotive systems, and public infrastructure. The company is a trusted partner to major manufacturers including Panasonic, Toshiba, Hitachi, and Canon.

## Business Areas

- **Security systems** — video surveillance, license plate recognition, image enhancement, crime prevention
- **Healthcare systems** — ultrasound diagnostics, electronic medicine notebooks, electronic medical records, dialysis equipment
- **Cashless systems** — multi-payment terminals, e-money chargers, KIOSK terminals
- **SCM** — warehouse operations, logistics/delivery management, store inventory management
- **Public infrastructure** — disaster prevention, ETC, water management, broadcasting
- **IoT systems** — residential/non-residential IoT, edge/cloud/network computing
- **Automotive systems** — safety/autonomous driving, digital meters, body control
- **Digital manufacturing** — production management, ERP, AI/IoT
- **IT services** — verification, PMO support, maintenance/operations
