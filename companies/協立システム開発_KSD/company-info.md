# 協立システム開発 / KyoRitsu System Development (KSD)

## Overview

Long-established IT services company founded in 1979, based in Kawasaki, Kanagawa. Provides comprehensive computer-related services including system development, operation, and maintenance across production management, public sector, medical, and embedded systems.

- **Website:** https://www.ksdnet.co.jp/
- **Address:** 210-0006 Kawasaki, Kawasaki-ku, Sunago 1-7-1, DK KAWASAKI Bld. 6F
- **Phone:** 044-210-5678
- **Founded:** 1979
- **Corporate Motto:** 「社は人なり、人は和なり」 ("The company is its people; people are harmony")

## Business Areas

- Production management system development, operation & maintenance
- Public sector system development, operation & maintenance
- Open systems development
- Medical system development & implementation
- Embedded system development

## Recent Developments

- May 2025: Became a wholly-owned subsidiary of 協立ホールディングス株式会社 (Kyoritsu Holdings Co., Ltd.)
- March 2026: Started recruiting for FY2027 graduates
- July 2023: Change of representative director
