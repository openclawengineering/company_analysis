# 株式会社日本インテリジェントビジネス / Japan Intelligent Business Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Japan Intelligent Business (JIB) is a Yokosuka-based IT company celebrating its 30th anniversary. With a primary focus on employee welfare, they provide software development and dispatch services. Their motto is "work seriously, relax amicably." They have Privacy Mark certification and are members of the Kanagawa Information Service Industry Association and YRP Information Industry Cooperative.
