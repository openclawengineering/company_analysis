# 日本データスキル株式会社 / Nihon Data Skill Co., Ltd. (NDS)

## Overview
Nihon Data Skill (NDS) is a Yokohama-based IT solution company founded in 1974 (50+ years of history). They provide total IT system development — from requirements definition through design, development, operation, and maintenance — specializing in public sector, financial, manufacturing, and embedded control systems. They also offer package system implementation support.
