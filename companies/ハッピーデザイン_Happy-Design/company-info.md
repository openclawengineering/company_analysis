# 株式会社ハッピーデザイン / HAPPY DESIGN Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Happy Design is a Tokyo-based IT company founded in 2012, providing system development, IT infrastructure construction, help desk/administrative support, PC/network equipment kitting, and system engineering services. With ~60 employees, they leverage cloud, big data, AI, and IoT technologies. They emphasize "speed and power that won't lose to the times" and green management through various green space projects.
