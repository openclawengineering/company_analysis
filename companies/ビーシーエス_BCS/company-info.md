# 株式会社ビーシーエス / Business Continuity Systems Inc. (BCS)

## Overview
BCS (Business Continuity Systems) is an IT infrastructure company focused on ensuring business continuity for clients. They provide three solution pillars: infrastructure design/construction, operation management, and security (including vulnerability diagnosis and source code analysis). Privacy Mark certified.

## Key Details
- **Website:** https://www.biz-cs.com/ (also http://www.biz-cs.com/)
- **Privacy Mark:** Certified (registered at privacymark.jp)

## Business Areas
- **Infrastructure Design & Construction:** Network devices, servers, middleware — optimal network infrastructure tailored to client needs
- **Operation Management:** Comprehensive operation management systems (Tivoli, JP1) — design, implementation, testing, operation; ITIL consulting available
- **Security:** Vulnerability diagnosis from attacker's perspective (penetration testing), source code diagnosis
- **Business Continuity:** Core philosophy — ensuring high-level customer satisfaction in IT system continuity
