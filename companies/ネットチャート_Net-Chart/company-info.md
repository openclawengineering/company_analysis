# ネットチャート株式会社 / Net Chart Japan Inc. (NCJ)

## Overview
Net Chart Japan is a Yokohama-based ICT infrastructure solution provider and an IIJ group company. They offer one-stop solutions spanning network construction, security, systems, facility management, BCP, healthcare, IoT, and free engineer dispatch. They describe themselves as creating "future infrastructure through network construction."

## Business Areas
- **Network & Security:** LAN cabling, internet connectivity, security construction for enterprises, universities, and government
- **System & Support:** System development and post-deployment operation/maintenance
- **Facility:** Office relocation support, tenant/furniture procurement, construction, waste disposal, IT equipment disposal
- **BCP Solutions:** Cloud backup for disaster recovery, lightning protection for data centers
- **Medical/Healthcare:** High-speed diagnostic imaging platforms, medical collaboration networks
- **Free Engineer Dispatch:** Career-matched project placement (primarily IIJ Prime-listed company projects)
- **IoT Development:** End-to-end IoT solution development
