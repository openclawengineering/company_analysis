# 株式会社スカイリミット / Sky Limit Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Sky Limit is a small Yokohama-based IT company founded in 2013, specializing in manufacturing industry business systems. They offer full lifecycle development from planning to maintenance, with ERP package introduction and custom development as their core business. The company has been profitable every year since founding and has achieved consistent growth.

- **Website:** http://www.sky-limit.co.jp/
- **Founded:** November 2013
- **Capital:** 7 million yen
- **President:** Kenji Takagi
- **Employees:** 11 full-time, 15 total active

## Business Areas
- **Custom Software Development:** Planning, design, development, and maintenance of business systems (primarily for manufacturing industry)
- **ERP Package Introduction:** Production management package implementation and customization
- **System Operation & Maintenance:** Support for 30+ systems (their distinguishing feature)
- **SES/Technical Staff Dispatch:** Technology provision services
