# 株式会社パルディアス / Paldies Inc.

## Overview
Paldies is a small Tokyo-based IT company providing system development and engineer dispatch services. The company name comes from Latvian "Paldies" meaning "thank you," reflecting their philosophy of "always acting with a heart of gratitude." They hold a general worker dispatch license.
