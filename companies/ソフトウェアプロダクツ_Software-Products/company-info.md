# 株式会社ソフトウェアプロダクツ / Software Products Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Software Products is a Yokohama-based system and software development company with approximately 30 years of track record. They specialize in business applications, automatic control systems, monitoring/control solutions (especially for water infrastructure), and IoT/AI-enabled systems. ISO 9001:2015 certified.

## Business Areas
- **Monitoring & Control Solutions:** Centralized monitoring of distributed water facilities and water quality meters on Windows-based open systems with web operation capability
- **Business Applications:** Custom business software development
- **Automatic Control Systems:** Manufacturing line control systems (PA/FA: Process Automation / Factory Automation)
- **IoT/AI Systems:** Visualization (web/mobile), Business Intelligence, AI utilization
- **Infrastructure Design & Construction:** Network and server infrastructure
