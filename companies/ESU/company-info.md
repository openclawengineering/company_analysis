## Notes

Founded in 1991, ESU Inc. is a Yokohama-based company providing network/server infrastructure design, implementation, and operation services. Their approach emphasizes post-implementation consideration, including lifecycle operation and continuous improvement. The company also operates staff dispatch and real estate businesses.
