# トライシステム株式会社 / Tri System Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Tri System is a Kawasaki-based software and system development company offering system integration, software development, system support, and consulting services. They maintain a Privacy Mark certification and describe themselves as having an at-home (アットホーム) company culture — "a good kind of not-so-typical IT company."
