## Business Lines

Ftime is an "Infrastructure System Integration Service" company and a **Platinum Partner** of Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) Japan:

- **HPE Product Sales & Support** — Enterprise servers, storage, networking (Aruba)
- **System Integration** — Infrastructure design, construction, and migration
- **SimpliVity** — Hyper-converged infrastructure solutions
- **Aruba Networking** — Wi-Fi and network security solutions
- **3PAR/StoreServ** — Enterprise storage solutions
- **Cloud Services** — Microsoft solutions, DX support
- **Technical Training & Seminars** — Regular executive seminars and technical training events nationwide

## Key Differentiators

- 30+ years of Unix and open system expertise
- Highest-tier HPE Platinum Partner
- 5 offices nationwide (Tokyo, Osaka, Nagoya, Fukuoka, Sendai)
- ISO 14001 certified
- Privacy Mark certified
- Active seminar/training program with executive events in resort locations
