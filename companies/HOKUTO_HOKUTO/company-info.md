# 株式会社HOKUTO / HOKUTO JAPAN Co., Ltd.

## Overview
HOKUTO is a Kanagawa-based "problem-solving EMS" (Electronics Manufacturing Services) company providing one-stop ODM/EMS services from planning and specification through electrical, housing, and mechanism design, prototyping, mass production, and sales. They also manufacture measuring instruments (strain gauge indicators) and amplifiers. They have offices in Yokohama and Yamagata.

## Business Areas
- **OEM/EMS Services:** Full one-stop from planning/specification → electrical design → housing design → mechanism design → mass prototype → mass production → sales
- **ODM Development:** Complete original development and custom products using commercial parts
- **Measuring Instruments:** Strain gauge indicators and amplifiers
- **Case Studies:** Wet towel warming/cooling cabinets, remote-controlled IH cooktops, industrial harness checkers
