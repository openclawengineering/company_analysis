## Business Lines

NTT TechnoCross is a "Software Leading Company" that creates new value by combining NTT laboratory research results with domestic and international technologies. Key focus areas:

- **Customer Experience (CX)** — Voice processing, AI, data analytics for CX improvement
- **Employee Experience (EX)** — Supporting diverse workstyles in an aging society
- **Digital Transformation (DX)** — DX acceleration while ensuring security and internal controls
- **Healthcare & Medical ICT** — Health management, medical evolution, community care
- **Smart Agriculture** — AI/IoT for labor-saving and high-quality production
- **Security** — 30+ years of security products and services
- **AI** — Leveraging AI to help enterprises unlock value from massive information
- **Media** — Supporting corporate activities in media society
- **Communication** — Simple, secure communication services
- **Cloud** — High-quality cloud solutions
- **IOWN Initiative** — Contributing to NTT's next-gen communication infrastructure vision (4D digital, medical ICT, remote world platforms)
