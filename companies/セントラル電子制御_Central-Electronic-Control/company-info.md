# セントラル電子制御株式会社 / Central Electronic Control Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Central Electronic Control is a Kawasaki-based comprehensive system manufacturer handling everything from hardware/software development to production. A member of the Futaba Group (a major Japanese electronics manufacturer), the company develops products across mobile solutions, home security, POS/payment terminals, automotive equipment, station broadcasting systems, industrial drones, and inspection equipment.

## Business Areas
- **Mobile Solutions:** Mobile technology and communication control development (smartphones, tablets)
- **Home Security Equipment:** Intercoms, security cameras, and related devices
- **POS/Payment Terminals:** Cash register and payment processing terminals
- **Automotive Equipment:** Car navigation, drive recorders
- **Station Broadcasting Systems:** Automatic station announcement systems
- **Inspection Equipment & Jigs:** Custom inspection tools for development and production sites
- **Industrial Drones:** Inspection and disaster response drones with IT and communication control technology
- **Research Equipment:** Custom experimental apparatus for research institutions
- **Production:** Assembly and manufacturing services (large to mobile, mass production to custom one-offs)
