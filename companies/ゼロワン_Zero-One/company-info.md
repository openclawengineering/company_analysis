# 株式会社ゼロワン / Zero One Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Zero One is a Yokohama-based embedded systems development company specializing in control and embedded system development. Their motto is "make the idea to product." The company operates three business lines: SES (System Engineering Services), contracted development, and OEM/ODM.

## Key Details
- **Address:** 224-0041 Yokohama, Tsuzuki-ku, Nakachodai 1-32-10
- **Website:** http://www.zerone-01.co.jp/
- **Recruitment:** New graduate, mid-career, internship, dispatched/contract

## Business Areas
- **Embedded Systems Development:** Control systems and embedded software development
- **SES Business:** Technical staff dispatch
- **Contracted Development:** Custom development projects
- **OEM/ODM Business:** Original equipment/product design and manufacturing

## Notable
- Corporate philosophy centered on "Japan's world-class manufacturing"
- Many female engineers on staff (noted in recruitment messaging)
- Welcomes inexperienced candidates and provides training from scratch
- Focus on understanding and pursuing customer needs
