# 日本ノアーズ株式会社 / Nihon Noah's Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Nihon Noah's (NOAH'S) is a Yokohama-based software development company founded in 1998. They develop software across microcomputer, open systems, and mainframe environments, and also build and operate servers. They run an iPhone app development business, an e-book publishing service, and sell their own package product "Baka-Ure Tenpo" (online shop software). At-home company culture with monthly all-staff strategy meetings.
