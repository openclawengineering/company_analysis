# 株式会社ソフト 東京支社 / Soft Co., Ltd. (Tokyo Branch)

## Overview
Soft Co., Ltd. is a Toyama-headquartered independent software company with a Tokyo branch in Yokohama. Founded over 43 years ago, they offer one-stop IT solutions from consulting through development to maintenance. With 420 development projects and 91 developers, they emphasize "soft skills" alongside technical expertise. Registered as a "Toyama DX Partner."
