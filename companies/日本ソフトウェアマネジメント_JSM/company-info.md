# 日本ソフトウェアマネジメント株式会社 / Japan Software Management Co., Ltd. (JSM)

## Overview
Japan Software Management is a Yokohama-based software company specializing in local government (municipal) administrative systems. Their flagship school lunch fee management system holds 80%+ market share among government-ordinance-designated cities that have implemented public accounting for school lunch fees (14 of 17 cities). They also develop systems for school fee collection, allergy management, hospital reception, and DNA/bioinformatics analysis. ISO 9001 and ISMS (ISO 27001) certified.
