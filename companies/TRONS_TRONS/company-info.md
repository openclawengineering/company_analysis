# 株式会社TRONS / TRONS

## Overview

TRONS is a small, flexible IT company founded in May 2015 in Yokohama. Originally focused on system development, TRONS expanded into content development in 2019, including game development planning and business tool creation. The company operates with a lean team and emphasizes agility and close client relationships.
