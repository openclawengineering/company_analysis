# 株式会社ビジネスランド / Business Research and Development Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Business Land is a Kawasaki-based IT company that celebrated its 40th anniversary. They propose solutions for all business and IT-related problems with the motto "reliable technology and free thinking — proposing new lifestyles." The company changed leadership in 2019 with Yuichi Onishi becoming president.
