## Business Lines

### Contract Software Development
OIS handles in-house contracted projects across multiple domains:

- **Embedded Technology** — Digital multi-function devices (MFPs), parking systems, in-vehicle equipment systems
- **Next-Generation Services (IoT / AI / Cloud)** — AI-assisted development, IoT-related systems
- **Mobile Applications** — iOS and Android app development
- **Web / Desktop Applications** — Windows, Linux, and web application development
- **Medical Systems** — Healthcare and medical device software

### Own Products
- **ReDois** — WebRTC-based platform that connects everything in real time
- **Szia** — Web conference / seminar system built on WebRTC middleware
