# 日本テクノストラクチャア 新横浜営業所 / Nihon Technostructure (TESCO) Shin-Yokohama Office

## Overview

Nihon Technostructure (commonly known as TESCO) is a major Japanese IT systems developer founded in 1975. Headquartered in Fuchu, Tokyo, the company develops application-centric computer software systems across a wide range of domains. TESCO serves as a long-trusted partner to major manufacturers including Panasonic, Toshiba, and Hitachi, contributing to critical social infrastructure development.

## Business Domains

- **Security:** Video surveillance, license plate recognition, image enhancement, crime prevention
- **Healthcare:** Ultrasound diagnostics, electronic medication notebooks, electronic medical records, dialysis equipment
- **Cashless:** Multi-payment terminals, e-money charging machines, KIOSK terminals
- **SCM:** Warehouse operations management, logistics/delivery management, store merchandise management
- **Public Infrastructure:** Disaster prevention, ETC, water management, broadcasting
- **IoT Systems:** Residential/non-residential IoT, edge/cloud/network computing
- **Automotive:** Safety/autonomous driving, digital meters, body control
- **Digital Manufacturing:** Production management, ERP, AI/IoT
- **IT Services:** Verification, PMO support, maintenance/operation
