# 株式会社ティーエスアールパブリック / TSR Public Co., Ltd.

## Overview
TSR Public is a Tokyo-based SI (System Integration) and technical staff dispatch company. A group company of TSR Inc., they provide total SI services from system planning through construction and post-implementation maintenance, technical staff dispatch, and hardware sales (primarily Fujitsu). Certified as a "Healthy Management Excellent Corporation" since 2020.

## Business Areas
- **SI Services:** System planning, design, construction, and post-implementation maintenance/operation
- **Technical Staff Dispatch:** Providing engineers with advanced technology skills to client sites
