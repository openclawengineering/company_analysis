## Business Overview

I-NET Data Service Corp. is an IT-related service company that supports corporate back-office operations through data entry, scanning, and kitting operations. With strengths in accuracy and carefulness, the company approaches each task sincerely and provides high-quality services.

As a special subsidiary of I-NET Co., Ltd., the company actively promotes the employment of persons with disabilities and creates a workplace where diverse personnel can demonstrate their respective strengths. The company is also committed to providing an environment where employees can grow for a long time with peace of mind, as well as working on health management.

## Products & Services

### 1. Data Entry (Keypunch)
Input operations converting customer-provided paper materials such as purchase and sales ledgers, customer information, and various questionnaires into electronic data accurately. Using dedicated entry machines, Excel, or dedicated software, the company provides rapid and careful response, making data management and analysis easier.

### 2. Scanning
Converts customer-provided documents and materials into high-quality digital data from paper documents. Facilitates document sharing, helps with organization and storage, and supports efficient information management.

**Requirements to Confirm:**
- Document size
- File unit
- File resolution
- Document delivery method
- Delivery format (file format, resolution)
- Delivery method for deliverables

### 3. Business Card Production
Handles everything from original business card creation according to customer requests to printing and delivery. Carefully checks design and content to propose business cards that are easy to read and trustworthy, matching the intended use and preferences. Carries out creation and delivery quickly and smoothly to deliver high-quality business cards suitable for business scenes.

**Requirements to Confirm:**
- Layout
- Font
- Logo
- Color
- Content to be published
- Proofreading confirmation method
- Delivery method for deliverables

### 4. Kitting/Mailing Service
Performs simple assembly and combination of parts and materials provided by customers, followed by envelope insertion and sealing, and finally shipping. Supports preparation of customer products and materials.

### 5. Magnetic Erasure & Physical Destruction Service
Provides two services for safely and reliably erasing/destroying data from hard drives removed from PCs and servers provided by customers:

- **Magnetic Erasure Service:** Using dedicated magnetic erasure equipment to instantaneously apply a strong magnetic field to the hard drive, erasing data in an instant
- **Physical Destruction Service:** Physically destroying the hard drive by punching holes in it, making data reading impossible

Both services are completed within the company's facilities, minimizing the risk of data leakage.

## Company Philosophy & Profile

I-NET Data Service Corp. is a member of the I-NET Group and primarily contracts relatively standardized work. The company aims to further accumulate knowledge and expertise related to the employment of persons with disabilities and strive to create employment opportunities so that persons with disabilities can fully demonstrate their abilities.
