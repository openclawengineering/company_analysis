## Notes

And One Software Engineering was founded in 2019 with the mission of "responding with technology and continuing to evolve." The company has achieved ISO/IEC 27001 certification and holds Kawasaki City recognitions for SDGs (Gold Partner) and workplace reform. Their representative product exhibited at the Sports Business Industry Exhibition is the "ArenaScoreDisplaySystem." The company offers comprehensive software development, web development, system maintenance, staff dispatch, and IT engineer training services.
