# 株式会社メリウステック / Melius Tech Inc.

## Overview
Melius Tech is a Yokohama-based IT solutions startup providing system development and network/server construction services. They specialize in PLM (Product Lifecycle Management), MES (Manufacturing Execution System), and SCM (Supply Chain Management) system construction for manufacturing clients. Their philosophy: "Creating the future with technical ability and human ability." Currently recruiting startup members.
