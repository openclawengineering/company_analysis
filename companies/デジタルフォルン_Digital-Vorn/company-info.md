# 株式会社デジタルフォルン / Digital Vorn Co., Ltd.

## Overview
Digital Vorn is a Tokyo-headquartered global system solution provider founded in 1961 as a mechanical design company. Today they offer a broad range of services spanning both "monozukuri" (manufacturing) — mechanical design, circuit design, embedded software — and digital services — SaaS-based data analysis, RPA, DX, application development, and pharmaceutical industry SFE support. Known for their one-person-per-project approach across all phases.

## Business Areas
- **Pharmaceutical SFE Support:** Data-driven sales force effectiveness for the pharmaceutical industry
- **DX/Digitalization Support:** RPA implementation, digital tool selection, business automation
- **Data Analytics/Data Platform:** One-stop support from data infrastructure construction to reporting
- **System Development:** Cloud services, application development, infrastructure design
- **Mechanical/Circuit Design/Embedded Software:** From mechanical design through mechatronics support
- **Other Services:** Security and marketing support
