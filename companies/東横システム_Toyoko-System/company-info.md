# 東横システム株式会社 / Toyoko System Corporation

## Overview
Toyoko System is an independent Tokyo-based software development company founded in 1982. With approximately 150 employees and 43 consecutive years of profitable operations, they develop software across multiple domains from Windows/Mac/Linux applications to embedded systems for various devices. Their management philosophy centers on being a "top-tier subcontractor for top-tier enterprises" — valuing encounters and keeping promises.

## Business Areas
- **Application Software Development:** Windows, Mac, and Linux application development (upstream design through implementation)
- **Embedded Software:** Software for various devices and equipment
- **Network/Communication Systems:** Communication-related development
- **TV Broadcasting Systems:** Supporting daily television program delivery
- **Medical Systems:** Software for medical devices (human-trusted manufacturing)
- **Photography/Image Systems:** Photo storage and memory preservation solutions
- **Video/Projection Systems:** Video messaging and projection technology
- **Software Testing/Verification:** Software quality assurance services
- **IT Support Services:** Computer lifestyle support for connected society
