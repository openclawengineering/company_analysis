# 株式会社匠技術研究所 / Takumi Gijutsu Kenkyujo

## Overview
Takumi Gijutsu Kenkyujo (Takumi Technical Research Institute) is a Kawasaki-based IT infrastructure and consulting company with four core business pillars: "Connect" (network design), "Build" (AI/IoT/server/cloud), "Operate" (system monitoring and operation), and "Grow" (technical education). They also run the Takumi Technical Seminar program and a medical/dental network division.

## Business Areas
- **Network Design & Construction:** Enterprise communication network planning and building
- **System Infrastructure:** Server, cloud, and IoT system design and construction
- **System Operation:** Network/cloud server monitoring, distributed backup services
- **Technical Consulting:** IT consulting services
- **Technical Education (匠技術研修):** Yamaha router seminars and IT training programs (yamaharouterseminar.com)
- **Medical/Dental Network:** Network services for medical and dental institutions
- **Information Security:** SPAM filtering and security solutions
