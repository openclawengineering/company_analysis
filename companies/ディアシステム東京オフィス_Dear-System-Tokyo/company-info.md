# ディアシステム 東京オフィス / Dear System Tokyo Office

## Overview

Dear System is an Osaka-headquartered IT company founded in 1985. The company provides system development, web production, consulting, and outsourcing services across a wide range of industries. With 98 employees and offices in Osaka, Kyoto, and Tokyo, Dear System has built a broad client base including NEC, NTT Data, Kubota, and various local governments.
