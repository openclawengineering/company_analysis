# 日産車体コンピュータサービス株式会社 / Nissan Shatai Computer Service Co., Ltd. (NCS)

## Overview
NCS is an IT subsidiary of the Nissan group that supports global automotive manufacturing through IT technology. They develop and maintain production management systems that form the backbone of automobile production. With offices in Kanagawa, Okinawa, Northern Kanto, and Northern Kyushu, they combine advanced IT with regional support.
