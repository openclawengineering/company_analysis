# ディアシステム株式会社 / Dear System

## Overview

Dear System is an Osaka-based IT company founded in 1985 with nearly 40 years of history. The company provides system development, web production, and IT consulting services across multiple offices in Osaka, Kyoto, and Tokyo. With 98 employees, Dear System has earned a reputation for reliable enterprise software development and was recognized with IPA's "SECURITY ACTION Two Stars" certification in 2025.
