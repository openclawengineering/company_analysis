# 株式会社宮川製作所 / MSK Technologies Co., Ltd. (Miyakawa Seisakusho)

## Overview
MSK Technologies (Miyakawa Seisakusho) is an 85-year-old Japanese technology manufacturer with headquarters in Meguro, Tokyo and a Yokohama office. Originally founded in 1939 as a producer of telephone microphones and coils, they now develop cutting-edge solutions including RFID (rafis), facial recognition, environmental monitoring, industrial LTE routers, and audio/video products. Their slogan: "Shaping the future of society with connecting technology."

- **Environmental Monitoring:** F&M-Portal — power, temperature, humidity monitoring from small to large scale
- **Industrial LTE Routers:** efPORT series — M2M/IoT communication devices
