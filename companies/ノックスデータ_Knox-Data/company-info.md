# ノックスデータ株式会社 / Knox Data Corp.

## Overview
Knox Data is a software house specializing in network application development, with the philosophy "Footwork × Teamwork × Network." They operate across embedded systems (imaging/automotive), space/defense, web/cloud systems, FA, and public systems. Part of the ISB Group. Has offices including Nagoya.

## Key Details
- **Website:** https://www.knox.co.jp/
- **Recruitment:** New graduate, mid-career

## Business Areas
- **Embedded Systems:** Software development for imaging equipment and automotive devices (firmware through applications)
- **Space/Defense:** Defense system development and space-related systems (cutting-edge field)
- **Web/Cloud Systems:** Network-based transmission/monitoring systems, full-stack (frontend to backend)
- **FA & Public Systems:** Factory automation and public sector systems
