# 株式会社PE-BANK / PE-BANK Inc.

## Overview
PE-BANK is a major IT human resources company founded in 1989 (36+ years), operating nationwide with 12 offices. They provide four core services: freelance IT engineer agent, IT engineer dispatch/referral, IT talent education (PE-BANK College), and welfare benefits for IT professionals. Their mission is to "create a society where the social status of freelance IT engineers is further elevated."

## Key Details
- **Website:** https://pe-bank.co.jp/
- **Founded:** 1989 (36+ years)
- **Offices:** 12 locations nationwide
- **Service sites:** pe-bank.jp (freelance), career.pe-bank.jp (dispatch), pebank-college.jp (education), splus.pe-bank.jp (welfare)

## Business Areas
- **Freelance IT Engineer Agent:** Project matching for freelance engineers (pe-bank.jp)
- **IT Engineer Dispatch/Referral:** Staffing services for IT professionals (career.pe-bank.jp)
- **IT Talent Education:** Corporate training via PE-BANK College (pebank-college.jp)
- **Seminar Business:** Seminars for freelancers and IT engineers
- **Welfare Benefits:** Support Plus benefits service for IT professionals (splus.pe-bank.jp)
