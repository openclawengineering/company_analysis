# 株式会社ネオ・システム / NEO SYSTEM

## Overview

NEO SYSTEM is a Tokyo-based IT company established in January 1995. With a focus on system development, IT infrastructure construction, and design services, the company serves clients across the Tokyo area. NEO SYSTEM also holds a staffing dispatch license and emphasizes internal employee development and motivation.
