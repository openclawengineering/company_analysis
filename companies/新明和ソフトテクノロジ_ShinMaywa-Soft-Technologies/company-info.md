# 新明和ソフトテクノロジ株式会社 / ShinMaywa Soft Technologies, Ltd.

## Overview
ShinMaywa Soft Technologies is an IT company and member of the ShinMaywa Group (formerly ShinMaywa Industries). Founded in 1986 as a spinoff from ShinMaywa Industries, it serves as the group's sole IT enterprise while also providing solutions to external manufacturing clients. Specializes in manufacturing industry systems, IoT, CAD, security, and mobile solutions.
