# 株式会社メディノス / Medinos

## Overview

Medinos is a Yokohama-based engineering company established in 1998, combining software and hardware expertise. Operating with a three-division structure (Development, Solution, and Product), Medinos provides embedded software development, application development, hardware development, FA solutions, and AI-powered image analysis services. The company has been expanding its operations since opening a Yokohama office in 2000.
