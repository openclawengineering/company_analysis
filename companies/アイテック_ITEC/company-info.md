## Company Profile

ITEC was founded in May 1983 as "株式会社情報処理技術者教育センター" (Information Processing Technology Education Center Co., Ltd.) and changed its name to "株式会社アイテック" in January 1991. As Japan's first private education center established specifically for information processing technology exam preparation, the company has specialized in IT technician education for over 40 years.

In June 2005, ITEC established "株式会社クリエーティブソリューションズ" (Creative Solutions Co., Ltd.) and began its HR business. In April 2018, Creative Solutions Co., Ltd. and ITEC merged to form the current ITEC.

Since 1983, ITEC has served approximately 11,000 corporate organizations and 1.3 million individuals with human resource development services. The company contributes to the sustainable development of enterprises through human resources and education businesses.

## Corporate Philosophy & CSR

ITEC supports CIESF (Children's International Education Support Fund), a non-profit organization that primarily conducts activities to improve the quality of basic education for children overseas, centered on Cambodia.
