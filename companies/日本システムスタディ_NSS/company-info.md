# 日本システムスタディ株式会社 / NSS (Nihon System Study)

## Overview

Nihon System Study (NSS) is a Fukuoka-headquartered software company established in 1976. With offices in Fukuoka, Tokyo, and Yokohama, NSS provides solution services, package product development, and cloud services. The company has achieved significant scale with ¥887 million in annual revenue (FY2024) and 57 employees. NSS also operates its own cloud platform, B-THREE Cloud.
