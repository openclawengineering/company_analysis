# 株式会社シンクタンクオブジャパン / ThinkTank of Japan Co., Ltd.

## Overview
ThinkTank of Japan is a Kawasaki-based IT company providing network construction, software/product development, and evaluation services. Founded in 1983, the company celebrated its 40th anniversary in 2023. Their corporate vision is to be "Japan's brain trust."

## Business Areas
- **Network Construction:** Network planning, design, construction (conformance testing), operation and maintenance for enterprise ICT systems
- **Product Development:** IT application development, embedded software development, LSI development; particularly strong in multi-vendor environments, embedded/control systems
- **Evaluation Services:** Verification and evaluation, third-party evaluation, conformance evaluation for complex digital/network systems
