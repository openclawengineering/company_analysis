### Products & Services
- **RayBrid** — Video cutout service (beautiful cutouts even from video)
- **RayBrid KeyMaker** — Chroma-key-free compositing
- **BIZ-AR** — Business-oriented AR solutions
- **Pinspect** — AR app for inspection work efficiency
- **Check+** — Cloud service for work procedure creation, progress management, and reporting
- **MONOLIST** — Digital twin platform using point cloud data for facility position management (registered with MLIT NETIS)
- **AI Slump Tracker** — AI-based concrete slump quality management system (registered with MLIT NETIS)
- **Smartphone de Survey** — AR app for solo smartphone surveying
- **Pitacon** — AR app for concrete placement quantity calculation
- **Chocotto Cloud** — Lightweight cloud service

## Patents
