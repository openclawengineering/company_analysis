# 田辺IT産業株式会社 / TII (Tanabe IT Industry)

## Overview

TII is a Yokohama-based IT services company established in July 2017, specializing in Microsoft 365 consulting, deployment, and operational support. The company positions itself as a "phoneless company" — replacing phone calls with Teams, email, and chat to minimize work interruptions and maximize productivity. With ~95% telework adoption, TII embraces modern remote work culture.
