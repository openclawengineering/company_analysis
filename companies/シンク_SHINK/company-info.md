# 有限会社シンク / SHINK Ltd.

## Overview
SHINK Ltd. is a small Yokohama-based company offering graphic design, web production, UI design, digital signage content, drone photography, and SI/IT services for SOHO and small offices. Established in 2003.

- **Address:** 231-0014 Yokohama, Naka-ku, Tokiwa-cho 5-72, Yokohama Tsusan Building 403
- **Phone:** 045-227-5671
- **Website:** https://www.shink.jp/
- **President:** Naoki Shiraishi

## Business Areas
- **Graphic Design:** Web/UI design, digital signage content, print materials (business cards, brochures, posters, etc.)
- **Web Production:** Website creation and operation from planning to deployment
- **UI Design:** User interface design for optimal user experience
- **Digital Signage Content:** Digital signage content creation
- **Drone Photography:** Aerial video/stills and inspection services (MLIT-licensed, drone registered, liability insurance)
- **SI Services:** IT infrastructure design, construction, deployment, operation, and maintenance for SOHO and small offices
- **PC Maintenance:** PC, peripheral, and network maintenance/troubleshooting for small offices

## Notable
- Established 2003
