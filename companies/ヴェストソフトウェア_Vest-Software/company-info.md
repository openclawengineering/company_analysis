## Notes

Founded in 1983, Vest Software Co., Ltd. is a Yokohama-based custom software development company with 18 employees. Their key clients include Sony, Mitsubishi Research Institute, and CIJ. They specialize in medical systems (electronic medical records, claims processing), RFID-based access management systems, produce industry ordering systems, and a unique human power generation system. They also develop defense-related systems.
