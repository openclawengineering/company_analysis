# 株式会社ソフトウェアコントロール / Software Control Corporation

## Overview
Software Control Corporation is a Tokyo-based IT company with over 40 years of experience in traffic management systems, core business systems, and facility monitoring systems. They emphasize "3 S's": Sincerity, Speciality, and Sensibility. Also engages in game/app development as a maker activity.

## Key Details
- **Address:** 144-8721 Tokyo, Ota-ku, Kamata 5-37-1, Nissei Aroma Square 15F
- **Website:** https://www.scnet.co.jp/
- **Recruitment:** New graduate, internship, dispatched/contract

## Business Areas
- **Production Management Systems:** Manufacturing and production control systems
- **Passenger Management Systems:** Transportation and passenger flow management
- **Defense & Space Systems:** Defense and aerospace-related systems
- **Monitoring & Control Systems:** Facility monitoring and SCADA systems
- **Geographic Information Systems (GIS):** Map and location-based information systems
- **Railway Systems:** Railway-related IT infrastructure
- **Custom Software Development:** Contracted software development
- **Embedded Systems Development**
- **SES Services:** Technical staff dispatch

## Notable
- 40+ years of continuous operation
- JISA (Japan Information Technology Services Industry Association) member
- ISMS certified
- Maker activity: develops games like "Smile Muscle Trainer" and "Flip☆Panic!" exhibited at BitSummit and Maker Faire events
- Western Japan office relocated November 2025
- Active in community engagement (Maker Faire Tokyo, Kyoto Micro Maker Faire)
