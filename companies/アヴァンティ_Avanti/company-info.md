## Company Profile

Piu Avanti Corporation was founded in February 2006 by members who had been engaged in IT infrastructure design, construction, operation, and consulting at major telecommunications carriers and SI enterprises. Although the company is still young and small, all employees have extensive experience and high technical skills. The company can respond to a wide range of customer needs, from system planning to design, construction, operation, and diagnosis.

The company was established to realize the founder's vision of:
- Going to the field where customers are
- Providing what customers need at reasonable prices
- Being appreciated by customers

### Corporate Philosophy

Piu Avanti operates based on three core principles:

1. **"Work is built on individual skills"**
   - Unlike companies that aim for the same results regardless of who performs the work, Piu Avanti believes work outcomes depend on who performs it. The company focuses on creating an environment where each individual can maximize their abilities.

2. **"Work cannot be built on flattery alone"**
   - Rather than avoiding unpleasant facts or situations, the company believes in correctly recognizing the current situation, discovering problems early, and implementing appropriate countermeasures quickly. Piu Avanti is willing to present harsh truths to customers when necessary.

3. **"Get to the essence of things"**
   - When building or updating systems, the company doesn't simply follow customer specifications. Instead, Piu Avanti identifies the root cause of problems ("Why is this happening?") and proposes solutions, explicitly stating which investments are necessary and which are not.

By implementing these three principles, Piu Avanti strives daily to be a trusted partner for customers.

## Products & Services

### 1. System Design & Construction
Designs and constructs systems that match customer needs, whether for intranet or internet. The company can handle everything from selecting server and network equipment models to installing, configuring, racking, and wiring operating systems and middleware. Both comprehensive execution from design phase to construction phase, and partial execution of only specific phases, are possible.

### 2. System Consulting & Security Consulting
Proposes solutions to resolve problems customers are facing. Regardless of whether systems are already in operation or planned for construction, Piu Avanti conducts current status analysis and requirement analysis, presents the essence of problems customers are facing, and proposes measures to solve those problems.

### 3. Facility Design & Construction
Designs and implements network cabling for customer offices and data centers. Responds to various needs beyond just new construction, including investigation and expansion of existing cabling, 19-inch rack installation, and seismic reinforcement. Also handles design and construction of IP phones, business phones, and power cabling.

### 4. System Operation Outsourcing
Performs all maintenance and operation of customer systems on behalf of customers. Equipped with remote maintenance environments, the company implements system operation and monitoring from its SOC (System Operation Center) and partner data centers. Customers can concentrate on their core business without being burdened by system operation.

### 5. Training & Seminars
Conducts training on fields where the company's active engineers specialize. Since active engineers conduct the lectures, the training content is more practical and useful than just classroom learning, using actual equipment. Suitable for new employee training or introducing skills outside of expertise within the company.
