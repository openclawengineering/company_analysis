## Notes

Founded in 2005, Atago Inc. specializes in system development (billing, telecom, sales, payment, logistics) and cloud development across AWS, GCP, Azure, and Alibaba Cloud. The company also develops proprietary services in emerging technology areas including token economy, payment platforms, VR/AR/MR, data science, digital signage, and mobile applications.
