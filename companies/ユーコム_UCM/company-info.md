# 株式会社ユーコム / UCM (Your Computer, Universal Computer)

## Overview

UCM is a Kawasaki-based IT company providing software development, network/communication services, and internet security solutions. The company name reflects its founding aspiration: "Your Computer / Universal Computer" — building a more convenient, accessible computing society. UCM holds ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 certifications and operates with a "Total Management System" (TMS) integrating quality, security, and privacy management.
