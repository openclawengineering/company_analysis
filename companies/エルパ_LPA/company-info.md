- **Integrated Hardware/Software Package Systems** — Planning, development, and sales
- **Embedded Software Development** — Firmware and embedded systems development division (est. 2003)

## History & Milestones

- 1985: Founded, established as corporation
- 1991: Started arcade/consumer game software development
- 1997: Started PC game and commercial package software development
- 1999: Began enterprise core business system development
- 2003: Created embedded software/firmware development division
- 2006: Opened Tokyo office in Kayabacho
- 2010: Established New York subsidiary for expanding US projects
- 2010: Relocated HQ to Kannai, Yokohama for 25th anniversary
- 2015: Consolidated all offices (Yokohama HQ, Tokyo, NY) into Shin-Yokohama for 30th anniversary
- 2019: Established "LPAcK." company credo framework
- 2022: Obtained Privacy Mark certification
- 2023: Leadership transition — Kataoka retired, Watariguchi appointed president
