# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社アイネックス (Inex Co., Ltd.)

## Executive Summary

**株式会社アイネックス (Inex Co., Ltd.)** is a Japanese IT software development company headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. The company operates under a unique "Engineer First" philosophy, differentiating itself in the competitive SES (System Engineering Services) market.
