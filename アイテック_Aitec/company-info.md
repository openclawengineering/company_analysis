# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社アイテック (Aitec Co., Ltd.)

---

## Executive Summary

株式会社アイテック (Aitec Co., Ltd.) is a specialized IT education and human resources company based in Tokyo, Japan. Established in 1983, it holds the distinction of being Japan's first private-sector education center for the Information Technology Engineer Examination (情報処理技術者試験). The company operates as a subsidiary of Forval Corporation and has built a 42+ year legacy in IT professional development.

---

## 1. Company History and Founding Date

### Founding Timeline
- **Founded:** May 1983
- **Original Name:** 株式会社情報処理技術者教育センター (Information Processing Technology Engineer Education Center Co., Ltd.)
- **Name Change:** January 1991 - Changed to 株式会社アイテック (Aitec Co., Ltd.)
- **Key Milestone:** 1983 - Launched education services for the national Information Technology Engineer Examination as Japan's first private-sector provider in this field

### Major Corporate Events
- **2005:** Established Creative Solutions Inc. to enter the human resources business
- **2018:** Merged Creative Solutions Inc. with Aitec Co., Ltd., consolidating both education and HR businesses under the Aitec brand

**Historical Significance:** As the pioneer in IT certification education in Japan, Aitec has been a trailblazer for over four decades, supporting the development of Japan's IT workforce since the early days of the country's information technology industry expansion.
