# Company Analysis Report: 株式会社アクウェア (Acquware Co., Ltd.)

## Executive Summary

**株式会社アクウェア (Acquware Co., Ltd.)** is a Japanese IT systems integration and software development company established in 2001. Based in Atsugi City, Kanagawa Prefecture, the company specializes in system design, software development, IT consulting, and technical staffing services. Acquware has built a strong reputation in developing systems for public infrastructure, social services, and private enterprises, serving as a trusted partner for major Japanese corporations and government entities.

### Organizational Culture

#### Anti-Seniority Philosophy
Acquware was founded on the principle of breaking away from traditional Japanese corporate culture:

> *"When Acquware was first established, all members were people who had changed jobs. Most companies follow a seniority-based system where even capable young employees face issues like 'opinions don't get through' and 'salaries don't increase without age advancement.' We believed that if we didn't flexibly incorporate the opinions of young employees who will drive the future, we couldn't build a good company."*
> — Kazuhiko Sekino, Founder & CEO

#### Key Cultural Values:
1. **Flat Organization** - No rigid hierarchy based on age or tenure
2. **Voice of Youth** - Young employees' opinions actively shape company direction
3. **Work-Life Balance** - Emphasis on reasonable working hours (avg. 15-20 hours overtime/month)
4. **Continuous Learning** - E-learning support, skill-up sheets for career growth (1/3/5 year milestones)
5. **100% Mid-Career Culture** - Everyone started as a transfer, creating an inclusive environment
