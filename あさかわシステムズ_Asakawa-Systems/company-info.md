# Company Analysis Report

## あさかわシステムズ株式会社 / Asakawa Systems Co., Ltd.

---

## Executive Summary

Asakawa Systems Co., Ltd. is a specialized Japanese IT solutions provider focused exclusively on the construction and industrial engineering sectors. With over 40 years of industry experience, the company has established itself as a leading provider of ERP solutions and software products tailored for construction companies, industrial waste management, and related industries across Japan.

### Product Evolution (Gulliver Series)
The company's flagship product "Gulliver" has evolved continuously since 1984:
- 1984: Generic terminal (predecessor)
- 1991: Office computer version released
- 1994: Gulliver/WIN released
- 2004: Gulliver ProStage/Gulliver NEXT released
- 2017: Gulliver Takumi released
- 2020: Gulliver Enterprise released
- 2024: Gulliver X released
- **2025: Cumulative installations exceeded 1,000 companies**
